Purchase Orders | Subrental tab



 

This article will go over the Subrental tab in Purchase Orders. It will also go over the process of creating a subrental purchase order. This is not the preferred method for creating a purchase order for a subrental. To view the recommended process, see our article Subrental Process. 


 

Topics included in this article
 

  • Getting there
  • Subrental tab
  • Creating a Purchase Order




Getting there

Expert: Program Menu > Purchase Orders
Elite: Left Menu > Purchase Orders >  Purchase Orders


Subrental tab

The Subrental tab holds all subrental purchase orders created with the below process. Purchase orders created by following the recommended process will appear in the Open tab.



Creating a Subrental Purchase Order

You can create a Subrental purchase order by creating a new PO with a vendor marked as subrental.
