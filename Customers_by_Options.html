Customers by Options




Overview
 

This article will discuss the Customers by Options Report. This report lists customers based off what is in the options field in their customer record.


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Income Menu > Customer Income by Grouping > Group 1: Customer Options
Elite: Right Menu > Reports > Search: Customer Income by Grouping


From the report generator, select Customer Options for Group 1.
 


This report is a drill down type of report where the first screen will look like the following:

 

 

You can double click on the options in blue font or on the income amounts to view additional detailed information.
