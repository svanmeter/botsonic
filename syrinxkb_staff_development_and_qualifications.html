Syrinx® - Staff Development & Qualifications 

 

 

 

 

Overview: This guide is designed to help you with staff development and qualifications.

 

 

 

 

Before adding development and qualifications for staff members, the Development Categories and Items will need to be set up.

 

This can be done by clicking the Staff tab and in the Setup section of the ribbon, selecting Development Categories.

 

Click on Add New and name the category you wish to create. If this category will be required for the operation of equipment, then tick the "Required for Equipment Operation?" box and click OK.

 

 

Once the category has been created, select Development Items, click on Add New, select the category from the dropdown menu, name the Item and click OK.

 

 

Complete the fields in each tab, except the Staff tab. In the Required to Operate Equipment & Required to Service Equipment tabs, select the category and fleet items that will fall under these qualifications/requirements.

 

 

Once completed, go to Staff Members and select a staff member to whom the qualification will apply, and click on Amend. Select the Development tab and click on Add New. Select the Category and Name of the development/qualification that is relevant to this staff member and click on OK.

 

 

Populate the fields in the Booking tab with the information on the development and click Close. This will add the development against the staff member.

 

  

Within the Amend screen, in the Development tab, there are several buttons along the bottom.

 

 

 

 

 

 

Once marked as complete, under the Completion History tab, there is the ability to Remove Completion Details or Amend Costs.

 

 

Now that the developments have been added, the Staff tab in the Development Items window will now reflect which staff members are qualified for these developments.

 

 

It is a great idea to use the User Alerts feature to assist with the management of upcoming bookings and appointments. This feature can be found under the User tab. Click on Options and in the Find: bar, type in development or part of the word. Select one or both Alerts by ticking the "Enabled?" box/boxes. The days can be adjusted to suit each individual user by simply typing the number of days in the Days block.
