Subrentals

This article explains the process and management of subrentals within the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Understanding subrentals
  • Creating a subrental order
  • Managing subrental inventory
  • Subrental billing and payments

Understanding subrentals

Subrentals are transactions where items are rented out to another business or customer, often to fulfill special requests or large orders.

Creating a subrental order

Follow these steps to create a subrental order in the system.

  1. Identify the items to be subrented and check their availability.
  2. Create a new order and select the ‘Subrental’ option.
  3. Enter the details of the subrental, including the customer, duration, and terms.

Managing subrental inventory

Keep track of subrental inventory to ensure items are available when needed and returned on time.

  1. Maintain a separate inventory list for subrental items.
  2. Regularly update the inventory status to reflect current availability.
  3. Monitor the return dates and condition of subrented items.

Subrental billing and payments

Manage billing and payments for subrentals accurately to ensure proper accounting.

  1. Set up billing terms specific to subrentals in the system.
  2. Generate invoices for subrentals based on the agreed terms.
  3. Record payments received and track any outstanding balances.
