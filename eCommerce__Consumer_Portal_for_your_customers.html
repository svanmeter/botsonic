eCommerce | Consumer Portal (for your customers)




Our Consumer Portal is the next-level tool for you to see what you have out of our rental store. It's designed to be mobile-friendly, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices, from desktops and laptops to phones and tablets.


Topics in this article include
 

  • Logging in to the Consumer Portal
  • Home Screen
  • Contract Screen
  • My Rentals
  • Tracking



 

Logging in to the Consumer Portal

You can enter your username and password when you first get to the login screen. You can create an account through this portal if you have a contract number or account. If you forget your password, you can click the Forgot Password link underneath the login screen. You can even create an account from this screen as well.
 

 

If you have your email associated with multiple accounts, you will have to choose one and click confirm.
 

 

Once you log in, you will be able to see your home screen.
 

 

 

Home Screen

 All tiles are clickable and you can view your contracts, invoices, rentals, and track information as well.
 

 

Coming Soon: The ability to have advertisements, and coupons.

 
 

Contract Screen

The contract screen is filterable allowing you to search for what you want to see. By default, it will show all contracts.

 

If you need to, you can export the table view into a CSV file.
 

 

On the table view, you will be able to see the contract number, amount, amount due, status of the contract, start and close date, location, and job number.
 

 

When you click on the contract number, you'll see the details of that contract. You will be able to see the items, contract start and end dates, amount total, the amount due, and view the most recent copy of the contract via PDF.

In the bottom right corner, you'll be able to select Pay to make a payment on the contract. 
 



When you click on Pay, you can pay the contract full or another amount.


 

My Rentals

The My Rentals tab, displayed as MY HIRES (for your UK/AU counterparts), displays the items you will have, currently have, or had on rent. Use the filters to narrow down the items displayed. You can also view the contract number associated with each item.



When you select an item or items you currently have, you can request a call-off. If you're selecting multiple items, press the green Select Complete button to view the below options. 

Requesting a Call-off

You can request a call-off for one or more items, at once. When making the request, you can request when to call off a rental and view and modify the Site and Returner information.


 

Once you're ready, click the Submit Request for Pickup button. An email and notification will be sent to the store. A copy of the email will also be sent to the email you entered as well. 


 

Tracking

Tracking will allow you to see the items you currently have out on site allowing you to see where each item is located.
