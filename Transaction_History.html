Transaction History

 

This report shows all the contracts that have this item rented/sold on the contract.  The contract number, customer name, date out, date in, hours, and the price will be displayed for each contract.

*Note:  The records can be sorted and resorted by left-clicking on the column heading.  For example, to sort the above by customer, simply put the cursor over the “Customer” heading and left-click on it.

Grouping - allows you to group by Contract, Contract Info, Customer, Date In, Date Out, Job Site Month In, Month Out, Status, or Store.

    Ex. The contract was selected, this will display all contracts related to this item, Store, Status Customer, Date Out, Date In, Hours, Qty, Price, Contract items, and Job Site. Topline will give you the total summary of all contracts listed.
