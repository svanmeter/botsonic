Yesterday’s Closed Account Contracts


Location:  Program Menu > Accounting Reports > Accounting Report Menu > Yesterday’s Closed Account Contracts


Overview: This report prints all the account contracts that were closed on the previous day.  You will have the option of either printing a summary or detail version of this report. The report will consist of (Customer Name, Contract Number, Dollar Total, Amt Paid, Close Date, and Employee associated with the closed contract), as well as list the items within the contract.
