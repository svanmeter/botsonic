Adding New Delivery, Event, and General Locations

This article provides a step-by-step guide on how to add new delivery, event, and general locations in the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Adding new delivery locations
  • Setting up event locations
  • Managing general location information

Adding new delivery locations

Expand your service reach by adding new delivery locations to your system.

  1. Go to the Locations section in the system.
  2. Choose the option to add a new delivery location.
  3. Enter the address, contact details, and other relevant information for the new location.

Setting up event locations

Event locations can be added for easy reference and coordination during planning and execution.

  1. Select the Event Locations option in the system.
  2. Click on Add New Location and provide the necessary details such as name, address, and capacity.
  3. Save the location for future reference in event planning and logistics.

Managing general location information

Keep general location information up to date to ensure accurate service and delivery.

  1. Review and update existing location information regularly.
  2. Add or remove general locations based on changes in service areas or business operations.
  3. Ensure all location data is accurate and complete for operational efficiency.
