Income Compare by Month  

 

This option will show the item income broken down by month and year and display the times out for each month. 

 

The graph will breakdown the total income by month and year, for the current year and the past seven years.

        *Note: You have the option of selecting individual store income or all stores. If select all stores, it will break out and display each store's income totals.
