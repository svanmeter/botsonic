Item Super Categories

 

How to get there: Program Menu > Configurations > Item Configurations > Item Super Categories

 

A Super Category is a combination of individual categories that would be used in conjunction with each other regularly on single contracts. Weddings and birthday parties would be separate Super Categories while they both share items like tables and chairs, the typical wedding does not include inflatable.

 

1.Create the Super Category through #9 Configuration>Item Configurations>Item Super Categories.

Click Add and fill in the necessary information for the Super Category. The more relevant web tags entered the more likely your website will come up in internet search results.

2.  Select Categories, while holding the “Ctrl” button, select each relevant category, and click select. This creates a Super Category.  Keep in mind a single category can go into many different Super Category. On the Point-of-Rental integrated website, a potential customer will be able to select what event they are having (i.e. weddings) and instantly be able to view all related categories for a wedding
