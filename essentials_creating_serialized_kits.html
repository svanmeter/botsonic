Creating Serialized Kits

This article explains how to create serialized kits in the system, which consist of multiple serialized items grouped together as a single unit.

Topics included in this article

  • Understanding serialized kits
  • Steps to create a serialized kit
  • Managing and updating serialized kits

Understanding serialized kits

Serialized kits are used to track groups of items that are often used or rented out together. Each kit has a unique serial number for tracking.

Steps to create a serialized kit

Follow these steps to create a new serialized kit in the system.

  1. Access the Inventory or Kit Management section in the system.
  2. Choose to create a new kit and select ‘Serialized’ as the kit type.
  3. Add individual serialized items to the kit, specifying the quantity of each.
  4. Assign a unique serial number to the kit for tracking and management purposes.

Managing and updating serialized kits

Regularly manage and update serialized kits to ensure accurate tracking and availability.

  1. Review the contents of kits regularly and update them as necessary.
  2. Track the usage and condition of each kit and its individual items.
  3. Adjust the kit composition to meet changing needs or requirements.
