6/14/2022 - Version 2022.08


  • Storefront enhancements- We have made 3 big enhancements for those of you using the storefront.
    • Speed when selecting categories - Our development wizards have done some voodoo magic to make it speedy fast when clicking through categories. Your customers will be able to place orders faster, therefore giving you money faster.
    • Instant availability - The availability on the storefront for each item now updates instantaneously. So if other orders are being placed at the same time either on the counter or through other orders on the storefront, the availability is updated automagically.
    • Delivery/Pickup to not pre-populate - When you go to enter a different address on the storefront, it does not pre-populate the customers address
 
  • Quickbooks online enhancement - This has been a BIG request. For those of you not in the United States, Quickbooks online integration has not been available. No matter where you are now, the integration is available.
 
  • Enhancement for delivery/pickup event info - You now have the ability to expand the Event/Delivery/Pickup information in a transaction and it will keep that setting (expanded or collapsed) on a per user basis.
 
  • Contract enhancement for 'TBD' - One of our Texas customers said “Howdy, our fine customers here in this big state don't know what TBD means on their contract. They are making a fuss and hollering at us. It'd be mighty neighborly if you could remove that." Thanks for the tip JR. I reckon we could do that. So now when you print a customers contract and a serialized item has not been selected, TBD is no longer underneath the item. 
 
  • Multi Store Data Importing - One of our Multi Store customers in California had an idea for us. He said "Hey Bruh, it's gnarly that when I pull new items in, OMG totally only works for 1 store. It'd be way cool if you could fix that. I’d be so stoked LOL." Fortunately we speak Rad, so we were able to add a 'company' column so you can import multiple stores items on the same spreadsheet through the Data Importer.
 
  • Review of past features you may have either never seen or you just forgot about
    • Absolute Time: Have you ever been in a transaction, and the start date will say ‘Today’ and the end date will say ‘Tomorrow’? Wouldn’t it be nice to have an option to just show Dates and times? Now under settings, you have an option to change it. It’s per user so you can have it one way, and another user can have it the other.
    • ‘Pinning’ of Filters: If you create a non-shared filter in Inventory/Workbench/Customers, you can pin it so that it will stay on that selection when you come back to it, and will hold that setting for your user. 
Quickbooks Online Customer Sync - Since all the non USA customers can now use Quickbooks Online Integration, the customer sync released recently will also work for you.
