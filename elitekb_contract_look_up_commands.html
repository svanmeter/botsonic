Contract Look Up Commands




Overview
 

This article will walk you through the various shortcut commands that can be used within the Point of Rental software. Any of the following shortcut commands can be used in junction with a Counter System menu shortcut (i.e., RSC (R for Reprint/Inquire).


Topics included in this article

  • Commands
  • Counter System



Commands
 

Note: Enter these commands in the Contract No field when searching for a contract.
 

SC - Search by Customer
 

SI - Search by Item
 

SD - Search by Date Contract Written
 

SDD - Search by Contract Due Date
 

SSO - Search Open Contracts
 

SSR - Search Reservation Contracts
 

SSQ - Search Quote Contracts
 

SSC - Search Closed Contracts
 

SSW - Search Work Orders
 

ALL - Search All Active Contracts
 

Same - will bring up the same contract or item or customer you were just dealing with.
 

Note: The “Page Up” button on the keyboard will act as if you are typing in “Same.”
 

Prev - will bring up the previous contract or item or customer.
 

More - will bring up more contracts or items or customers beyond the one you were just dealing with.
 

Next - will bring up the next contract, item, or customer beyond the one you were just dealing with.


 

Counter System

Press the Tab key from the enter ID screen to open Day at a Glance. Ensure the mouse cursor is inside the text box.
 

Press Alt while in the Counter System to underline the key letter for each menu option. Or refer to the Ease of Access document under Windows Server to turn on this feature permanently.
 

In the Enter Selection box type the underlined letter from the desired selection and press Enter.

To open a new contract you can enter ‘N’ and press Enter or you can enter ‘N’ and the corresponding letter from the next menu, then press Enter (i.e. ‘NR’ = New Reservation, ‘NQ’ = New Quote, etc.).

Menu options (Close, Modify, Send Out, Inquire, Void, and Clone) can be used and followed by a contract number to bypass the contract selection screen (i.e. c123456 = Close Contract 123456, m123456 = Modify Contract 123456, r123456 = Inquire Contract 123456).

This can also be used in conjunction with the search terms listed about (i.e., rssr = Inquire and show me a list of reservations to choose from, msso = Modify and show me a list of open contracts to choose from).
