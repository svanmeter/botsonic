Net Help

Allows you to find desired help topics quickly and easily. Please use the navigation tools in the left panel to browse the Point of Rental help topics.

Once you click the help in the upper left corner of your Counter System, you will be taken to our NET HELP HOME PAGE. You will be able to access the Table of Contents or Search to look for any related topic. The Net Help will also give you access to other functions and events, such as upcoming trainings, Newsletter, etc.
