Step 1: Confirm/Create folder structure
 
  • In order to create and manage images/documents for Resources on your integrated POR website, you will be saving files directly on your POR Server in the following location:
 

C:\POR\WEBCONTENT\site001\Gallery\WebDoc

 

Note About the Above Folder Path
 

  • Replace C:\ with whichever drive your POR system is installed under, e.g. D:\, etc.
 
  • If you have more than one integrated website or are running Mobile Workforce, replace /site001 with the correct site #, e.g. /site002. You can usually tell by looking in the sitexxx folders in your /webcontent folder - the one you want will already have a /gallery folder in it. Ask the POR Web Design Team if you’re unsure which folder is the correct one.
 
  • If you do not see the subfolder /WebDoc, create it.
 
 

Step 2: Create & organize your images and/or documents

 

  • If you are creating guides, policies, credit apps, or other forms, save them as PDF files, no larger than 1.4Mb.

 

  • If you are creating coupons, create them as JPG images, no larger than 550px by 100px. It is most user-friendly if you create a border around your coupon. (Multiple coupons may be saved as a PDF instead of .jpgs)

 

  • Do NOT use special characters in document or image filenames (i.e. no quotes, double-quotes, commas, #, $, &, etc.)

 

  • The name you choose for your PDF file will become the name that is shown as a link on the Resources page on your website.


 

Step 3: Copy your images & documents to your POR server
 

  • Your documents should be organized into subfolders such as: Forms, Policies, Guides, Coupon, etc.. These folders will become sections on your Resources page on the website, so create appropriate subfolders in the /WebDoc folder:
    C:\POR\WEBCONTENT\site001\Gallery\WebDoc\Forms
    C:\POR\WEBCONTENT\site001\Gallery\WebDoc\Coupon
    etc.
     

  • Copy the image & document files that you created in Step #2 into the corresponding subfolder on your POR server under C:\POR\WEBCONTENT\site001\Gallery\WebDoc.
     

  • Your nightly website update (or the manual “Update Website” from the POR Configuration menu if you’d like to see your immediate changes) will then pick up the images/documents from your POR server and upload them to your website.
     

  • Notify the POR Web Design Team that you have completed Step #3.
     

 

About Guides & Forms
    

  • Your PDF documents will be linked to the Resources page on your website during development.
     

  • Once the Resources page is set up on your site, you may then upload updated PDF documents at any time without the help of the POR Web Design Team. You may also add/delete/update sections and docs at your discretion by adding/deleting PDFs, .jpgs and folders.
     

  • Please be careful with your Gallery subfolders – if you accidentally delete them and “update website”, then your website’s document links will be broken!

 

About Coupons
 

  • Our newly-enhanced Coupon page will automatically display any images you have stored in your Coupon subfolder. There is no need to maintain a particular naming convention for your files in order for them to display on the site. Any file found in your Coupon subfolder will be displayed, so be sure to keep your Coupon subfolder clear of outdated coupon images.
     

  • If you have multiple coupons you want to show at the same time, create a PDF with all the images and place the PDF in the Coupon subfolder.

Maintaining Your Resources Gallery


You will at some point want to add, remove, or update items in your gallery.
 

  • To add, simply create your new PDF and copy it to the appropriate folder. After the next website update, it will show up in the gallery, under that folder's heading.
    • IF, however, you decide to create a new folder, you'll need to let the Web Team know so your code can be updated to include the new folder.
 
  • To remove, simply delete the PDF from the folder.
 
  • To update, we recommend you update a document of the same name as the one that is currently in the folder, then simply replace it. After the next website update, the new version will show up in the gallery.
