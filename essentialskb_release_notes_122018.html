Account Customers

There are new settings for your site and your customers to support a credit limit by customer.

New Customer Credit Limits

Do you have those customers that you limit on how much credit they can have?  Essentials has made it easier for you to set this so your counter user won't rent to a customer that has met their credit limit.  You can set this in the customer record by clicking the edit icon next to the customer and choosing Advanced Mode.
Under Payment Configuration, you can also set your system to block people from renting who go over their credit limit.  Unchecking this box will force the customer to pay before taking out a rental if it goes over their credit limit.

New Payment Screen

Since you can now set a credit limit for a customer, the payment screen has been enhanced to show you more information.  If you just collect the total due every time, you can still just click that total and collect it. This new view just give you more information if you want it.

If you collect deposits and want to adjust the deposit amount, just click the pencil icon.

If you have a customer with an account limit, that information will also show here.

 

Small Updates to Transaction Overview

You can now see if a particular line item is taxed.  Look for the 'T' in the last column.
You now add rental discounts using a button in the menu.  Discounts will now calculate as line items.

New Look to Calendar Availability

Before the change, calendar availability overview showed you how many of an item you had available at the beginning of the day, the end of the day and the minimum available throughout the day.  We still do show this but with an option to see more.  Once you click More details at the bottom, you now have an option to see an availability chart.  When you hover over the availability dot, you will be able to see the quantity you own, the quantity on the transaction(s), the total and time of the transaction(s).  
You can also show multiple items on a single chart by selecting multiple items and selecting "Availability Chart" under Actions.

New Maintenance Feature

Do you have maintenance schedules set up for your serialized items?  Want to know when those maintenance schedules are or if there is one due upon receiving the item back into availability?  We have now made it a little easier for you.  When you have a maintenance schedule set up, if that maintenance schedule is due for an item upon receiving it back from rental, you will receive a maintenance due message and the item will automatically be placed into maintenance for you.  You just have to remember to receive it back into stock once the maintenance order is completed.

         

 

New Transaction Event Log Feature

Need a reminder to call a customer in regards to a transaction?  Or, maybe even a note for your staff to follow up with the customer on an item they had questions on?  Essentials now lets you add a reminder note, or an event log, to the transaction overview.  Just click the + Add Note button at the bottom of the transaction where you see the Additional Custom/Terms and Special Request.  Enter the note and reminder date and time.  Click Save and you now have an event log on the transaction.

Once the reminder has been completed, click the check mark next to the reminder to complete it.

New Payments Collected Overview

Want to know what, if anything, your customer has paid?  Now you can see all of their payments and refunds on the transaction overview screen without going to the contract overview screen.  

You can find this in the Notes section under the Additional Custom/Terms and Special Request fields.

New Invoice Format

We now give you a choice on how you want your invoice formats to be.  If you aren't a fan of our Modern format, you can now choose our traditional format for your invoices but still have your contract format as our Modern format.  

As long as you have invoicing enabled under Admin-Configuration-Configure Printing Options, you will have a drop down next to the invoice terms to choose which format you want.
You will also be able to see the customer's credit limit on the payment screen when you collect payment for any outstanding transactions.  This will also show you the minimum payment required for this customer.  If the customer is paying more than the minimum required, you will see the difference on this screen.

 

New Customer Overview Screen

When you edit a customer's record, you will now be able to see the customer's history including transactions, payments and notes.  

 

 

New Percentage Amounts Required Feature

You've always had the ability to set a certain percentage amount due upon a quote being converted to a reservation or a reservation being sent out.  In the past, you or your counter user has had to manually calculate this amount.  No more manually calculating.  As long as the percentage amounts are set up within Admin-Configuration-Configure Tax and Payment Options, the amount of the percentage of the transaction amount is listed for you.  

The percentage amount can also be overridden from the payment screen.

Other Updates

  • If you have automatic billing set up within a transaction and the payment method is Card, a receipt is now sent instead of the invoice.
  • When sending a Quote to a customer, you now have the ability to not send the terms since it's not a true reservation or order.
  • The Honeywell Captuvo barcode scanner is now compatible with the mobile app to easily scan items upon sending out or quick receive or when you edit an existing transaction thru the mobile app.
  • You can now collect cash payments from the mobile app.
  • e-Signature for arbitrary documents now allows you to send an email notification when a document has been signed to a specific email address other than the user who sent it or who the notification is set up for.
  • e-Signature for arbitrary now gives you the option to allow the customer to refuse the transaction and not sign it.  
  • Damage waiver, coupons and discounts now show on the transaction as line items on the overview screen.
  • You now have the ability to add multiple coupon codes.
  • Many updates to report.
