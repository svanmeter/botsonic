Syrinx® - eSign

 

 

 

 

Overview: This guide is designed to help you with eSign. eSign is designed so you can send documents to your customers and ask for electronic signatures.

 

eSign is available to use on selected documents including

  • Hire Contract
  • Delivery Note
  • Hire Confirmation
  • Hire Quote
  • Off Hire Collection
  • Customer Estimate

 

If you print the document to your screen, you can then select eSign from the Ribbon.

 

Once selected, the document will convert to a PDF and you will then be faced with the contact selection screen.

 

 

The top section is customer contacts. You can request multiple signatures by changing the min signatures required and then highlighting the contacts. The bottom section is for an internal notification to staff. This notification tells them when the document has been signed.

 

Once you click OK, the next screen will be the final screen before you send the documents. In this screen you can add any additional message for the customer.

 

This can also be pre-set in the configuration. Once ready, click Send.

 

 

The customer will then receive the email. All they need to do is click on the link and this will take them to the signature page.

 

 

They will see an electronic version of the contract.

 

 

 

 

Once they scroll to the bottom, they can sign the document by clicking the yellow signature box.

 

 

 They then have the option of signing on screen using their finger or they can do text input, i.e., their name.

 

 

Once signed they can click Sign Document.

 
To finish off, they will need to click Sign Document to finalise.

 

 

 

The customer will then need to tick the box to agree and has the option to send a copy to themselves.

 

 

If you selected a member of staff to get notified, they will have received a notification of the document being signed.
