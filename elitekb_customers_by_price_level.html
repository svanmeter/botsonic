Customers by Price Level




Overview
 

This article will discuss the Customers by Price Level report. This report lists customers by the price level listed in their customer record.


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Income Menu > Customer Income by Grouping > Group 1: Price Level
Elite: Right Menu > Reports > Search: Customer Income by Grouping


From the report generator, select Price Level for Group 1.
 


This report is a drill down type of report where the first screen will look like the following:

 

 

You can double click on a price level in blue font to view additional information.
