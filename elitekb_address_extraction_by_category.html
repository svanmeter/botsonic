Address Extraction by Category



 

This report allows you to export a listing of customers’ addresses by selecting the desired rental or sales item category, listing all the customers who have bought or rented something from that category. 


Topics included in this article
 

  • Address Extraction
  • Address Extraction Export

 

Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Marketing Reports > Address Extraction by Category
Elite: Right Menu > Reports icon > Address Extraction by Category


Address Extraction
 

1. You will get the below menu. Select your desired item category. 
 

 

2. Once you select your desired category, you will be prompted to select a date. The report will display items in that category that have been rented since the selected date. 

 



Address Extraction Export


1. With your report open, click on the second icon on the upper left ribbon. 

 

 

2. Your report will export and open in Excel.

 

 

Note: If you do not have access to Excel you can still export your report. 
 

(To export without Excel)
 

3. With your report open, click on the sixth icon on the upper left ribbon. 

 

 

4. Enter a file name, choose your file save type, and select your save location.
