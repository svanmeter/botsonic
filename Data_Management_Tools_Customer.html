Data Management Tools – Customer Rewards

 

Select # 14 - Customer Rewards From the Menu.

 

Make your Selection for the Customer Rewards From the applicable Drop-down Menus.

Select Status

Select the type of customer

Select the Customer Group.

Check the Exclude Options you would like.

 

 

Select Go and the customer records will be updated.  When complete a pop-up window will let you know the process is done.
