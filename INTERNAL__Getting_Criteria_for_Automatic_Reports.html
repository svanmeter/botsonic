INTERNAL | Getting Criteria for Automatic Reports







This article will walk you through how to get criteria from reports in Expert/Elite to set up Automatic Reports. Please note this guide is ONLY for POR Reports, NOT Custom Reports. 
 


 

Getting Criteria for Automatic Reports

 

1. Remote into the customer's computer via TeamViewer. 

 

2. Determine which report the customer wants to configure as an Automatic Report.

 

3. Go to and open up the report, manually. Run your report with your desired criteria.

 

4. Click Generate.
 

 

5. To email the report to yourself, click the email button.
 


 

6. When it asks for your User ID, enter our admin credentials.
 


 

7. A new prompt will ask if you want the Crystal .rpt file. Click Yes.
 


 

8. Send an email to yourself. Don’t forget to add a subject and a message.


9. Click Send.
 

 

10. Once you get the email, save the .rpt file to your desktop.
