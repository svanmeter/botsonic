• ESign 2.0: ‘Big Bucks and No Whammies!’ You win again on ESign as we have made several enhancements to this feature
 
  • Capture Initials - You can collect customer Initials along with full signature on any documents where both are required
 
  • Demo Mode - Allows you to see what your documents will look like before going live with them
 
  • Auto support Essentials contracts. You can have a signature box on the "signature" line as well as initials boxes on each Line Item that has a ContractTerms field filled out
 
  • Simple Config - User friendly configuration for simple and quick setup
 
  • Multiple Input Fields - We call them tokens, but you will have a token of appreciation for being to place initials and signatures in multiple places
 
  • Enhanced Input Boxes - Enhanced user experience includes box transparency to collect a Signature or Initials without blocking text visibility. Also, you can adjust the location of the input boxes on both the X and Y axis
​​​​​​​
  • General Improvements - Improvements to speed (reduced loading time) and reliability
​​​​​​​
  • New Import file types: ‘I’ll take all spreadsheet types for $500 Alex.’ When you use the data importer it can now accept the most common spreadsheet types, including .xls, .xlsx, and ODS.
​​​​​​​
  • Manage columns on counter: ‘What’s behind Door#1?’ You win no matter what door you choose. Several of our screens on the counter, including customers and items, now have a manage columns option, so you can only display the relevant information to you and your staff to make contract writing more efficient.
