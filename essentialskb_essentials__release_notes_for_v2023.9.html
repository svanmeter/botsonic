Our development team has been working hard and working overtime to come up with some really great features that have been asked for by you. We love our customers, so we have made the changes you have asked for. We have marked those features so that you know they came from you.

  •   Delivery/Pickup Fee Enhancements
    • Separate Delivery/Pickup Fees - You now have a global (configuration) option to break out your pickup fee from your delivery fee
      • Delivery/Pickup by Zip Code - You can also set your delivery/pickup fees separately by zip code. You can set a Timbuktu Delivery Only, Timbuktu Pickup Only, or a Timbuktu Delivery and Pickup.
    • Delivery/Pickup Fee Wording - Previously, if you did a delivery, or a delivery and pickup, or just pickup, it said ‘Delivery Fee’. Now Delivery is ‘Delivery Fee’, Pickup only says ‘Pickup Fee’, and Delivery and Pickup says….are you catching on yet? ‘Delivery and Pickup Fee’.
 
  • Guest Checkout on Storefront - You now have the ability to allow a guest checkout when your customers access the storefront in addition to the option of creating an account. Also, you can set the required customer fields for guest checkout. It will allow your customer to put in their information, but not create an ‘account’ and will log the transaction as coming from guest checkout. This option is not recommended if you want to require full information and want your customers to create an account with a login that they can reuse.
 
  • Mobile Styling Changes - We have made some changes to the mobile experience for the better. We have uniformly sized checkboxes, receive buttons, and more. We think you will like the view
 
  • Estimate Pricing Change - When you receive items, we have made some visual changes to this section to make it easier to view. You will also notice that we have hidden the ‘Recalculate’ option when there is no price difference.
​​​​​​​
  • Workbench not showing next Tuesday orders when default date is set to Wednesday - Some of you noticed there was an issue with orders on the workbench not showing up until scrolling to the next week. We 'fixed the glitch'
