What's new?

uLearn offers new features to help you and your team succeed!
 
  • Your team can now view open cases and updates from uLearn (your account must be verified).
  • Need to contact us? Start a live chat with a representative! (Available only for Premiere Support members)
  • The help library now includes videos! You can identify videos in the search results by the "Video" tag. 
  • We've fine-tuned the search engine. Search results will display the most relevant articles based on the topic and keywords.
  • Find and read trending articles! You can find the Trending Articles section throughout uLearn. 
  • Make software suggestions straight from uLearn!
 


Getting to uLearn

Expert:

  • From the Counter System > select Help from the top left > select uLearn. 

  • From the Program Menu > select Help from the top left > select uLearn



Elite:

  • From the Elite home screen > Select the Help icon from the right menu > Select uLearn 




How to signup

Signing up is optional. You can access uLearn without having to log in. However, in order to use the Case Management feature, you will need to sign up and have your account verified by the Product Support team.
     
  1. Click the Sign Up link, in the bottom right corner of the login screen. 

 
  1. Fill in your information and press Submit. Please use your company email address and phone number.  
Please note, if you have an existing uLearn account, you will still need to sign up and reregister to link your old account to the new uLearn.
  1. Once you have submitted your information, uLearn will send an email to the email provided. 
    1. If you already have an existing account, the email will provide you with a link to reset your password.
    2. If you DO NOT have an existing account, the email will confirm your registration or notify you that we have created an internal case to confirm your information. Meanwhile, you'll still be able to access help articles and videos. To view cases your account must be verified.
 
  1. Using the email you provided as the username, return to uLearn and log in.



Navigating uLearn 





 1 | Toolbar


Use the top menu bar to explore all article topics, view your cases, and view release notes. To view cases you must be logged in to a verified account.



2 | Search Bar



Use the search bar to find help articles and videos. You can also use the filters to quickly narrow down search results. 



3 | Topics



Use these options to browse through help articles or videos that relate to a topic. You can choose from Accounting, Configurations, Daily Operations, Hardware, Modules, Reports, and Security.
When you’re viewing an article, above the title, you can click on the topic to find other related help articles.  




4 | Trending Articles



On the right-hand side throughout uLearn, you’ll see trending articles. The articles with the most amount of views will be listed.


5 | Contact Support & Submit Your Ideas



Contact Support:
When you need to contact us, you can click on Contact Support. You’ll be taken to a new screen where you can submit your inquiry directly to our team.
 
Submit Your Ideas:
Tell us your program enhancement ideas by clicking on Submit Your Ideas. This will take you to our online feedback management system, uInnovate, where you can vote on an already-existing idea or continue to create your own. You can also easily check out all the current suggestions and vote for the features that you’d like to see in the software. You can also be notified when your suggestions are voted for and see where each suggestion is in the process of being adopted.

This area may not be available until your uLearn account is verified.
