Merge Duplicate Customer Records

This article provides guidance on how to identify and merge duplicate customer records in a database or CRM system, ensuring data accuracy and consistency.

  • Identifying duplicate records

Challenges of duplicate customer records

Understand the potential challenges and issues that arise from having duplicate customer records in your database.

Identifying duplicate records

Learn how to identify and locate duplicate customer records using various data matching and deduplication techniques.

  1. Utilize automated deduplication tools and software.
  2. Manually review and compare customer records for duplicates.
  3. Establish criteria and rules for identifying duplicates.

Merging duplicate customer records

Discover the step-by-step process for merging duplicate customer records to create a single, comprehensive customer profile.

  1. Choose the primary record as the basis for merging.
  2. Transfer and consolidate information from duplicate records.
  3. Update links and references to the merged customer record.

Preventing duplicate records

Explore strategies and best practices for preventing the creation of duplicate customer records in the future.

  1. Implement data validation and entry rules to catch duplicates at input.
  2. Educate and train staff on duplicate prevention techniques.
  3. Regularly audit and clean the customer database.
