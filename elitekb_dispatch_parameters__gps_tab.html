GPS TAB

 

 

GPS Refresh Intervals

 

A user-defined time between inactivity to refresh the GPS data from trucks and stops. From within the Dispatch Center, it will default to refresh every five minutes.

 

Confirm Route GPS Upload Delay

 

The amount of time after the route was initially confirmed for the Dispatch Center to wait until the route information is transmitted to the GPS units. This allows safety in case we make a mistake. Time is set to default every five minutes.

 

Geoforce Radius Feet

 

The number of feet TSO recognizes the location of the vehicle. This is based on the radius of the vehicle location sent from TSO to determine the vehicle's location.
