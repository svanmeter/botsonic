INTERNAL | Item Purchase Detail Report


 

This article will go over the Item Purchase Detail Report. This article will cover looking to see why both the Items Quantity and Purchase Detail tab do NOT match. This should not be sent to customers and is for INTERNAL use only.



Topics in this article include
 
  • Accessing Internal Custom Reports
  • Report Parameter Values
  • Report Details



Access Internal Custom Reports

Here's how to get there
Elite: Right Menu > Reports > Custom

 

1. In the Report Name field, type in <DataDrive>:\POR\Reports.

2. Click View.


 

3. Enter in the POR Admin Passcode, if you don’t know ask a friend.


 



4. After you enter the POR Admin Passcode, this is going to open all the reports that are available in that folder. Look for the report called ‘ItemPurchaseDetails.SQL.rpt’.

5. Double-click to open the report.




Report Parameter Values

Once you open the report, it will ask you to enter the parameter values. In this report, there is only one, ItemNo. Place an * in the field and click ok.




Report Details

This will run the report in a list view and show all items where there is a discrepancy between Item Quantity and the Purchase tab.
