Syrinx Guide® - Labour Charges (Per Hour) 

 

 

 

 

Overview: This guide will help you create labour charges to be billed at month-end on a hire contract.

 

 

 

 

I have created in your database a category to add the different labour types as bulk items within Syrinx. To create new categories you can add new from the category screen within the Fleet tab.

 

 

To update, amend or create new items within this category, select bulk items from the fleet tab, then select Add New, Remove, or Amend from the bottom.

 

 

When building your hire contract, select labour type from the asset number section of the hire contract, and you can have the charge amount reflected per hour. The HOUR period is not periodic in that it only is added via charge units and not time like other hire charge periods.

 

 

You can add the hours at end of month before running the batch invoice for continuation hires in the Invoice tab, under Charge units.

 

 

When confirmed it will then be ready to invoice via a batch invoice.

 

It will display like this on the month-end invoice.

 

 

You can also add charge units to the off-hire, in the off-hire screen it will show at the bottom (Units).

 

 

This will update the cost box to reflect the labour charge imputed.

 

 

*The qty placed in the contract should always be left at (1). When entering units the quantity would duplicate based on the quantity selected at the contract level.
