Data Management Tools – Item Markup Percentage Change

 

Select # 5 - Item Percent Change From the Menu

 

Now you can make your Selection Criteria using the drop-down Boxes.  You can select a Division,  A Category, and or Items from a specific Vendor.  from the drop-down menus or leave them at all Categories.  

 

You Must also select the Type of Item that you want to update. This is the Type designated in the Item Record -- Rental Item, Sales Item, Etc.

 

You can enter the Previous item Percent in the From field, You could check the box for all Items.  In the box to the Right Enter the New Markup Percent that you want.

   

 

Click GO and the system will update item records.  You will get a pop window letting you know the process is Done.
