Income History by Group

 

This option will show the combined item income from all the items within the current Group.  This option is only available for items that have a Group defined. 


The chart will breakdown the total income by month and year, for the current year and the past seven years.

        *Note: You have the option of selecting individual store income or all stores. If select all stores, it will break out and display each store's income totals.
