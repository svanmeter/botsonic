12/19/2022 - Version 2022.17

 
  • Copying Suggested Items - For those of you with tents and components, you can now copy that 30x30 tent with all the poles and ropes and things to a 30x60, and it will copy the suggested items and you can just change quantities
 
  • Event and Delivery Notes enhancement - We have added a field in event/delivery locations called 'Additional Instructions'. If you add any new locations (not existing), you deliver to all the time, and want to store information that prints on the contract, here's your chance. "Gate code 1234. Set up inside the main gazebo area by the Bayou, and watch out for the Gators."
 
  • Rotate Condition Photos - If you take condition photos and they come up wonky sideways, you can now rotate to proper orientation before saving.
 
  • Sellable Items Report - Even if you still have an item marked for sale after you sold it, the report still showed it as for sale. No more.
 
  • Invoice Print View with a scroll bar - Some of you noticed that when viewing an invoice you ended up with a condensed version you had to scroll in order to see all of it. We corrected it, and made it full screen again.
