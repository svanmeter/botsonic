Customer Call Log by Group




Overview
 

The Customer Call Log by Group report prints all the call logs entered for the time you chose. The report can be grouped by Customer Name, Call Log Type, or Operator Name.


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Call Log by Group
Elite: Right Menu > Search bar > Customer Call Log by Group

 

The report will look like the following:
 


Note: You can click on the operator’s name on the left side of the screen to select the calls for that operator.
