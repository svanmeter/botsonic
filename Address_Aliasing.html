Address Aliasing




Overview

Address Aliasing allows you to enter the latitude and longitude for the delivery address. Address aliases can be used for drop locations that don't have an address just yet (new home developments, any construction jobsite, etc.). Aliases can also be used for when your customer needs their rentals to be dropped at a specific location (acreage, large farm property, large venue site, school/government campuses, etc.).

 

In the address line of the delivery address use this format to manually enter the latitude and longitude:

    
      LOC: 56.2356895,89.5487452
 

Note: ‘LOC:’ must precede the latitude and longitude.
