Launchpad - Transferring Sale Items into Rental Inventory

 

Overview: There are times when you might be running low or out of stock on certain rental items, you need to have transferred from your sales inventory. With a few clicks of a mouse, you can now move items from sales inventory to your rental inventory.

•Note: You must have a store manager or above to be able to transfer sales inventory to rental. Inventory.

1. From file record, select, Functions, then Transfer Sales Item to Rental

2. Enter Yes, will allow you to transfer the sale item into the rental item.

3. Select NO, if you already have the item created in your rental inventory. This will allow you to add inventory to the existing record. If you select YES, then Point of Rental will try to create a new rental record for you.

Note: If you select Yes, then a Rental Item Record will be created, where you can change the key name. Mostly used for new items that have not already been created in the inventory.

 

4. Select the rental inventory you are going to transfer the item into

5. Once selected, you will be prompted on how many you would like to transfer.

6. If the item has a header record, then the serialized record will automatically be created. Point of Rental will create the next serialized record in the sequence.

* Purchase detail will pull over to the rental item that was transferred.

*Last purchase price and purchase date will transfer to the rental item if the item is a bulk item.

 

In the case of serialized sales items, the user may wish to retain the sale item’s original item number/key when it is transferred to rental.  

  • The option in configurations must be enabled to use the item’s number as its key.  
  • The item number of the rental header the sales item is to be transferred to, must also be entered in the related header field on the options field of the sales item.
  • When the sales item is transferred to rental, the original item number/key will be carried forward to the rental item, and what would have been the rental item’s number is assigned to the old sales item.
