If you wish to amend the payment terms text found in the lower left of most invoice prints in Syrinx, you can do this via Syrinx SP Configuration\Documents tab\Payment Terms. There are various terms in the system which are potentially used for different invoice types. Also, keep in mind that specific payment terms text entries can be linked to specific customer records via the "Payment Terms" field on the "Accounts" tab of the "Amend Customer" screen.Â 

Users will also commonly add their bank sort code and account number to the payment terms entries so that these appear on all invoices.
