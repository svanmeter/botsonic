Customer Item History




Overview
 

This report will show all contracts and items rented on those contracts for a customer within the date range selected. The report includes the contract number, date, total amount, and item names. You will be asked to select the customer you want for the report as well as the date range. 


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Contract History Reports > Customer Item History
Elite: Right Menu > Search bar > Customer Item History

 

1. Select your desired customer.

2. Set your report date parameters.
