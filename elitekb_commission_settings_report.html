Commission Settings Report




Overview
 

This report displays the different types of commission based on the sales rep and the aging commission, customer commission, and item commission.


Here's how to get there: 
Expert: Program Menu > Accounting Reports > Sales Rep Reports > Commission Settings Report
Elite: Right Menu > Reports icon > Commission Settings Report
