Customer Payment History




Overview
 

This report will show all payments for a customer.  The report includes the payment number, contract number, date, paid amount, payment method, check number, and payment description. 


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Contract History Reports > Customer Payment History
Elite: Right Menu > Customer Payment History


Select the customer you want for the report.
