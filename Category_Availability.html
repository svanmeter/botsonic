Category Availability 

 

 

 

Overview
 

This article will discuss how to search for item availability by category and group.

 

Topics covered in this article include:

 

  • Viewing item availability
  • Category availability
  • Group availability

 

 

Viewing item availability

 

1. Item Availability can be accessed in two different ways: through Program Menu > Item Availability or through Counter System > Item Availability. ﻿

 

2. Select the item from the inventory selection window.

 

3. In the upper left of the window, click Availability and then either Category Availability or Group Availability.

 

 

  

Category availability

 

This opens a new window to display the availability for items in the corresponding category.

 

 

 

 

Group availability

 

This opens a new window to display the availability for the items in the corresponding group.
