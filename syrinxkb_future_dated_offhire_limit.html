If you attempt to print or confirm an offhire and the system issues the message, "The Off Hire Date cannot be this far in the future!!!", this means that your system has been set to not allow offhires dated greater than a specific number of days in to the future. You can alter this setting via the "Future Off-Hires Limit" setting on the "Off Hires" tab of Syrinx SP Configuration\System Options\Hire Contracts. Users may have to log out and then back in to Syrinx SP for this change to take effect.
