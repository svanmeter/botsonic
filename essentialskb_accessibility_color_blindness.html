As Point of Rental Software strives to be ADA compliant, this article will show you how to set the colors of your browser appropriately to accommodate the colors of Essentials. You can use Stylebot to make Essentials accessible to people with color blindness.

Topics included in this article:

 

  • Installing Stylebot on Chrome
  • After Stylebot is Installed

 

Installing Stylebot on Chrome

You can install an extension to your Chrome browser called Stylebot by following these instructions.

 

Navigate to https://chrome.google.com/webstore and search for Stylebot. Click Add to Chrome.

 

 

  

After Stylebot is Installed

1. After the extension is installed, right-click on the icon on your Chrome taskbar.

  

 

 

2. Navigate to Styles and click on Add a new style…

 

 

 

 

3. Enter https://pointofrentalcloud.com to the URL Section.

 

Note: This works for our other Rental Essentials sites as well. Please contact our support department to make sure that you are using the right URL.

 

 

  

4. Finally, paste in the styles below and click Save.
