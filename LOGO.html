LOGO

 

Overview: This button allows you to store your company logo in the database. It can be added to the top of your contracts and reports.

 

        a.    The logo must be saved on your server. For example, (drive): \pictures.

        b.    From server, right click on the logo image, select copy.

        c.    Go to Configurations>> System Configurations>> Parameters>> Store Logo 

            d.    Select Logo Button - Right Click>Paste

 

Logo Requirements

    • Image Size - Images should be 300 DPI or higher and should not exceed 640 pixels.
    • File Type - Images must be saved as a PGN, JPG, or JPEG.

 

 

 

NOTE*: To add a logo to the tops of contracts or reports, see Laser Contract Formats in Net Help.
