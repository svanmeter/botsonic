Dispatcher Notifications

 

Allows the dispatcher to be sent notifications based on the driver’s arrival or departure. Ex. The driver was late to arrive.

Check the appropriate box to set your message. Right-click inside the window to select special fields, to be incorporated into your message. These special fields will pull information based on the driver’s arrival or departure.

            Ex. If you select Contract Number, when the special field is added, the customer contract number will appear when receiving the notification.
