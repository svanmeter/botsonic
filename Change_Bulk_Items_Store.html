Change Bulk Item’s Store

 

 

 

 

Overview
 

You have written up a reservation and are excited to receive the revenue. However, you don't have the items in stock and so you will need to pull them from another location to capture the revenue. There is an option from within the contract to allow you to change a bulk item’s store. This will allow you to set all the bulk items to be from a different store than yours without changing the store of the contract.

 

 

Changing bulk item’s store

 

1. From within the contract, go to Tools > Change Bulk Item's Store. 

 

 

2. Select the store you are pulling the items from.

 

 

3. You will be asked to verify the sales tax jurisdiction for the new store location. The item will then be pulled from Store 2 and added to the contract.
