Overview Screen

 

 

Overview:  That you can move the overview screen from being the first screen you see to the end of the tab list. Also, you can remove certain tabs from Day at a Glance.

 

1.  Go to Program Menu>Security>Menu Security>Day at a Glance Tab

2.  Uncheck the overview box and select the terminals to move to the terminals in this group

Note: Your terminal Id can be found from your program menu under the Enter Selection (If your terminals are not by your name, then it could be list as PC1, 2, store1pc2, etc.)

 

 

3. Click OK
