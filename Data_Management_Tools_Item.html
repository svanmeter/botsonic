Data Management Tools – Item Percentage Change

 

1. Select # 4 - Item Percent Change From the Menu.

 

2. Now you can make your Selection Criteria using the drop-down Boxes.  You can select a Division and/or Category from the drop-down menus or leave them at all Categories.  You can also Manually enter the Old Item % in the box.  There is also a checkbox for all Items.  This will change all item records.

 

 

3. Enter the New Item percent in the NEW ITEM% Field.  

4. Click GO and the system will update item records.  You will get a pop window letting you know the process is Done.
