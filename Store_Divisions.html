Store Divisions

 

Store Divisions are used to add a larger grouping than Item Categories.  For example, if you have equipment rentals and party rentals at your store, you could create a division for Equipment Rentals and another one for Party Rentals.  Once a Store Division is created, you would set the appropriate Division in each Item Category.  For example, the Table & Chair category would be set to the Party Division.
