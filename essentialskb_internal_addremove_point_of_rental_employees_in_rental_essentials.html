Internal Notes

1. From the Point of Rental Billing Site go to People > Employees.

 
 


 

2. Click on +New Employee.
 


 

3. Fill out the following information that is required: Username, First\Last Name, Job Title, Email, and Main Phone Number.
 


 

4. Under the Role, select the one that is correct for the Employee's needs.
 


 

Note: If the Pointer needs impersonating powers, they should choose the ROLE_CUSTOMER_SERVICE. If they need to look at the companies tab to view multiple companies and add\remove company features they should be in ROLE_SUPER_ADMIN. The rule of thumb is that Product Support Tier 1 Agents get the ROLE_CUSTOMER_SERVICE. And Product Support Tier 2 Agents and Professional Services Consultants that have gone through Billing Site Training with Learning and Development should get ROLE_SUPER_ADMIN.
 

5. Document the creation of the account in Salesforce under the INTERNAL SYSTEM ACCOUNT using the following Subject Line ‘INTERNAL:ESSENTIALS:BILLING:PRODUCTION SITE:POINTER NAME‘

 

Deactivate the Employee

 

1. From the Point of Rental Billing Site go to People > Employees.
 


 

2. Search for the Employee that needs to be deactivated using the search bar.
 


 

3. Click the check box next to the pencil.
 


 

4. Click on Actions > Deactivate Employees.
 

 

5. Document the deactivation of the account in Salesforce under the INTERNAL SYSTEM ACCOUNT using the following Subject Line ‘INTERNAL:DEPARTMENT OF POINTER:ESSENTIALS BILLING:OFFBOARD:Pointers’ Name and ALL BILLING SITES WILL NEED TO BE CHECKED.
