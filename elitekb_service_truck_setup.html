Service Truck Setup

From Program Menu>Configurations>Delivery Trucks

A list will populate to show all your delivery trucks. Click on the Add button.

Store - Enter 000 for the store location.

Service Truck - Checking this box and entering 901 in the number field will allow you to store and/or transfer inventory to this location.

Weight – Weight of vehicle minus the payload.

Gross Vehicle Weight (GVW) – The total weight of the vehicle to include a payload.

Cubic Cap – This allows you to enter the max capacity of the be inside of the storage space by cubic inch.

 

When you go to transfer a contract through Counter Systems>New Contracts>Transfer Contracts. You will see this vehicle listed as a transferable location.

* Note: You must have the service truck module to transfer equipment to another location.
