Customers by No Statement




Overview
 

This article will discuss the Customers by No Statement report. This report lists all customers who have been marked as 'not to print a statement' in their customer record.


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Income Menu > Customer Income by Grouping > Group 1: No Statement
Elite: Right Menu > Reports > Search: Customer Income by Grouping


From the report generator, select No Statement for Group 1.
 


 

This is a drill down type of report where the first screen will look like the following:
 


You can double click on the options in blue font to view additional information.
