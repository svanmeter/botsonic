Changing Route Status

 

 
 

 

Overview

 

This article explains how to change the status of a delivery route in the Dispatch Center module. 

 

Topics included in this article:

 

  • Changing route status
  • Maintain stop sequence

 

 

Changing route status

 

1. Once the contract has been assigned to a truck, you will need to go to that truck and change the route status. In the left window pane of Dispatch Center, find the truck you have the route assigned to. Under the truck number, it will show you the assigned route.  

 

 

2. Right click on the route under the truck number. Select Route View.

 

 

3. Here you can change the route status from the drop-down menu. When finished, you will have the options to save and close, grid only, or map and grid. Select save when done.

 

 

4. When the status has been changed, you can drag and drop the contract from this route into another route. 

 

 

Maintain stop sequence

 

Right clicking on a route will give you the option to move a contract to the next business day. You can then select the option to maintain the original stop sequence when moving a contract or a route to a different day. This keeps service stops in order or keeps a priority stop at the top of the new route.
