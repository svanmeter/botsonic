  • Pictures on Contracts - You know what would be really cool? If you could print small pictures of your items on your contracts for your customers to see. Do you know what would be awesome cool rad? If you could print pictures on the contract, and you could also choose what statuses you want them to print on. Guess what? You can now do both.

 

  • Mobile Payments on Counter styling improvements - We have standardized spacing when taking payments on a mobile device.
 
Storefront Enhancements
 
  • Rate Display - The new rate display highlights either your daily rate or the smallest time rate. In addition the "View More Rates' now opens a carousel of rates, starting with the lowest on the left, with arrows to the right if there are more to display. PRO TIP - To optimize your consumer's experience and avoid redundancies, we suggest that you remove the word "Rate" from your price sheet where you may have Hourly Rate, Daily Rate, or Weekly Rate (as an example) and just have it read Hourly, Daily, Weekly (all caps or Title case will work). This will make a much cleaner user experience.
 
  • Storefront Minimum Period and Rate - We have changed the display so that if your lowest rate is the same as the minimum period and rate, we simply don't display the minimum since they are the same. If you do have a minimum that is different, then we will still highlight it using the new "stacked rate view" as the normal rates, but with a light grey background to call them out
 
  • Storefront Availability improvement - You now have a front-end configuration to Hide availability when no dates are selected

Note on Storefront Changes: We are constantly working to improve the user experience, performance, and visual layout of the Point of Rental Storefront service. As part of our ongoing efforts to improve the user experience, we have identified necessary structural changes to the Storefront HTML that include a high chance of impacting Custom CSS layouts. If you have Custom CSS, please follow the steps below to make sure you are not impacted.
 

  • Review your Custom CSS
  • Please check your storefront to confirm there are no conflicts with your custom CSS
  • Make any changes needed to ensure that your designs still function as intended
