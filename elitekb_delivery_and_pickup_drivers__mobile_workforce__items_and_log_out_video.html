Delivery and Pickup Drivers Mobile Workforce Items & Log Out

In this video, you will learn how to Items work within the Mobile Workforce Module. You will be able to see different search items and select from the following:
  • Take Photo
  • Count Items
  • Bin Update
  • Search for other items
Do these items have to be on contracts?

No, these can be from stock or on contracts. 

Once the item has been selected what can I do?
  • Update Meter Items
  • Take Photos
  • Update and See Counts
  • Create an IRO or IMO request
  • Update Bin Location
