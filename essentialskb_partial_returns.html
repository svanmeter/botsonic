This article is going to go over how to partially return a contract.



Do you have a customer who is done renting an item but needs to continue to rent other items? With a partial return on a contract, they can do just that.


1. In Essentials, you can click on Received to start the process.
 

 

2. When you're on the Receive Items Screen, select the item(s) that you want to receive.

3. Then choose your pricing. You can keep the original price or recalculate it.
 


On the contract, it is going to show the item(s) as returned.
