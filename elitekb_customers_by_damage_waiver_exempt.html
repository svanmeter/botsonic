Customers by Damage Waiver Exempt




Overview
 

This article will discuss the Customers by Damage Waiver Exempt report. This report lists all customers who have been marked 'damage waiver exempt' in their customer record.



Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Income Menu > Customer Income by Grouping > Group 1: Damage Waiver Exempt
Elite: Right Menu > Reports > Search: Customer Income by Grouping


From the report generator, select Damage Waiver Exempt for Group 1.
 


This is a drill down type of report where the first screen will look like the following:
 


You can double click on the options in blue font to view additional information.
