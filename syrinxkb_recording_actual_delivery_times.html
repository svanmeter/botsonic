Recording Actual Delivery Times

 

If required, Syrinx lets you record record the actual time that the deliveries take place, so that you can keep a record of late deliveries within the system.  The actual delivery time should be recorded after the delivery has been completed, but before the contract is confirmed:

If the delivery was later than the (proposed) delivery time, then you can also record the reason why it was late.  Extra reasons may be entered via the Reference Lists screen (In Configuration>General Setup>Reference Lists)

The same details can also be captured on the Delivery tab of the Enter Advice Note screen.

The Time Collected can also be captured on the Off Hire Contract screen.  When an actual time collected has been recorded, the off hire note is flagged as ‘Returned’ in the Unconfirmed Off Hires screen:

There is also an option on the ‘Must Enter’ tab of Syrinx SP Configuration\System Options\Contract Entry screen that can be set to enforce entry of the times:

Enabling this setting will affect both contracts and advice notes even though it would appear to be a contract specific setting.

 

Note: If the Driver App is in use on the system the actual delivery/collection times will be populated with the completed time value from the corresponding mobile delivery/collection job and does not need to be manually entered.
