Elite | Item Exchange History Report




This report will show exchanges for a given date range and can be grouped by store, division, category, item header, job site, or customer. It is useful for determining why exchanges are happening so that those areas can be addressed and improved to lower the exchange rate. This report is only available in Elite.

Here's how to get there

Elite: Right Menu > Item Exchange History

The following window will appear, allowing you to adjust your date range and your report grouping selections.
