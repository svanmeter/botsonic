Syrinx - Raising New Sales

 

 

 

 

Overview: This guide is designed to help you with the basics of raising a sale.

 

 

 

 

From the ribbon at the top, you need to go into Sales and click on New Sale. You will then be presented with the Identify Customer Box.

 

 

Once you have identified your customer, click Advice Note.

 

This will then open the Enter Advice Note screen. Start at the top and work your way down the screen.

 

 

The first section is all about customer information. In here you can store an Order Number, Delivery Address and Order Contact.

 

Note:   If the contact or site address isn’t in the drop-down, then you can press Insert on your keyboard.

  

Once you have entered all the information, you can move on to the item details.

 

When entering the items, you can do it in several ways. You can use the dropdowns and select a category, followed by the stock number. Alternatively, you can search on description or stock/serial/barcode number by typing these into the stock no field and press Enter.

 

Once you have entered the item details, the information will be populated as below:

 

There are various tabs running across the page that can be used:

 

Items

Details of the items being sold to the customer.

Orders

Allows you to raise purchase orders if the item being sold is not in stock.

Details

You can add notes here, you can also select how the customer heard of you.

Delivery

You can select an additional contact, and add delivery/site notes.

Log

This tab will show you any changes made to the sale and the user responsible for the change.

Profit

This will show any profit on the sale, you are able to switch this off at a user permission level.

 

Once you are happy with your advice note, you can then print any documentation required. When you click Print at the bottom of the screen, you will be faced with various options as shown below. Select which document you require and click OK.

 

 

 

From here you can print the document by clicking Quick Print (this will use your default printer), or, alternatively, you can email the document straight to your customer.

 

Once you have printed/emailed your document, your last step is to confirm the advice note. This is done by clicking the Confirm button, and now the advice note will be charged. If you do not want to confirm the advice note immediately then you can click Close and confirm it later.

 

 

 

Notes

Any notes entered here will appear on the documentation when printed.

Depot

This is the depot that has generated this sale.

Split

If you have multiple items on the sale and you would like to split the sale into two different sales, you can do this here.

Payments

On clicking Payments, the system will tell you how much this sale is going to cost the customer.

This button will only become active if it’s a non-account customer.

Quote

Ticking the quote box allows you to raise a sales quotation.

Print

This allows you to print/email any paperwork to the customer.

Confirm

This will confirm the sale on to the system, on clicking confirm the customer will be charged and the stock will have updated.

Staff

This is the person that initially raised the sales advice note.

Close

This will close the sales advice note screen down once you click to close the sale will then sit in unconfirmed advice notes.
