Driver Trip Sheet



 

This article will discuss the Driver Trip Sheet. This report is used with the Dispatch Center. The report lists all the items on the route along with any associated comments. It also gives the delivered item weight and pickup item weight and provides a place for the driver to fill out a checklist of checking oil, lights, brakes, etc.

The Driver Trip Sheet will report more information if there are more details entered in the truck details. For more information see our article, Truck Details. For example, if the vehicle weight or cubic capacity is left at 0 it will not display in the Driver Trip Sheet.



Getting there

Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Counter System > Dispatch Center > Reports > Driver Trip Sheet by date
Elite: Left Menu > Tools > Dispatch Center > Reports > Driver Trip Sheet by date
