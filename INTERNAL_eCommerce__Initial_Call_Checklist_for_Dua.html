eCommerce | Initial Call Checklist for Dual and WP Plugins

INTERNAL

 

 

 

Topics in this article include

  • Questions for the customer
  • Tasks for us to do

 

 

Questions for the customer

 

Do they have item images inserted for most of their items in POR, none, or very few?

 

Do they use header records?

 

Will they have multiple catalogs, i.e., party, equipment, sales, parts, supplies, etc. - discuss Divisions

 

After reviewing their categories, discuss name changes, organizational ideas, etc. as needed.

 

Is there a need for Suggested Item Package records?

 

 

 

Tasks for us to do

 

  • Ask for a TeamViewer connection and take a look at their categories. With the customer's help, exclude categories that should not upload to our server.
  • Send links to the following how-to articles:
    • Item Images
    • All About Item Records
    • eCommerce | All About Item Specs
    • Excluding Items & Categories
    • Super Categories
    • Updating the Website in Auto Mode
    • Updating the Website in Configuration Mode
    • Website Organization
    • Suggested Item Package Records - if needed
  • Give them a target date for the first review of data changes
