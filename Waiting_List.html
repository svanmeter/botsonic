Waiting List

 

Overview: There are times when you might not have your equipment available for a customer; you have the ability to add them to a waiting list. Upon the return of the item, you will be prompted with a message there is a customer waiting for this equipment.

 

A customer can be added to a waiting list through Item Availability, select the waiting list button at the bottom of the screen.

* Note: If the waiting list button is missing, then the item you are viewing in item availability does not have the box checked to allow waitlist in the file record.

 

Wait List – This button will put a customer on the waiting list for this item. This button will only be displayed if the item has “Allow Wait List” checked. When you click the button, the following screen is displayed:

 

Number -  Will assign a number to record the customer is being added to the waiting list.

Item – Description of the item out of stock

Call taken by – Person entering order

Call in on – Date customer place order

Call back by – Person calling customer once the item is back in stock

Call back on – Date customer was called back

Customer – Name of the customer requesting equipment.

            * Note – Clicking on the blue customer name will open the customer selection screen.

Phone – Customer telephone number

            * Note – Once the customer is selected, the phone number will auto-populate in this field.

Quantity – How many of the item

Notes – This allows you to add in any notes or comments.

 

 

Once the customer has been added, the following message will appear on the screen, from Item Availability or if someone tries to add the item to a contract.

 

You can view all customers who are actively on a waiting list from Day at a Glance. Go to Counter System>Day at a Glance>select Waiting List tab.

 

 Deleting Waiting List

 

You can delete the customer from the waiting list either through Item Availability or Day at a Glance.

 

Item Availability - Select the item customer is waiting for. Waiting list message will populate, it will ask you “If you want to view”, select Yes> Click on the delete button.

Day at a Glance – Click on an item, waiting list message will populate>You will ask you “If you want to view”, select Yes> Click on the delete button> Do you really want to clear customer, select Yes
