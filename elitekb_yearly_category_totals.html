Yearly Category Totals


Location: Program Menu > Analysis Report > Income Analysis Reports > Yearly Category Totals.


Overview: The Yearly Category Totals report shows each category’s total income for the current year-to-date and each of the previous years on record.

These totals are made up of the individual income from the (Rental, Sale, or Parts) assigned under each category. 
 

View Options:  You can choose how to view the Yearly Category Totals:
 
  • Rental Items,
  • Sales Items,
  • Part Items,
  • Store Location
