External Transport Order

 

If your company outsources the delivery of inventory, there is an easy way to create transport documentation and Purchase Order for your transport company.

 

1. Create an item with key that starts with |ETR. This should be a bulk sales item. You can create as many |ETR items as needed (i.e. |ETR-LARGE |ETR01 |ETRA etc…)


 

2. Write your contract as you normally would, but when prompted to select a Delivery Fee, choose one of your |ETR delivery records.
 

3. Right click the |ETR item and choose the ‘Transport Order’ option.
 

4. The Transport Order screen will open to this:
 



5. Complete all required fields.
 

6. The transport order can be saved and modified until you are ready to create an order for your transport vendor.
 

7. Create Transport Order will create a new Purchase Order that can be printed or emailed to your vendor.  This will not be available in the modification the ETR item is added during.  The contract will need to be saved first and then the user can go back and creat the transport order.  This prevents users from cancelling the contract modification and creating a stranded transport order.
 

a. If required information is missing, for example, the address, the user will be flagged and these fields must be completed first.
 

8. The Transport Order is created as an open purchase order (as opposed to a pending status when ordering inventory).
 


 

9. The Transport Order resembles a standard purchase order, with a few exceptions:

a. Ship to area is replaced with a ‘Transport Order Detail’ button in the top left corner.

b. This will open the transport order screen from the contract in a ‘read only’ mode.
 

10. When modifying the Transport Order from Purchase Orders, the user will be notified that no adjustments to the line items are allowed.

a. Changes with respect to line items will have to be done from the transport order screen in the contract.

b. Additional line items cannot be added to the Transport Order.
 

11. Once the transport is complete, right click on the PO from the dashboard and ‘receive all items’. From there, it follows the usual process of assigning an invoice number, payment method, etc. to ready it for posting to AP.
 

12. Once the purchase order is posted, no more changes can be made within the transport order screen; unless the PO is unposted first.
 


13. Printing the purchase order for the external transport will use a specific format that gives the transport company all the information needed to complete the job.
