Understanding the bill of lading

A bill of lading is a legal document between a shipper and a carrier detailing the type, quantity, and destination of the goods being carried.

Creating a bill of lading

Generate bills of lading for shipments to ensure proper documentation and tracking.

  1. Access the shipment module in the system.
  2. Enter the necessary details of the shipment to create a new bill of lading.
  3. Include information such as shipper, carrier, item descriptions, and destination.

Managing and updating bills of lading

Maintain accurate records by managing and updating bills of lading as needed.

  1. Review and verify details in the bill of lading for accuracy.
  2. Make updates to the bill of lading if there are changes in the shipment details.
  3. Ensure all parties involved have the updated version of the bill of lading.
