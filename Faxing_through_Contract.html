Faxing through Contract

 

From any contract, once you pass the payment screen, you will be given the option to either, print, fax, or email your contract to your customer. Once you select fax, you will see the No Print/Print button, change to No Fax/No Fax.

 

 

If you are faxing the contract to the customer, you can select the method on how they are to send the fax. Either as regular fax or fax with Cover Page. If you are selecting fax with a cover page, see document (creating cover page) under Net Help Email documents.

 

If sending a fax, select a fax and enter the fax number. You will have the option from the drop-down menu to select a fax cover sheet if one has been created.

 

You have the option to view the contract prior to sending the fax by selecting the Preview PDF button.

 

Select Send.
