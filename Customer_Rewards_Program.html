Customer Rewards Program

 



Overview

These rewards can be used for future rentals and you can define the expiration date on the rewards, setting them to be valid for a certain time, to encourage your customers to come back within the valid time frame to get the benefit.

  • Customer Rewards Details

How to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Configuration > Customer Configurations > Rewards Program
Elite: Gear Icon > App Configurations > Customer Configurations > Rewards Program



Customer Rewards Details
 

 

From the customer record in File Maintenance > Customer File > Pricing Tab, you check the box for Customer Rewards Membership, enrolling the customer into the program.
 

 

Cash Customers - Customer Rewards will be accrued on contracts paid in full. There are no rewards accrued on partial payments.
 

Note: If a cash contract is paid in full and items are returned, then a negative reward would be deducted from the accrued rewards amount.

 

Account Customers - Customer Rewards will be accrued only on completed contracts.
 

Accrued Button - Will display a list of all contracts the customer has accrued rewards. The Rewards Detail record will list total rewards earned and remaining on the top line, followed by the breakdown of the rewards by each contract.

 


 

Customer Rewards are calculated at the end of each day when you finish your close. The customer rewards are not available for the customer until the next day.

 

Note: If you close monthly, then the Customer Rewards will not be available until the next month.
 

If the customer is enabled for the customer web portal, they will also be able to see the rewards they have accrued in the portal. This allows them to easily reference and track what is available for use. They will need to reach out to the rental counter to have them applied to a contract, as they are unable to do this through the portal.

 

 

When a customer accrues rewards, you will receive a message from within the payment screen on a new contract, allowing you to apply the rewards to the contract.
 

Note: You will not have the option of applying less rewards then the amount accrued. If the amount of the rewards exceeds the value of the contract, then the rewards balance will roll over.

 

Note: Payment type will be listed as rewards in the transaction payment history through the contract and on the disbursement report.

 

 

Refunding Rewards

 

Moving your mouse over the Use Rewards button will display the rewards amount in the Tool Tip pop-up. A detailed list of the rewards can be seen in the customer record. You can refund customer rewards back to the customer's account by simply selecting refund the amount applied.
 

For example, if the contract total was $500, and the customer paid $495.50 in cash and used 4.50 in rewards, you would first refund the real monies paid, then you can refund the rewards separately. When refunding the rewards, you will see the following message.

 


 

General Ledger Entries
 

When rewards are applied to a contract, accounts receivable is lowered and a disbursement account is increased. If you have created a disbursement account with a name that starts with Customer Rewards, or Loyalty, it will auto choose this disbursement; otherwise it will look for a misc. disbursement item or use disbursement #1.


 

Mass Update Customers for Customer Rewards

 

Users can enroll customers into the customer rewards program in mass. See Database Management Tools for more information.
 

Note: By selecting Mass Database all customers entered in the program will share the same percentage of rewards assigned to the rental or sale of a contract.
 

For example, if two customers are in the rewards program and there is a 10% reward, both customers will receive the same percentage. If the amount of business each customer does is negligible, the percentage is still the same. Reward percentages cannot be customized per customer.
