Preventing Rate Changes

 

Overview

If Syrinx has been configured with accurate hire rates/sales prices for all items that you hire/sell, and if you have pricing agreements for all your customers that have non-standard rates, prices or discounts, then you may want to prevent your users from amending the charges when contracts are entered or amended.  Similarly, you may want the same restrictions for advice notes.

There are two role privileges in the system configuration that can be revoked to prevent certain users from changing the rates/prices on Hire Contracts and/or Advice Notes.  There is also a menu option that can be used by a manager to authorise changes on a contract by contract basis.

Configuration

To turn the feature on, you must revoke one or both of the privileges ‘Front Desk.Amend Contract Rates’ and ‘Front Desk.Amend Adv Note Prices’ from users that are to be forced to stick to the given rates/prices.  

You should also grant access to the screen ‘User Setup.Authorise Non-standard Rate’ to any users that are to be allowed to grant update access to individual hire contracts and advice notes.

Contract Entry / Amendments

If the privilege ‘Front Desk.Amend Contract Rates’ has been revoked from a user, then the hire rate, sales price and discount fields cannot be amended when entering new contracts:

This is also true in the Add/Amend Hire/Sales Item options within the ‘Amend Hire Contract’ screen.

Manual Override

To let a user change the rates/prices on a particular contract or advice note, you can use the ‘Authorise Non-standard Rate’ screen on the ‘User Setup’ menu:

The user must provide you with the contract or advice note number, and the reason why the change is required.  You can then use this screen to grant the request, entering the details provided.  The user can then go back into the contract/advice note and the fields will then allow amendments to be made.

The manual override only applies to the contract/advice note stated, and only applies for the duration of that day.

 

Hire Contract Log

Rate change authorisations are shown in the contract log alongside regular log entries:

They can also be queried and printed from the ‘Query Contract Log’ screen.

Allowing Hire Desk staff access to the Manual Override screen

If you have all your rates, prices and customer agreements set up, and you want further control but you don’t want to have to authorise every adjustment, then you could consider giving access to the ‘Authorise Non-standard Rate’ screen to your users.  This way, each deviation from the given rates will be documented in the contract hire log, and you can then review these contracts via the ‘Query Contract Log’ screen.
