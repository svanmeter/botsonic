Capitalized Equipment Listing

 

 

This report shows all the capitalized equipment that was purchased between the dates you select. 

 

 

Generating the report

 

  1. Go to Program Menu > Accounting Reports > Fixed Assets Reports Menu > Capitalized Equipment. Enter your password when prompted.
  2. Select the date range and click Generate.
  3. Enter the minimum purchase price to report and then click OK.
