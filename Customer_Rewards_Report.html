Customer Rewards Report




Overview
 

The Customer Rewards Report will show you accrued rewards given to customers who are enrolled in the Rewards Program.

Topics include:

  • Active Rewards
  • Inactive Rewards
  • Accrued Summary



Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Accounting Reports > Accounting Reports Menu > Customer Rewards Report
Elite:


 

The report will break down the rewards by Accrued, Redeemed, and Remaining, showing you, the total amount of rewards set in Active or Inactive status. 

You can see which customer accounts are eligible for the rewards program by going to Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Income Menu > Customer Income by Grouping > Group 1 - select Customer Rewards. 

 


 

Active Rewards
 

This section will break down the total amount of rewards accrued, redeemed, and remaining. The total amounts will be broken down when the rewards are set to expire by month. Expiration dates are constantly rolling; the rewards will expire between the time customer was entered in the Rewards Program to the expiration time set in configurations.
 

Note: You can set the expiration date under Program Menu > Configuration > Customer Configurations > Rewards Program.

 

 

Inactive Rewards

 

This section will display the rewards that have been deemed inactive. These rewards have either expired or the customer has been deemed not to receive the rewards, by un-checking the Customer Rewards box in the Customer Record. You will still see the rewards accrued, but the customer will not be able to use them.
 

Note: You can opt a customer out of the Customer Rewards Program by going to File Maintenance > Customer File > Pricing Tab > Uncheck the box for the Rewards program.


 

Accrued Summary

 

The active rewards are accumulated and posted to the report at the close of each day. The summary will show the total amount of rewards accumulated in the current and previous year. To view redeemed rewards for a date range, run the Payments Summary Report in Accounting Reports.
