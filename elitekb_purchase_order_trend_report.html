Purchase Order Trend Report



 

The Purchase Order Trent Report, also known as the Purchase Order Analysis Report, shows the dollar value of purchases broken out for rental inventory, resale, and parts for three user-defined periods. This report can be grouped by company, store, division, category, vendor, item, or month.

Getting there

Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Purchase Order Analysis
Elite: Right Menu > Purchase Order Analysis
