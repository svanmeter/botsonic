eCommerce | Replacing/Adding Format to the Website

INTERNAL



 

When a customer needs to change, delete, or add PDF forms, policies, guides, etc. to their website, most likely on their Resources page, they need to follow the directions found in our help article, eCommerce | Managing Resource Galleries.

If, however, they need updates to a landing page (which is an actual page on the website, not a linked PDF), they will need to send their changes to eCommerce Support.
