If a service reminder is not appearing as "due" even though the reminder date falls within the "Show Reminders due within next X days" setting when running the service reminders report, you should verify that the item has been set as an individual hire item. This can be checked on the "Stock" tab of the "Amend Hire Stock Items" screen which is accessible via the Syrinx SP\Fleet tab\Amend menu option. If the "Individual?" tick box is not ticked against the hire stock item, the reminder will not appear in the service reminders screen.
