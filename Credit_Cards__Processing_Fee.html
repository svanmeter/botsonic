Credit Cards | Processing Fee



This article is going to show you how to create a Credit Card Processing Fee. Credit card processors charge businesses a certain percentage to handle their transactions. Sometimes, the business will want to pass this cost on to its customers. Point of Rental has a specific way of handling this. Your system must be credit card integrated to utilize this feature.

If you also have the WebPay Module that is managed by Rental Hosting, Contact the Product Support team at support@rentalhosting.com so they can enable the Credit Card Fee Option in your WebPay Configurations.


Topics in this article include
     

  • Creating a processing fee item
  • Making credit card transactions on a contract
  • Making credit card transactions on an account payment
  • Adding a credit card processing fee through Rental Hosting 
  • How the Credit Card Processing Fee shows up based on Reporting





Creating a processing fee item

The only reporting for the Credit Card Processing Fee will come from this item.

Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > File Maintenance > Item File
Elite: Configuration Gear > File Maintenance > Item File

When you're in File Maintenance, click on ‘Add Item’. The item must be created with the following specifications.


  • To use this feature the Key must be ‘CC-Fee’ or CC-Fee-#, where # is the Store Number. This is so that you can track the revenue from the fee per store as well as if you are needing to change different fee amounts

  • Type: Sales-Normal

  • Qty: 99,999

  • Under Rates place the fee that you want to charge in the Sell Price. For example, if you want to charge a 3% fee, you will enter the fee as 3.00 in the Sell Price field.

  • Under the Options tab, check the box to state that this is a ‘Bulk Item’.
    Also, under the Options tab, specify if this item is taxable or nontaxable.

  • Credit should be marked as Suppress fulfillment and Item Availability.




Making credit card transactions on a contract

Once the item is set up, you can take a credit card payment on the contract screen. In this example, we will take a $100 credit card payment on the contract.



The total that will be charged is the payment plus the fee percentage of the Credit Card Processing Fee. This will reflect in the amount section of the Credit Card Screen.



Once the payment is made, the credit card processing fee and any applicable taxes will be applied to the contract. Any subsequent credit card payments will be added to the credit card processing line item. This item is added by the system in the credit card payment process and does not have to be added by the user.



 

Making credit card transactions on an account payment

This fee is also charged on any account payments that are taken by credit card. When this occurs, a closed contract is automatically created to apply the credit card fee. In the example below, a $100 payment plus a 3% credit card fee was taken. The $100 would be applied against the reservation and the $3.25 against the closed CC Fee contract.


 

Adding a credit card processing fee through Rental Hosting

After you are done adding the item, if you also have the WebPay Module, please contact Rental Hosting Support. This is so they can enable the Credit Card Fee Option in your WebPay Configurations.


Reporting Credit Card Processing Fees

The Credit Card Processing Fee will only be reported through this item. It's recommended that you create a separate category to track these fees. Check out our article on creating Item Categories for more information on how to create and assign item categories.
