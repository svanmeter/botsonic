Custom Questions

This article explores the use of custom questions to gather specific information from users, customers, or employees in your system or surveys.

Topics included in this article

  • Benefits of using custom questions
  • Designing effective custom questions
  • Implementing custom questions in your system or surveys
  • Collecting and analyzing responses

Benefits of using custom questions

Custom questions offer valuable insights and help tailor experiences and solutions to individual needs.

Designing effective custom questions

Create custom questions that are clear, relevant, and yield actionable information.

  1. Define the purpose of each custom question and what information you aim to gather.
  2. Use concise and unambiguous language in your question wording.
  3. Consider different question types, such as multiple-choice, open-ended, or rating scales, based on your goals.

Implementing custom questions in your system or surveys

Integrate custom questions seamlessly into your system or surveys to gather targeted data.

  1. Access the customization or survey creation tools in your system.
  2. Add custom questions to relevant forms or surveys.
  3. Test the questions to ensure they function correctly and provide the desired data.

Collecting and analyzing responses

Efficiently collect and analyze responses to custom questions to inform decision-making.

  1. Set up automated response collection processes, if possible.
  2. Analyze responses to identify trends, preferences, or areas for improvement.
  3. Use the insights gained from custom questions to enhance user experiences or make informed decisions.
