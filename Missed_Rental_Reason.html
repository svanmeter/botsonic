Missed Rental Reason

 

How to get there: Program Menu > Configurations > Item Configurations >Missed Rental Reason

 

This option allows you to create a list of reasons why the customer was not able to rent what they were looking for. The reason will be listed on the Missed Rentals Report that you find under Analysis Reports; Inventory Reports.

If you want to remove the Rental Reason, then click on the line, and select Inactive.
