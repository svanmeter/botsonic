What is uLearn? 

uLearn is a Salesforce Community Site that gives our customers access to manage their cases and view our Salesforce Knowledge Base, which includes help articles, videos, and release notes. Our goal for uLearn is to empower our customers by giving them the tools to learn about our products while reducing support dependence.
 

How can users access uLearn?

Both our customers and fellow Pointers can access uLearn using a Chromium-based browser (Microsoft Edge, Chrome, Vivaldi, etc...). Customers can access uLearn through their products, while Pointers can find uLearn within Salesforce's POR Tools. uLearn can also be accessed by using the below links.

💡Please note - Customers DO NOT need to create an account or sign in to access uLearn.  
 

  • Expert and Elite - https://por.my.site.com/uLearn/s/
  • Syrinx and Syrinx 365 - https://por.my.site.com/syrinxkb/s/
  • Essentials - https://por.my.site.com/essentialskb/s/



Accessing uLearn through Each Product

Follow the below paths to get to uLearn:

  • Expert: Program Menu or Counter System > Top Menu Options > Help > uLearn
  • Elite: Home screen > Right Menu > Help > uLearn
  • Syrinx (only versions v4.40.6 and later) : User tab > Feedback > uLearn
  • Syrinx 365: Home screen > Right Menu > Help > uLearn
  • Essentials: Help Icon 


Do customers have to signup for uLearn? 

No! Customers can access uLearn without logging in. However, they must do so and have their account validated to view cases. 


How do customers create an account?

Customers do NOT have to create an account to access uLearn. Below are the steps our customers can use to create a uLearn account. Customers must have a verified account to manage/view their cases in uLearn.
  1. Click the Sign Up link, in the bottom right corner of the login screen. 

 
  1. Fill in your information and press Submit. Please use your company email address and phone number. 
 

 
  1. Once you have submitted your information, uLearn will send an email to the email entered above.
 
  • If you already have an existing account, the email will provide you with a link to reset your password.
 
  • If you DO NOT have an existing account, the email will confirm your registration or notify you that we have created a support ticket to confirm your information. Meanwhile, you'll still be able to access helpful articles and videos. To view cases your account must be verified.
 
  1. Using the email you provided as the username, go back to uLearn and log in.



What happens after the customer signs up for a uLearn account?

Once a user attempts to register, they will receive a message either CONFIRMING registration or letting them know we created a case to look into their registration request. 

A case will be created internally for the CSS team to validate the user's request and identity if Salesforce cannot match the request with an existing contact. 


Here are the scenarios:

  1. If the customer already has an existing uLearn account, the email sent to them will provide them with a link to reset their password.
  1. If the customer DOES NOT have an existing account and the email provided DOES NOT MATCH with a contact record in Salesforce,  the message they will receive will let them know that Point of Rental has created a case to verify their account.
    • Let the customer know that we are working on validating their account. They will still be able to access helpful articles and videos. They will receive an email to create their account. 
  1. If the customer DOES NOT have an existing account and the email DOES MATCH with a contact record in Salesforce, they will receive a message that their registration was successful. They'll also be sent an email to create their password. 
To learn how to validate the customer's uLearn account, please see the topic below "Deactivate & Validate Users".

Validating & Deactivating User Accounts

By default, when our customers create a uLearn account and their email address isn't recognized by Salesforce, they'll be added to Salesforce as an "unverified" contact. 
 
 
💡Please note - If there are contact duplicates see the next topic Duplicate Contact Records.

You must verify the contact per an authorized Point of Rental user (i.e. Manager, Owner, Regional Manager).
  • If you're unsure of which contact is authorized to verify the new uLearn user, contact your supervisor or management team. 
  • Per Call Support customers CANNOT be verified.

After following up with an authorized user regarding the contact, you can update the Contact's Type field (Main account (Rental Co) > Contact (Jane Doe) > Detail (tab) > Type (field). There are 3 types. It's important to know what each type signifies and behaves as they are all different.
 
  • Unverified - With this status the contact will be able to access help articles and videos within uLearn. These users cannot view cases. This status is assigned to the contact by default.  
 
  • Verified - Customers will be able to view cases and access help articles and videos. Change the contact to this status once you have verified them.  
 
  • Non-user - Customers will not be able to view cases and access help articles or videos. Change the contact to this status if the contact no longer works for our customer or if their manager/supervisor does not want their employee to have access. 

How to change or update the contact status

In Salesforce, find and open the customer's account. 
  1. Locate and open the Contact record
 
  1. Go to the Details tab
 
  1. Modify the Type field by pressing on the pencil icon and select an option. 


Duplicate Contact Records

Contact support management to merge users. 
If there is not a uLearn User record with the email that was used to sign up, then the first contact record with a matching email is used to create a new uLearn (CommunityUser) record. Duplicate contact records can be merged at any time.


Chat Feature

The chat feature is not available for all uLearn users. Here's the breakdown of who can use the chat and how it works. 
 
     Essentials 
  • General Essentials Customer - Will have access to chat that is directed to general support. 
  • Premiere Customer - Will have access to chat that is directed to the Premiere Support team.

     Expert/Elite
  • General Expert/Elite Customer - Will not have access to chat.
  • Premiere Customer - Will have access to chat that is directed to the Premiere Support team.
   
     Syrinx/365 
  • Customers will not have access to chat.
 
How it works

Salesforce will grant access to the Premiere chat for uLearn users (contact) that belong to an Account that is a Premier Support Account. You can confirm the status of the customer's support type status by going to Customer Account > Details tab. If the box is unchecked, the customer is not a Premiere Support member.



 

Troubleshooting | Accessing uLearn 

 
  • Verify the customer is using a Chromium-based browser, for example, Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Vivaldi. Mac users will be able to use Safari 15+. However, this has not been tested.

 

  • Verify the customer has their Chromium browser set as the default browser. If a customer does not have a Chromium-based browser, they cannot log into uLearn.

  Troubleshooting | No Audio/Sound

Elite/Expert: If a customer is unable to hear audio from help videos or they have NO SOUND, have them contact their IT department to see if their server has sound drivers installed.

  • If a customer is still having issues AFTER installing sound drivers onto their server, create a ticket for the Salesforce team.

Essentials: Give the customer basic sound troubleshooting options. For example, making sure the browser tab or computer is not muted or restarting the web browser and computer. 


Troubleshooting | Forgot Password

  • If a customer forgot their password, they can click on the Forgot Password link on the site. This will send them an email to reset their password. 

  • If the customer is still having issues, you may manually reset the customer's password. Your Salesforce profile will need to be given access to reset passwords for uLearn users. If you are unable to do so, contact the Salesforce team.

    • Click this article on how to reset the uLearn (community user) password, Reset Passwords and Deactivate Community Users.

 


Troubleshooting | Limited View & Navigation Bar Options

Limited View
Within Expert/Elite some components like the Contact Support and Submit Your Ideas will not be available for Unverified users. 

Limited Navigation Bar Options
For Expert, Elite, Syrinx, and Syrinx 365If the customer's uLearn navigation bar is limited or does not have access to every option, they're account may still need to be validated. Unvalidated users will not have access to all options. For further assistance, please see the previous topic "Validating or deactivating user accounts."
