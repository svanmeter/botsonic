Customer Online Storefront

This article provides information on setting up and managing your customer online storefront.

Topics included in this article

  • Setting up the storefront
  • Customizing storefront appearance
  • Managing storefront products
  • Customer interactions

Setting up the storefront

Setting up your online storefront involves several steps to ensure it accurately represents your business and offerings.

  1. Access the Storefront settings in your system’s configuration menu.
  2. Choose the templates and layouts that best fit your brand.
  3. Set up categories and products to be displayed in the storefront.

Customizing storefront appearance

Customize the look and feel of your storefront to match your brand’s identity.

  1. In the Storefront settings, select the Customize Appearance option.
  2. Adjust colors, fonts, and layout to match your branding.
  3. Preview changes before applying them to ensure they meet your expectations.

Managing storefront products

Manage the products displayed on your storefront for an optimized customer experience.

  1. Add new products or edit existing ones under the Products section.
  2. Set product descriptions, images, pricing, and availability.
  3. Organize products into categories for easier navigation.

Customer interactions

Engage with customers through the storefront to enhance their shopping experience.

  1. Enable customer reviews and feedback for products.
  2. Set up automated responses for customer inquiries.
  3. Monitor customer activity to understand shopping preferences and behaviors.
