Correct Previous Payment






This article will discuss how to correct a previous payment. There are many reasons why you may need to correct an account payment. This article will cover two primary reasons a payment may need to be corrected. One reason may be that the payment itself is incorrect and needs to be voided. The other may be that the payment is correct but applied to the wrong invoice(s). In this case, the payment does not need to be corrected except to be reposted to the correct invoice(s).
 

Note: This method should only be used if you are correcting a payment on the same day. It is not recommended to use this option if you are trying to correct a payment from a different day.



Topics in this article include
 

  • Voiding a Payment
  • Correcting a Payment

 

Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Accounts Receivable > Account Payments
Elite: Accounting > Accounts Receivable Dashboard
 

Note: In Elite after you have made it to the Accounts Receivable Dashboard, you will select your desired customer. This will open the Customer Dashboard. Select Tools and then Make Account Payment.

 


Voiding a Payment
 

1. Select the desired customer.

 

2. Select Correct Previous Payment.

 

 

Next you will see a display listing all payments and contracts for this customer, with the most recent at the top.
 


 

3. Click one of the lines displaying the payment you want to void.
 

Note: There may be more than one line if multiple contracts were paid; it is only necessary to select one of the lines.

 

4. You will see a message box asking to verify that it is the correct payment to back out. Select Yes. 

 

Note: The screenshot below shows what appears to be seven payments; there are actually five payments. Each payment has its own unique reference number or check number and the grid shows each contract paid.
 


 

5. A payment receipt screen will appear. You will have the option to print, fax, or email the receipt. When finished, select OK.

 

 

6. You will be asked if you have any more payments to enter. Select Yes.

Note: This will return you to the customer selection screen.



Correcting a Payment
 

1. Return to the customer you just voided a payment for.

2. Select Make a Payment.


 

3. A payment window will appear. Enter your payment amount.

4. Select your payment type from the dropdown.

5. Check the box if this payment will be going into your drawer.



6. Press OK.
 

7. A confirmation window will appear. Select Yes.



8. Enter the payment description. Press OK.



9. Once finished with the contract, press OK.
