Contracts Opened per Day




Overview
 

This report shows how many contracts were opened, sent out, and closed each day.


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Transactions Analysis > Contracts Opened per day
Elite: Right Menu > Contracts Opened

 

1. Select your parameters and click Generate.
