Depreciation Schedule

 

How to get there: Program Menu > Configurations > Accounting Configurations > Depreciation Schedules

 

You can have up to 5 depreciation schedules for each item record.  As installed, the 5 schedules are Federal Tax, State Tax, Bank Value, Property Tax Value, and Internal Value. The names of the schedules may be customized to suit your individual needs. Schedule 1 is the only depreciation schedule that will post to your General Ledger as indicated by the “GL Posting” next to it. All other schedules are included for informational purposes.
