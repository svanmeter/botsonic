Subrental Report

 

 

The Subrental Report shows all the items that need are scheduled to be subrented from another rental store.  You must first create the Subrental contracts under Counter System; Tools; Contracts; Subrental.

 

 

 

Subrental Analysis Report

 

The Subrental cost assigned to each item will be posted to that item's Subrental cost in POR and you will be able to see, by looking at the item record, the income generated and the cost incurred by the Subrental.  The Subrental Analysis report will show the cost of the item subrented from the vendor and the amount charged to the customer. The net amount will be displayed showing the difference between the two amounts.

 

Program Menu>Analysis Report>Transaction Analysis Report>Subrental Analysis.
