New Customer

This article guides you through the process of adding a new customer to the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Creating a new customer profile
  • Entering customer details
  • Setting up customer preferences
  • Customer account management

Creating a new customer profile

To add a new customer, start by creating their profile in the system.

  1. Navigate to the Customer section and select Add New Customer.
  2. Enter basic information such as name, contact details, and address.
  3. Save the profile to create the new customer account.

Entering customer details

Fill in detailed information to complete the customer’s profile.

  1. Add additional contact information, if available.
  2. Enter pertinent details like customer type, industry, or other relevant classifications.
  3. Attach any necessary documents or notes to the customer profile.

Setting up customer preferences

Customize the customer’s account based on their preferences and your business requirements.

  1. Set preferences for communication, billing, and service delivery.
  2. Adjust settings for discounts, special rates, or loyalty programs.
  3. Record any specific requirements or requests from the customer.

Customer account management

Manage the customer account effectively to ensure a positive ongoing relationship.

  1. Regularly update the customer’s information as needed.
  2. Monitor the customer’s transaction history and account activity.
  3. Provide support and assistance to address any queries or concerns.
