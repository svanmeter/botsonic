This article explains how to impersonate the customer so that you can view the customer's information.



Topics in this article include
 

  • Impersonating Customers via Role_Customer_Service
  • Via the Employee Menu
  • Impersonating Customers via Role_Super_Admin

 


 

Impersonating Customers via ROLE_CUSTOMER_SERVICE
 

Via Companies
 

1. On the Billing Site go to People > Companies.
 


 

2. Search for the customer.
 

 

3. Copy the URL Modifier to the company that you are wanting to impersonate.
 

 

4. In the upper-right hand corner of the screen click on Impersonation.
 


 

5. Now type “POR_CustomerURL Modifier” and click Impersonate.
 

 
 

6. Now refresh the browser screen (CTRL + R or Cmd + R) to open the dashboard for the customer.

 

Note: This is a live customer database and you should not make any changes unless you are given permission by the customer.




 

Via the Employee Menu
 

1. On the Billing Site go to People > Employees.
 


 

2. Search for the customer.
 


3. Click the Impersonate Button next to their name.
 


 

Note: This will take up a customer license and should only be used if the customer is having user issues. 


 

Impersonating Customers via the ROLE_SUPER_ADMIN Role
 

Via Customer File
 

1. On the Billing Site go to People > Customers.
 


 

2. Search for the customer that you are wanting to impersonate.
 


3. Click the Edit Button next to the customer.
 


 

4. Make sure that you are in Advanced mode.
 


 

5. Under the comments section of Administrative Information you will see the URL Modifier. Copy that code.
 


 

6. In the upper-right hand corner of the screen click on Impersonation.
 


 

7. Type “POR_CustomerURL Modifier” and click Impersonate.
 

 
 

8. Once it loads, click on the dashboard, and you will be impersonating the customer. 

 

Note: This is a live customer database and you should not make any changes unless you are given permission by the customer.
