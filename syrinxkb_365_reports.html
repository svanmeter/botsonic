365 Reports


There are many reports built into the system, that can be run from the right hand menu.
These can be searched and marked as favourites for ease of use.


 

Topics included in this article

 
  • Reports Menu    
  • Searching the Reports Menu
  • Setting Reports as Favourites
  • Last Viewed
 

 Reports Menu


The Reports menu can be accessed from the right hand menu and clicking the reports button

 

When you click this the report menu will open

 

 Searching the Reports Menu


When you open the Reports Menu it will take you to the Search tab.

This will display the reports that your user roles allow you to see.


You can click into the Search field and type part of a report name to return the relevant reports

You can also use the Filter By to just look at a section, for example I just wanted to see reports relating to Hire Contracts



Setting Reports as Favourites


You can flag any report as a favourite for your user by clicking the star at the end of the row, as per the example of the two flagged reports.


These will then display on your Favourites tab.

You can use the Search and Filter By options within this list

 

Last Viewed

The Last Viewed tab will display the reports that you have viewed during the current logged in session.
