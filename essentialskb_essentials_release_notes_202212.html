9/13/2022 - Version 2022.12


  • Transaction SubStatus addition - You have been reading us drone on about 'big stuff' that we have been working on and we have delivered. For each status (quote/Reservation...etc) you can add pulldowns for each one with your own defined selections. As an example, for Reservations, you can have substatuses of 'Ready to Pull' and ‘Pulled'. But of course many will create ones like 'Kinda Started Pulling', 'Almost done being pulled', and 'Really super close to pulled'. These substatuses also display on the workbench. Note: Setup for this is in Configurable Dropdowns.
 
  • Delivery Windows on Storefront - Speaking of delivered, We created this really awesome feature called 'Delivery Windows' for you to use on the counter a while back. We decided to take this a step further. You now have the option to determine which of your delivery windows to include for your customers to select on the storefront. We aren’t done enhancing this yet, so stay tuned.
 
  • Multiple Day Shutdown - In the past, if you wanted to shut your store down for periods of time for seasonal or long holidays, or if you just wanted to take a few days off to binge watch the latest season of Cobra Kai, you had to add closed days 1 day at a time. Now you can mark your store closed for a day or week at 1 time. Wax on, Wax off. 
 
  • Damage Waiver Acceptance -We have a field called Damage Waiver Refused Text that prints on the contract. We now have one for Acceptance of Damage Waiver. You can put something like "You have chosen to accept the damage waiver. You are covered for damage unless you use it for unintended use, or if you do something stupid." That will also print on the contract. Just an FYI, please don't use that example for real 
 
  • Rental Duration issue - We found out that if you selected the start and return dates on a transaction, then changed the return date again several times, while at the same time singing and dancing to "Rock Me Amadeus", that the rental duration was not updating. We 'fixed the glitch'. Rock on.
 
  • Multi Store Duplicated Custom Questions - There were certain off the wall scenarios that caused duplication of custom questions if you are multi store and used the configuration pusher to move from 1 store to another. We closed that loop.
 
  • Quickbooks Online Canada - We had a little fix we had to do in order to allow our Canadian customers to integrate with Quickbooks online. Then we also found out it affected our friends in Australia. We fixed it. Eh Mate?
