Excessive Resale Inventory Report

 

This report lists all your sale items that have a quantity on hand greater than the quantity sold in the past 12 months.  This inventory should be evaluated to determine if the items should be sold at a discount or returned to the vendor to free up retail space. The data comes from the Cost of Goods Sold history (not from the item record or transaction records). It includes all sales, returns, and inventory adjustments.
