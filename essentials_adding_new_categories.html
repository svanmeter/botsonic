Organizing items or data under categories

Assign items or data to relevant categories to improve navigation and accessibility.

  1. Review the items or data to be categorized.
  2. Assign each item or piece of data to the most appropriate category.
  3. Ensure that the categorization is logical and consistent across the system.

Maintaining and updating categories

Regularly review and update categories to ensure they remain relevant and effective.

  1. Periodically review the categories for relevance and accuracy.
  2. Update category names and descriptions as needed to reflect changes or new insights.
  3. Consider user feedback and system usage patterns to improve categorization.
