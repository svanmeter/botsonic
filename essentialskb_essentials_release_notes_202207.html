5/23/2022 - Version 2022.07


  • Max Rental Duration - You can now determine the maximum length of time a rental is made for as there is a configuration setting for this. Note: Works on both counter, and storefront.
 
  • Picklist Comment on Transaction - For each transaction, you can put a comment in that will print on the picklist.
 
  • Autopopulate Country/St/Zip for new customers - If most of your customers come from a specific area, you can have those fields automatically populate for each new customer you add.
 
  • Aging Days - No I don’t mean we are getting old. We know that already. The Closed Transactions with Outstanding Balances report now has a sortable column for Aging Days so you can easily see your most challenging non-paying customers. 
 
  • Hyperlink Emails on Storefront - The item hyperlinks are now part of the confirmation emails your customers receive when they order on the storefront. So you can put item based links to instructions or other links that might be helpful for your customers.
 
  • Technical Note:  For integrators with Rental Essentials we now provide OAuth2 client credentials grant/flow authentication as an alternative to our standard wsse token based authentication for backend/direct integration.  To make use of this alternative you will need to schedule a session with Point of Rental development to obtain authorization.
