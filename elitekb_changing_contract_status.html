Changing Contract Status

 

 

 

 

 

Overview 

 

This article explains how to change the status of a contract.

 

Topics in this article include:

 

  • Changing the contract status during the creation of a contract
  • Changing an open contract back to a reservation
  • Changing the contract status of a work order from the creation of the contract

 

 

Changing the contract status during the creation of a contract

 

When creating a new Express, Normal, Asset Sale, Quote, or Reservation contract, you can change the status of the contract from Quote to Reservation. You can also convert it to an Open status and back to a Reservation. You can make these changes as long as the contract has not been assigned a contract number.

 

Click on Change Status at the top of the screen within the contract to make the change.

 

 

When changing from Quote or Reservation status to an Open status, you will be asked if you want the time recomputed on the contract. Additionally, any item that has been added as the header record will require you to select a serialized item for the Open contract.

 

 

 

Note: You cannot change a Reservation or Open contract that has been issued a contract number back to a Quote. You can clone the Reservation or Open contract and change the status to Quote.

 

 

Changing an open contract back to a reservation

 

1. Go to Counter System > Day at a Glance > Open Items tab.

 

 

2. Right-click on the desired contract and choose Change to Reservation.

 

 

 
 

Changing the contract status of a work order from the creation of the contract

 

When creating a new Work Order, click on Change Status to change it to a Quote.
