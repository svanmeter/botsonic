Syrinx® - Loss & Damages 

 

 

 

 

Overview: This guide is designed to help you with loss and damages.



 

 

When a contract or items within the contract are being off-hired, Syrinx allows you to mark these items as either loss or damage.

 

 

By marking an item Lost, it will show on the off-hire note as not returned, by showing a zero in qty returned.

 

 

Continue with the off-hire and when you are ready to proceed with the Loss & Damage note confirm the off-hire. You will be presented with an information alert on the screen.

 

 

Loss/Damage unconfirmed will show all the unconfirmed notes that have been raised.

 

 

For anything lost, the Loss/Damage note will automatically input the replacement cost of that item as long as it has a cost set against that fleet item. For damaged items, you will need to input any parts or labour costs related to the repair.

 

 

 

 

When you print the note you have an option to select notification letter. This template can be amended to your own specifications within Syrinx Configuration > Documents > Loss/Damage.

 

  

Once the notification or advice note has been issued to the customer, you can confirm and it will be ready to batch within invoicing. You can also invoice immediately by changing the invoice period from Batch to Account now, Invoice will appear in your print options.
