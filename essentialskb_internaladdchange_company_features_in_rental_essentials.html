Internal Notes
 

1. From the Point of Rental billing site go to People > Companies.

 



2. Search for the customer that is requesting the change and click the  button.




3. From Current Live Company Features, click the down arrow, and search what you are looking for.



4. From the Current Company Packages, click the down arrow and search what you are looking for.



5. After you select it, click Add Package and then refresh the browser screen (CTRL + R or Cmd + R) to apply the update.

6. Verify the updates have applied in the Current Live Company Features and Current Company Packages.
