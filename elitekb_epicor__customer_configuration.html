Customer Configuration  

 

Account customers, Ace Rewards customers, and Total Rewards customers are linked between Point of Rental and Point of Sale using the Epicor SKU.  The Epicor SKU of the Epicor Account customer must be present in the Epicor Key field on the Account tab of the Point of Rental Customer File.  Non-taxable customers must be designated so in the Epicor Point of Sale and within the Point of Rental Customer File on the Pricing tab. Customers who require a Purchase Order within the Epicor Point of Sale must be designated as so in the Point of Rental Customer File on the Account tab.
