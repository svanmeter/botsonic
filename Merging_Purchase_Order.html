Merging Purchase Order

 

You now have the option to merge PO’s. Select the desired Purchase Order by clicking on the Purchase Order line, then select Merge.

A prompt will appear which asks you to confirm your choice:

Selecting No will cancel the Merge process. Selecting Yes will follow with this prompt:

After Selecting OK, the Purchase Order Selection will appear. This is where you select which PO to merge into:

Once you have selected the PO to merge into, a confirmation prompt will appear (see below) and you’re done!
