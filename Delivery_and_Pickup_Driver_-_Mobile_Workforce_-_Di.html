Delivery and Pickup Driver Mobile Workforce - Dispatch

In this video, you will learn how Mobile Workforce works within the Dispatch Center. 

* You would need to have the Dispatch module to utilize this feature. 

This will show routes assigned to a driver who is logged into their Mobile Workforce. Driver routes will be displayed allowing them to click on a route and display useful information as to their arrival and departure times, onsite times, Plan departures and arrivals as well as take pictures, captures signatures, clock in/out, and more.
