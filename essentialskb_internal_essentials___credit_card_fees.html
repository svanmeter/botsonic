INTERNAL Essentials | Credit Card Fees



In this article, we are going to show you how to configure credit card fees in Essentials. At this time, this feature is “Feature Wrapped”. This means it is currently in beta testing and only some customers have access to this feature.


Topics in this article include
    
  • Billing Site
  • Configurations



Billing Site

To enable this feature please work with your Tier 2 or Tier 3 professional. The feature is called ‘creditcardfees’.




Configurations

1. Now that credit card fees are enabled in the backend, it is time to set them up in the Essentials program. To do that, go to Configuration > Company Configurations.

 
 

2. Then click on Tax and Payment Options.
 

 

3. To set the percentage you want to charge customers when using their credit card, navigate to the Credit Card Fee % Section. You can also name and barcode the fee. If you want the fee to be tax-free, simply check the appropriate box.
 

 

4. When you select credit card from the Payment Method dropdown, you can bypass the fee by checking a box near that option. When you type in an amount in the Amount Being Collected field, the system will calculate the fee that is associated with the amount entered.
