Syrinx® - Partial Off-Hire

 

 

 

 

Overview: This guide is designed to help you with Partial Off-Hires.

 

 

 

 

A Partial Off-Hire is used when a customer no longer needs equipment on a contract, but some items within the contract may need to remain on hire for a longer duration.

 

You will see under the Hire tab in Syrinx, within the Off-Hire part of the ribbon, the option to select Partial Contract. Now search for the contract you wish to partially off-hire.

 

 

This will open up the Off-Hire note where you can now select which items you want to off-hire from the contract as well as select when you wish to off-hire these items.

 

By selecting the ‘Qty Returned’ box, it will show that item in the Off-Hire note as being returned. Ticking the ‘Collect Item’ box will show only that item in the collection note.

 

  

If at any time while the Off-Hire is unconfirmed the customer wishes to off-hire all items within the contract, you can change the Partial Off-Hire to a full Off-Hire by selecting Full at the bottom of the screen.

 

 

You can also select Partial from a full Off-Hire from within the unconfirmed Off-Hire screen, to change the full Off-Hire to a Partial Off-Hire.

 

 

Once the Off-Hired items have been returned, you can confirm the Off-Hire at the bottom of the screen, returning those assets to your system, leaving the other assets on hire to continue billing.

 

The contract will show the Off-Hire date alongside those items which you have confirmed as off-hired.
