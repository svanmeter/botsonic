Items Rented

 

This report will show the number of times an item was rented during a period that you select.  This is useful to see what items were hot the week before Christmas.  Or what items start renting in March so that you can get them serviced.  You will be asked whether to print the contract detail.  If you answer “Yes” then each contract will be shown.  If you answer “No”, then only the item, the times rented, and the total income will be displayed.
