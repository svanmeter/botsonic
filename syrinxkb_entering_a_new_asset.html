Syrinx® - Entering a New Asset
 



 

Overview: This guide is designed to help you with the basics of creating a new individual hire asset.

 


 

From the ribbon at the top, you need to go to into Fleet and click on Fleet Item. You will then be presented with the Enter Hire Stock screen.

 

 

 

 

 

Once you have decided the type of asset you are entering you need to click OK. 
 

 

 

The more information you can enter on this screen the better. Certain fields may be mandatory depending on your system configuration.

 

 

Note: The Date Avail affects the utilisation and the Purch Date affects the depreciation.

 

Once you have entered your information, you can click OK. You will be asked if you want to amend the details. If you need to add further information, then click Yes. If you select No, then the asset is now ready to hire.
