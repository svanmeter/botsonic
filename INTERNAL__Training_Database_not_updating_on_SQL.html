Internal | Training Database not updating on SQL



 

This article will go over some basic things to look at when the Training Database, POR2, is not updating in SQL. Unlike JET Databases, SQL does not use the Task Runner in Point of Rental to update the training database. It is done by the Task Scheduler in Windows. This means, even if the Training Database appears to not be updated when you switch to training from the software, Windows is still making a current Training Database, it's just that Point of Rental is not pointing to it. This typically happens after conversion from JET. The topics below will show how to identify and potentially fix the issue of the SQL Training DB not updating.

 

 



Topics in this article include
 

  • Checking the INI File
  • JET to SQL Training Database Migration Training Database Error
  • Other troubleshooting scenarios

Other troubleshooting scenarios

The customer is receiving a warning that they haven't closed for End of Day or Month?

The customer can either run End of Day for the day or month for each store (if multistore) or wait to access the Training Database until the next day when the training database refreshes.

 

The training database is asking for the POR Admin code when trying to enter the training mode.

This may happen after the customer runs an update.  The customer must call Product support for a Tier 1 to enter the POR admin password.
