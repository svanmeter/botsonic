  • Damage Waiver optional on Storefront - We have added 3 features for this.
    • You can now allow your customers to decline the damage waiver on the storefront. There is a configuration under Front End configuration to allow this.
    • There is custom text you can fill out for your customers to see what they are declining.
    • There is custom text for a confirmation screen if they do decline it.
 
  • Custom Questions enhancement - More than 10 custom questions? You want it, you got it. You can now set the number of custom questions all the way up to 50. Let us know how long it takes to write each contract with all 50 questions to answer.
 
  • Remove Item Rates from Storefront - On price sheets, you now have the ability to remove certain rates from the storefront. If you have 2hr/4hr/24hr…etc and you don’t want to allow storefront users to book for 2 hours, you can ‘Blacklist’ that rate so it doesn’t show. Admit it. You like that term ‘Blacklist’ as it makes you feel top secret like Jason Bourne when you use it.
 
  • Esign Enhancement - If you didn’t sign correctly, the error telling you that didn’t go away even after you did sign correctly. We ‘fixed the glitch’.
 
  • Multiple Photos - You can now add multiple pictures at the same time from a mobile device while doing condition photos instead of having to take and load them individually.

Auto Sign in for uInnovate - When you click on the light bulb in the upper right to go to uInnovate, it will now automatically log you in using your email address in Rental Essentials. Secure, and very cool.
