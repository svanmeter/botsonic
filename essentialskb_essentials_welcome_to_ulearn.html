What's new?

uLearn offers new features to help you and your team succeed!
 
  • Need to contact us? Start a live chat with a representative! 
  • The help library now includes videos! You can identify videos in the search results by the "Video" tag. 
  • We've fine-tuned the search engine. Search results will display the most relevant articles based on the topic and key words.
  • The search bar will now recommend relevant articles as you type.
  • Find and read trending articles! You can find the Trending Articles section throughout uLearn. 
  • Make software suggestions straight from uLearn!
 


Getting to uLearn

Website URL
  • You can copy and paste the following URL to access uLearn from any Chromium or Chrome based browser: https://por.force.com/essentialskb/s/


From Essentials
  • Login to Essentials > Select the "Need Help?" option (banner at the bottom) 
 


Navigating uLearn 






 1 - Navigation Bar


Use the top menu bar to explore article topics and videos, submit a uInnovate idea, and view release notes. 


2 - Search Bar



Use the search bar to find help articles, videos, and discussion topics! As you type, uLearn will display suggested help content.




3 - Topics



Use these options to browse through help articles or videos that relate to a topic. You can choose from Counter Features, Administration, Inventory, and Help Videos. When you’re viewing an article, above the title, you can click on the topic to find other related help articles.  





4 - Trending Articles



On the right hand side throughout uLearn, you’ll see trending articles. The articles with the most views will be listed.


5 - Contact Support & Submit Your Idea



Contact Support

When you need to contact us, you can click on Contact Support. You’ll be taken to a new screen where you can submit your inquiry directly to our team.
 

Submit Your Idea

Tell us your program enhancement ideas by clicking on Submit Your Ideas. This will take you to our online feedback management system, uInnovate, where you can vote on already-existing ideas or continue to create your own. You can also easily check out all the current suggestions and vote for the features that you’d like to see in the software. You can also be notified when your suggestions are voted for and see where each suggestion is in the process of being adopted.

 

6 - Chat



When you need assistance, contact our support team. Or, click the Live Chat feature found at the bottom right of the screen to instantly chat with a support team member.
