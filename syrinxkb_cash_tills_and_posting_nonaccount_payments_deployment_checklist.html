Introduction

The following supplement to the Cash Tills and Posting Non-Account Payments guide is a deployment checklist that can be used to prepare your system for implementation of non-account payment posting from Syrinx to the accounts package.

Deployment Check-List

Preparation

P-1

Read the Cash Tills and Posting Non-Account Payments guide to understand how the feature works and discuss with both the hire desk manager (person responsible for cashing up each night) plus the accounts staff.  Ensure that your staff are comfortable with how the feature works, and what postings will be applied for different scenarios.  If they are unsure of how well the working of the feature matches with how they want to operate, then they may be advised to not use the feature and continue with their existing process.

 

P-2

Ensure that the Transfer to Accounts tool is installed, and that the ‘Use Posting Files’ option is turned off in Syrinx SP Configuration.

 

P-3

Determine the nominal codes to be used to post till discrepancies

N/C for money lost: __________      N/C for money gained: __________

 

P-4

Contact Point of Rental support to obtain the patch script that will be used to enable the feature on ‘feature-live’ day.

 

P-5

If required, enter the new cash account customer record in Sage – ie if there is currently a separate customer record for different payment types.

Non-Account Customers Account No: ___________

 

P-6

Create the bank accounts in Sage for the Till cash payments, plus the Card transactions (if not already present).

Bank Accounts Cash: ____________ Card: _____________

 

P-7

Decide on the start date for the payments transfer.  You will need to allocate time before trading at the start of the day to count up the cash till for the opening balance, and complete the (albeit minor) Syrinx configuration changes.

 

Feature Live

L-1

Get all users logged out of the system – inform them to stay off until further notice.

 

L-2

Run the patch script to turn the feature on.

 

L-3

Go into Syrinx Configuration, and enter the till name on the General Setup / Cash Tills option.

 

L-4

Go to Syrinx Configuration/General/Depots screen.  Make sure the cash account number is identical for all payment types with the correct account number.  Enter the Bank Account numbers for Cash, Card payments.

 

L-5

Log into Syrinx on the counter PC, pick the till name, and then enter the opening balance of the till.

 

L-6

In Sage, enter the opening balance against the Till bank account.

 

L-7

If unpaid invoices have been previously posted to the accounting system, and if you have set up a new customer record for non-account transactions, then in the accounting system you need to move any non-account invoices that are genuinely unpaid from the old cash account to the new cash account.  This is so when the final payment is received that it can be matched up with the correct invoice; this will not be possible if the invoice was posted to one customer record, and if the final payment is credited to a different customer record.
