Do you have customers that just want to purchase something like a soda, candy bar or souvenir?  Maybe they’re in a hurry and just want to pay for it and get on their way so they can enjoy more rides on their bike rental or get back to work so they can go home sooner. This article will show you how to get the customer quickly in and out the door for when they only purchase items with no rentals.

Topics included in this article:

  • Start a Quick Sale
  • Provide Customer with a Receipt 

 

 

Start a Quick Sale
 

Click Quick Sale from the top counter menu. Add the items to the sale.
 

 
If you want to attach the Quick Sale to the customer account, click on the Edit icon next to cash customer:
 

 

If you want to add any fees or discounts you can add them on the top right of the contract screen.
 

 

If the customer needs to add a PO#, you can add it above the contract number.

 


Click Collect Payment to the right and enter the amount the customer gives you.  If they're paying by cash and they hand you more than what the total is, you will see the change due to give back to the customer for the difference.
 

       




Click Save and you’re done.  Now the Quick Sale is complete, and the customer is out the door.



Provide Customer with a Receipt
 
Don’t forget to print the customer their receipt. You can even send the receipt to the customer via email, or SMS if you have that add on feature. To do this, click the Email Receipt option on the payment screen.  Be sure to enter a valid email for the customer.
