eCommerce | Hours Are Incorrect on Website

INTERNAL

 

The hours on most websites are pulled from POR's Calendar Maintenance.  The customer should verify that the hours there are correct for all stores.  If they are, then a support ticket needs to be created for eCommerce.

If Special/Holiday hours are showing for the last day of the month, it is because the hours for the EOM/EOY entries in POR Calendar Maintenance are not the same as the regular business hours entered in Calendar Maintenance.  The customer should update the EOM/EOY entries to have the same hours as the regular days, and those days/hours will then not show on the website.
