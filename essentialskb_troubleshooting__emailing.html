Troubleshooting - Emailing

Customers receive an error message when trying to email.
 
   
  1. Email Error
  1. A customer can not send out emails through Point of Rental.
  1. Need to determine if the issue is with POR or their email provider.
  2. Go to Step 2.
2. POR Setting Verification
  1. Go to Configurations>Reports and Notification>Email Setup.
  1. Settings in each of these fields in POR,  MUST match the settings within the customer's email provider.
  2. Go to Step 3.
3. Run a Test Email
  1. Select Send Test Email button and enter in your email address.
  1. If the email was sent out with NO errors. Then the issue is with the customer's email provider.
  2. Have them verify the information with their provider.
  3.  Go to Step 4.
4. Email Provider Setting Verification
  1. Have the customer log into their email provider settings and verify the information is correct.
  1. If they do not have this information.
  1. Was any of the following listed below changed or edited with their email provider.
  2. Inform them to contact their email provider and get the following info:
  • Email Address
  • Email PSWD
  • SMTP Server
  • SMTP port No.
  1. Go to Step 4.
5. Error within POR
  1. If you sent out the email using the Send Test Email and received an error message.
  1. Then the issue is within Point of Rental.
 
          Escalate to Tier 1
