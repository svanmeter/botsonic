Deposit to Rental/Sale Amount

Â 

From Program Menu>>Configurations>>System Configurations>>Parameters>>Drawer Tab. Check the box Collect Deposit as Rental/Sale.

From within the Item record, you will need to enter a deposit for the item being entered on the contract. * Note: The deposit amount will only show in the rental/sale field from the deposit set in the item record.

Once the item is added to the contract, when you go to the payment screen, you will see the deposit added to the rental/sale field.
