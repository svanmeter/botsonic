Continuation Billing with a Sub-Rented Item


 

Overview

This article will show you how to continuation bill a sub-rental (re-rent) contract in the Expert and Elite software. 



Topics included in this article

  • Expert
  • Elite




Expert

Image of the Program Menu in Expert
 

In Expert, there are two steps in continuing a subrental contract. First, you must continue the original contract, then continue the subrental contract. Follow the steps below. 


STEP 1: Continuing the original contract
 

1. Close the original rental contract . This can be done from the Counter System options Close Contract and Inquire Contract (contract can also be inquired from Day at a Glance). 
 

2. Select Continuation Bill.
 


 

3. A calendar will appear displaying the next 28 day billing cycle.
 

4. Select a date.
 

5. Finish going through the contract by pressing OK.
 

6. The new contract will appear with the next continuation letter displaying at the end of the contract number. 
 


7. When you're ready, press OK and go through the Print Contract screen.
 

STEP 2: Continuing the Subrental Contract
 

Important: Subrental contracts can only be continued through manual continuation.
 

1. Close the subrental contract. This can be done through Close Contract or Day at a Glance through the Counter System. 
 

Note: Subrental contracts must be in an open status. If the contract is in the reservation status then it must be sent out first. 
 

2. Select Continuation Bill.
 


3. A calendar will appear displaying the next 28 day billing cycle.
 

4. Select a date.
 


 

5. Finish going through the contract by pressing OK.
 

6. The new subrental contract will appear with the next continuation letter displaying at the end of the contract number. 
 


7. When you're ready, press OK and go through the Print Contract screen.



Elite
 

Image of the Elite Home Screen



There are two ways to continue a subrental contract within Elite. Subrental contracts can be continued at the same time the original contract is continued or they can be done manually.
 

Manually
 

1. To continue the contract manually, follow the steps in the above section "Expert".


Automatically

With the new release in 2022.14, Subrentals can auto continue with the original rental contract.
 
  1. Continuation Billing: In the browser configurations, you can turn on an option called “Continue Subrental Contracts”. With this turned on, when a contract with a connected subrental contract is continuation billed, you will receive an additional prompt to continuation bill the subrental contract. A security option called “Continue Subrental Contracts” controls who can do this.
    1. To turn this setting on, select the Gear icon in the top left corner. This will take you to the browser configurations page. 
    2. Scroll down to the section labeled Transaction.
    3. Click on Continue Subrental Contracts to open its submenu. 
    4. Check the box "Subrental contracts linked to rental contracts will be continued..."
