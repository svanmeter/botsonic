Speed up and simplify credit card payment processing and credit card total reconciliation with Point of Rental’s Integrated Credit Card Module.
 

Integrated credit card processing with Point of Rental removes the hassle of processing credit card transactions twice, once on a 3rd party credit card terminal and another in Rental Expert. If you're tired of manually reconciling your credit card totals, then this module is for you.
 

 

Security and Fraud Prevention
 

Keep cardholders' information confidential by never storing credit card numbers.
 

Data breaches can happen anytime, anywhere. So, take the first preventive step with hacker-fighting technology. As a customer-facing, EMV enabled solution, the integrated credit card module utilizes tokenization and encryption to ensure customers' card data is safe, reducing the chance of security risks and fraud.
 

 

Integration and Security Features

 

  • Automatic reconciliation of credit card totals during Close for Day
 
  • Pre-authorization and completion support
 
  • Multi-Channel, Multi-Location Tokens - Converts sensitive information to a token. Merchants never handle customer data throughout online, in-store, and mobile transactions. Tokens are usable for up to six months. Don’t wish to use historical credit cards? No problem. This feature can be disabled store-wide or for a single customer.
 
  • Chip card acceptance (EMV) to reduce fraud liability
 
  • Can reduce a merchant’s PCI scope
 
  • Debit card acceptance
