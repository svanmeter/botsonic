In Rental Essentials, we now allow you to manage your credit cards directly without calling support. This article will show you how it can be accomplished.

 

 
Topics in this article include:

  • How to Get there
  • Integration Process
  • For Stripe
  • For Cayan



 

How to Get there


To get there, you will go to Accounting > Credit Card Processing.

 

 
 

Integration Process


With credit card processing, you can integrate Cayan and Stripe with your Point of Rental.
 

1. To begin accepting credit cards, click on the Connect with Point of Rental button.

 

 

2. Next expand the Organization info section and fill out the information. Press Save when completed.
 

 

3. Then under Payment Processors, click on Add Payment Processor.

 



4. Choose the Processor that you are setting up with, then click Select.
 


 

For Stripe


If you are integrating with Stripe, you will need to click on the Finish Stripe Registration and sign in with your account.

 


 

For Cayan


If you are integrating with Cayan (TSYS), you will need to make sure that you enter all the information that is required. This information is normally from Cayan. You can contact them at 800-941-6557.
