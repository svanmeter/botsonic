If a sales stock item does not have a "Latest Sales Price" defined against its sales stock item record, any discounts linked to that item via a customer's pricing agreement will not be applied when that item is added to a contract, advice note or service job. If you wanted to add customer specific discounts to these items, you must add a "Latest Sales Price" to the item via the "Stock Item" tab of the "Amend Sales Stock Item" screen which is accessed via Syrinx SP\Stock\Amend Items.
