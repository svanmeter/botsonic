Arbitrary e-Signature Documents

This article provides information on creating and managing arbitrary electronic signature (e-signature) documents within the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Understanding arbitrary e-signature documents
  • Creating e-signature documents
  • Managing and storing signed documents
  • Legal considerations and compliance

Understanding arbitrary e-signature documents

Arbitrary e-signature documents are digital documents that can be signed electronically, offering convenience and efficiency.

Creating e-signature documents

Create digital documents in the system that can be signed using e-signatures.

  1. Access the document creation module in the system.
  2. Design the document layout and add fields that require e-signatures.
  3. Set the parameters for signing, such as signatory details and order.

Managing and storing signed documents

Effectively manage and securely store documents that have been signed electronically.

  1. Store signed documents in a secure, organized manner within the system.
  2. Track the status of documents, including those pending signature.
  3. Ensure easy retrieval of signed documents for reference or legal purposes.

Legal considerations and compliance

Ensure that your e-signature process and documents are compliant with relevant legal standards and regulations.

  1. Understand the legal requirements for e-signatures in your jurisdiction.
  2. Ensure that the e-signature process adheres to these legal standards.
  3. Maintain proper records and audit trails for signed documents.
