Overview

This article will walk you through how to access uLearn from Essentials. It will also go through making a chromium browser your default browser. We recommend using Chromium based browsers to access uLearn.




Topics included in this article
 

  • Logging in through a Web Browser (Premiere Support members only)
  • How to Sign Up (Premiere Support members only)
  • Installing a Chromium Based Browser
  • Setting the Default Browser on Your Computer or Server


 

Logging in through a Web Browser (Premiere Support members only)

  1. You can log in through a web based application by using the following link https://por.force.com/essentialskb/s/ . 
  • The URL must be exact or you will be directed to a different site. 
    • Mac users will be able to use Safari 15+ (this has not been fully tested).
 
  1. Log in with your credentials. Need Help? > Log In
 
  1.  If you don't have credentials, create your account by pressing Sign Up. To learn how to sign up go to next section "How to Sign Up (Premiere Support members only)."
 


 

How to Sign Up (Premiere Support members only)

 

  1. Click the Sign Up link, in the bottom right corner of the login screen. 
 
  1. Fill in your information and press Submit. Please use your company email address and phone number.  
Please note, if you have an existing uLearn account, you will still need to sign up and reregister to link your old account to the new uLearn.
  1. Once you have submitted your information, uLearn will send an email to the email provided. 
    • If you already have an existing account, the email will provide you a link to reset your password.
    • If you DO NOT have an existing account, the email will confirm your registration or notify you that we have created an internal case to confirm your information. Meanwhile, you'll still be able to access help articles and videos. To view cases your account must be verified.
 
  1. Using your email you provided as the username, return to uLearn and log in.
