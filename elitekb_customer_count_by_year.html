Customer Count by Year




Overview
 

The Customer Count by Year report prints a count of all new customers subtotaled by the month and year that they were added. 


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Customer Analysis > Customer Count by Year
Elite: Right Menu > Reports icon > Search bar > Customer Count by Year 

 

1. You will be asked which customers to report.

 

2. Select either Account, Cash, or Account and Cash.
