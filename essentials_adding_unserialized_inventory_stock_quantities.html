Adding Unserialized Inventory Stock/Quantities

This article covers the process of adding unserialized inventory stock and managing quantities within the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Understanding unserialized inventory
  • Adding unserialized items to inventory
  • Managing stock levels for unserialized items

Understanding unserialized inventory

Unserialized inventory consists of items that do not require individual tracking via serial numbers, typically used for bulk or general items.

Adding unserialized items to inventory

Easily add unserialized items to your inventory for efficient tracking and management.

  1. Go to the Inventory section in the system.
  2. Choose to add a new item and select ‘Unserialized’ as the item type.
  3. Input the item details, including name, description, and total quantity.

Managing stock levels for unserialized items

Keep track of stock levels for unserialized items to maintain adequate inventory.

  1. Regularly update the quantity as items are added to or used from inventory.
  2. Monitor usage patterns to predict and plan for future inventory needs.
  3. Conduct periodic audits to ensure physical stock matches system records.
