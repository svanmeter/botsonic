If you would like to print a list of your hire rates, you should click on the "Categories" button in the "Setup" area of the Syrinx SP\Fleet tab. This will display a list of the hire categories that have been entered on your system. Select any of the categories in the list and press the "Show Detail" button at the bottom of the screen to open the "Amend Hire Category" screen. If you then press the "Print Hire Rates" button at the bottom of this screen and then select the "All Categories" option on the subsequent "Print Hire Rates" prompt, the system will print a list of all of your hire rates.
