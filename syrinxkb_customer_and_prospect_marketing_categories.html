Overview

Marketing Categories let you apply various classifications to your customers and prospects, to help you organise them according to your own requirements so that you can then do searches for targeted marketing campaigns (or for general enquiries).  For example you may want to categorise your customers and prospects by industry, or by the type of equipment they are interested in hiring or buying.  You can configure up to 6 different sets of categories, and a customer or prospect can be assigned to more than one category in each set.

Configuration.

To set marketing information against customers / prospects, you must first ensure the ‘Enter Marketing Info?’ option has been set, using the ‘Marketing’ tab on Syrinx Configuration\System Options\Customers:

Next, the ‘General’ menu in Syrinx Configuration has a ‘Marketing Categories’ menu option that is used to configure the marketing categories:

This will initially contain a single set/group called ‘Category’.  The Rename button can be used to rename this initial category group (in this example it could be renamed to ‘Type of Business’) and the Add button at the top can be used to enter additional groups:

The ‘Applies only to Company Type’ drop-down lets you indicate that a particular category should only be offered in the list if the customer is of the given type:

Note: The set of company types is defined via the Reference Lists screen.

The Add button can be used to assign one or more categories to the selected company.

The system will search for customers that have been assigned to any of the selected categories for each of the given lists.  In the example above, customers assigned to the ‘Plant Hire’ category that have also be assigned to either the ‘Dumpers’ or ‘Excavators’ category will be returned.
