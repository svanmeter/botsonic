Note: Support Team Lead or Management will need to approve this request that has been made by the customer. Support Team Lead or Management must place the approval in the Salesforce case before proceeding with changing the company URL prefix.

 


 

Internal Notes
 

1. From the Point of Rental Billing Site go to People > Companies.
 

 

2. Search for the customer that is requesting the change and click the  button.


 
3. Change the company URL prefix to what the number request it to be with the following requirements:
 
  • CANNOT be any spaces
  • If there is more than one word, there can be a dash to combine the word
 



4. Click the Save button and then refresh the browser screen (CTRL + R or Cmd + R) to apply the updated URL modifier.
