 Refund of Completed Contracts

 

 

This selection produces a report of all completed contracts that money was subsequently refunded on.  This is not an uncommon function; however, if one employee has a significant number of them especially cash refunds it could signal a problem.  First you must enter the beginning date and the ending date of the report.
