You cannot raise quotes for prospect customers through the standard "Enter Hire Contract" and "Enter Advice Note" menus. To do this, you will need to locate the customer in the "Prospective Customers" screen which is accessed via the Syrinx SP\CRM\Prospects menu option. Once you have identified and opened the prospect customer record, click on the "Prospect" tab and use the "Add" button to create a new hire or sales quotation. Quotations for Prospects and standard customers can be viewed through the Syrinx SP\CRM\Open Quotes menu.
