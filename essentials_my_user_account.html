My User Account

This article provides information on how to manage and customize your user account in the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Accessing and updating account information
  • Customizing account settings
  • Security and privacy settings
  • Account support and help

Accessing and updating account information

Easily access and update your personal and professional information in your user account.

  1. Login to your account and navigate to the account settings.
  2. Update personal information such as name, contact details, and address.
  3. Modify professional details like job title and department.

Customizing account settings

Customize your account settings to enhance your experience and workflow.

  1. Adjust settings like notifications, language preferences, and display options.
  2. Set up shortcuts and quick access links for frequently used features.
  3. Customize the dashboard to display relevant information and data.

Security and privacy settings

Ensure your account is secure and your privacy is protected.

  1. Manage password settings and enable two-factor authentication for added security.
  2. Review and adjust privacy settings to control what information is visible to others.
  3. Stay informed about security updates and privacy policies.

Account support and help

Access support resources for any account-related queries or issues.

  1. Use the help center for FAQs and troubleshooting guides.
  2. Contact customer support for personalized assistance.
  3. Stay updated with tutorials and guides for new features and updates.
