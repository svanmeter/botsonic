Damage Waiver Analysis




Overview
 

This article will discuss the Damage Waiver Analysis report. This report shows income from revenue and damage waiver with the total number of contract excepting damage waiver at a certain time.




Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Income Analysis > Damage Waiver Analysis
Elite: Right Menu > Reports Icon > Damage Waiver Analysis
 

1. Select your report beginning and ending dates.

2. Select your report data grouping.

3. Select which store, or all stores, to report on.

4. Define your printer.

5.  Select Generate.
