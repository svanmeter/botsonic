Average Days to Pay Report




 

This report prints the average days to pay for contracts opened each month of the year over a four-year period. 


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Accounting Reports > Accounting Reports Menu > Average Days to Pay Report
Elite: Right Menu > Reports icon > Average Days to Pay Report
