INTERNAL Troubleshooting | Account Payment Correction


Customers who are trying to correct an account payment.
 
   
  1. Verify the Information
  1. Need to determine what they actually did and what they were attempting to do.
  1. Run the Payment Report from the Customer Dashboard.
  2. Go to Step 2.
2. Payment History Report
  1. This report will tell you how the payment was made, date, who made it and what contracts the payments were applied to. 
  1. Verify how the payment was made, Cash, Check, Credit Card, Credit Memo.
  2. Go to Step 3.
3. Golden Rule
  1. You have to first undo what the customer  did…..before you can help them with the actual issue.
  1. Work your way backwards from what they did using the same payment method. IF THEY TOOK A PAYMENT  BY CASH UNDO IT BY CASH.
  2. Go to Step 6.
4.. Credit vs Debit
  1. A CREDIT is adding money to an account.
  2. A DEBIT is removing money from an account.
  1. IT IS IMPORTANT TO UNDERSTAND THE DIFFERENCE.
  2. Customers will often refer to payments as either a Credit or a Debit.
5. Put into Drawer
  1. Understand what this does.
  2. If payment is being corrected and this box was checked.
  1. Checking this box will add the payment to the Cash Drawer an account for this money as part of the customers End of Day.
  2. Then when making the correction select this option.
6. Same Day Payments
  1. If the payment was taken within the same day.
  2. Un-applying and Reapplying the payment is a cleaner way to see what has happened, when reviewing later.
  1. Undo the payment using the same payment method. See Rule 3.
  2. If payment was from another day then Go to Step 8.
7. Correct Previous Payment.
  1. Payment was applied using the wrong payment method.
  2. This is the ONLY TIME you would use Correct Previous Payment.
  3. DO NOT USE CORRECT PREVIOUS PAYMENT if it's NOT on the same day the payment was applied. This will create CREDITS.
  1. Run the payment history Report from the Customer Dashboard.
  2. From A/R make a payment and select Correct Previous payment. This will unapply the payments and enter the amount in the Funds Remaining.
  3. Reapply the payments to the correct invoices.
8. Payments being corrected from Another Day.
  1. DO NOT USE CORRECT PREVIOUS PAYMENT.
  2. If the dollar amount is CORRECT but just applied to the wrong account.
  1. Unapply a payment by Credit Memo and Reapply the payment to the correct account by Credit Memo.
9. Payments applied to the Correct Customer/ Wrong Invoices
  1. If the payment amount and method are correct and applied to the incorrect customer?
  1. Unapply the payment by Credit Memo and go to the correct customer Reapply payment by Credit Memo.
10. Credit Memos
  1. Credit Memos should only be applied if the correction is NOT within the same day.
  2. Credit Memo DO NOT affect the Cash Drawer.
  3. Credit Memo is also another way of adding fictitious funds to a person's account.
  1. Mostly used to move payments from one account to another after the initial payment has already been deposited.
  2. This is NOT REAL MONEY.
  3. DOES NOT Affect the cash drawer.
11. Escalation If you could not resolve the issue using any of the scenarios listed above. Escalate to a Tier One
