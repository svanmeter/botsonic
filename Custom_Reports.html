Custom Reports



 

Overview 

In Custom Reports, you'll be able to access any Crystal Reports that you or the Point of Rental team has created. 


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Custom Reports
Elite: Right Menu > Custom

 

This is the view of Custom Reports:

 

 

1. Select the report you want to see. 

 

2. Then select View. If there are any parameters that need to be selected you will see a screen labeled “Enter Parameter Values.”

 

3. From the list of parameters, select the parameter and then choose the value you want to use. The report will display on your screen, in the same way all Point of Rental reports display.

 

This is a great way to give your employees access to custom Crystal Reports without having to worry about them damaging the report in the Crystal program. Simply save any of your own custom reports to c:\por\reportscustom and they will display in the Custom Reports section.

 

You may also use Report Generator at the bottom to create your own text-only and print-only reports through the program. 
 

Note: Report Generator is NOT Crystal Reports and has its own method for saving user-generated reports.
