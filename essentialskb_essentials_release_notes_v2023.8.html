Release Notes for v2023.8 (6/5/2023)

 





 

  • Event First Storefront - We now have a front-end configuration where you can set your storefront to force your customers to input a date first before choosing any items.
 
  •   Contract Enhancements
  • Hours/Miles shown on contract - Previously when a customer rented an hour meter item, then brought it back, it showed the total hours used. However, it did not show the 'out' and 'in' readings. Now your customers will see the hours out/hours in on their printed copy.
  • Line Item Dates on Contract - We have added out and in dates per item on the contract printout
 
  • Storefront Rate Info enhancement - We have changed the view on the storefront when your customers select 'Rate Info'. They now get a horizontal larger much easier-to-read rate structure. 
 
  • Photo Display on Storefront - We have normalized the photo sizes on the storefront, to increase performance in loading.
