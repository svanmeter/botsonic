Guide on setting and maintaining Staff Development
  
 

Overview

With operated hires, it is common for the operators to be trained and to require certain qualifications before they can be hired out to operate the different machines.  There may also be similar requirements for service engineers who require specific qualifications to be able to service certain types of equipment.  With Syrinx, you can record the qualifications held by each machine operator or engineer, plus details of when these qualifications need to be renewed. You can also identify what training and exams need to be booked, and keep a full history for each staff member.  The qualifications required to operate/service the different types of plant can also be recorded, and the system will automatically alert you when an operator assigned to a particular contract does not have the requisite qualifications or block a user from assigning an engineer to a job for which they are not qualified.  Although primarily added for operated hire and fleet servicing, this feature can also be used to track the development of any staff member.

The next step is to categorise the types of staff development undertaken.  In the main Syrinx program, go to the Staff Development menu, and chose “Staff Development Categories”:

Against each category you must indicate whether it applies to machine operation.


Staff Development Items

The final part of the configuration is to define the different qualifications and other types of staff development to be tracked.  Chose the menu option Staff Development / Staff Development Items, and use the “Add New” button to add the items.  The first tab of the detail screen is used to note how much notice is required to book the associated training/exam, and if/when the qualification needs to be renewed:

For qualifications that apply to machine operation, the second tab is used to note which types of equipment require this qualification:

The “Staff Development Items” screen also has a “Reports” button that can be used to run various reports that can either list all of the development items or display which staff members possess or require a particular qualification:


Staff Development Screen

The Staff Development screen is used to book and record the qualifications obtained by each staff member:

The “Add New” button is used to add a new qualification, and to add dates/notes on the requirement and/or booking:

 

The “Mark Complete” button lets you enter the date when the qualification was obtained, plus any relevant notes.  Once complete, the Completion History tab on the detail screen shows the completion details, and the Remove Completion Details button can be used to undo the ‘Mark Complete’ operation.

Documents

The ‘Documents’ button on the Staff Development screen creates and opens a folder for that staff member, to allow storage of electronic copies of certificates, etc.  The root folder for all staff members is defined using the “Document Root Folders” screen in Syrinx Configuration:
