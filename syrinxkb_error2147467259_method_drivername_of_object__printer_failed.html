If there is not a default printer set on your PC, you may receive the below error message when attempting to print documents from Syrinx: 
ERROR--2147467259: Method 'DriverName' of object '_Printer' failed. 

You simply need to navigate to the printers menu on your PC and right click on one of the printers and select the "Set as default printer" option. Then return to Syrinx and retry printing your document.
