Set up on Customer Rental Essentials
 

1. Log in to billing profile of Essentials.

 

2. Impersonate customer.

 

3. Go to Accounting > Credit Card Processing.

 
 

 

4. Under Update > Merchant Credentials, click the merchant that the customer is signed up with.

 

Note: If they are using Stripe and they have not connected to a credit card processor, then the customer can click on Connect with Stripe in the Credit Card Processing.
 

 

5. Plug the credentials (if applicable) in the correct area on the billing site.

 

6. Click Save on the page.
