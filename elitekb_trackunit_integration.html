Trackunit Integration




Overview

Trackunit Integration allows you to maximize the utilization of your equipment by efficiently displaying the logistics, location, and status information for a fleet. This integration requires the GPS unit number and the Fleet ID to be defined on the item record in Point of Rental.  This integration will only work with Expert/Elite SQL databases.

 

Point of Rental offers integration with Trackunit Connect. This is a specific portal beyond the standard AEMP integration that you must subscribe to. This allows you to maximize the utilization of your equipment by efficiently displaying the logistics, location, and status information for a fleet. Trackunit will pass location and working hours into Point of Rental. Point of Rental will pass back pickup information, rental status, and certain contract information into the Trackunit portal.

 

 

 

Please contact Trackunit or the Point of Rental Sales Team for more information.
