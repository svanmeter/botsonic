Dashboard Module




Overview
 

Point of Rental’s Business Intelligence Dashboard empowers owners and managers with pertinent information to keep your business goals on target. Get at a glance reporting defined by the reports you want to view most often, including ROI, utilization on equipment, or account customer percentages. Highly customizable graphic displays mine historical and real-time data. There are multiple pre-defined dashboards tailored for specific operational and financial goals or you can define your own.
