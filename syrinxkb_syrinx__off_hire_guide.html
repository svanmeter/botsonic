This article is a guide designed to help you with the basics of off-hiring a contract.
 

1. From the ribbon at the top of the program, you will need to go into Off Hire.

 

2. Click on Contract, you will then be presented with the Search Box.



You can search on any of the fields below. If you know the contract number, you can enter this and click search.
Alternatively, as in the example below, you can filter by customer and item name.

3. Once you have finished, click Search or press enter.



Based on your search, if the customer has more than one machine on hire, then you will be presented with a Find Contract screen.

4. Select the contract you want by clicking on the line.

5. Then click OK.



Once you have clicked ok you will be presented with the Off Hire Contract screen as shown below:



You can click on the Collection tab and enter any information regarding collecting the equipment:



Once you have entered your info, you can click Print at the bottom and produce your collection note/off hire note:



From here you can print the document by clicking Quick Print (this will use your default printer), or, you can email the document straight to your customer.




If you are using the Syrinx driver app then there is no need to print the paperwork, simply tick ‘requires collection’, and the job will be available in the driver scheduler.

If the customer has returned the machine and you are happy with the condition etc., then you can confirm the contract as off-hired. 

If you are collecting the machinery, then you can click close at the bottom.

Once the driver has returned you will need to go back into the hire tab and then use Unconfirmed off hire:



You will have your unconfirmed hires listed as shown below. All you need to do is select the relevant one and either double-click on the date or single-click anywhere on the line, and then click OK.



The final step with the off hire is to enter any sales/return quantities and to confirm the off hire. See the screenshot below.



The rest of the buttons are described below.


 

Off Hired at

This shows the depot where the off hire is generated from.

Transport Order

This allows you to raise a purchase order for a 3rd party haulage company.

Partial 

You can switch the off hire to a partial off hire if not all of the equipment is returned.

Amend

The contract can be amended by clicking on this.

Add Sale/Charge

You can add additional sales/charges to the contract from here.

Meter 

If the machine has an hour meter, then you can log the hours at off hire.

Cost

This will show the cost of the hire. If this is a cash contract, this is where you would take additional payments or refunds from.

Confirm

This confirms that the contract is closed.

Close

This will close the off hire screen and leave the off hire unconfirmed.
