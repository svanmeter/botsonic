Customers by Discount




Overview
 

This article discusses the customers by discount report. This report lists customers based off what is in the discount field in their customer record.


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Income Menu > Customer Income by Grouping > Group 1: Discount Code
Elite: Right Menu > Reports > Search: Customer Income by Grouping


From the report generator, select Discount Code for Group 1.
 


This report is a drill down type of report where the first screen will look like the following:

 

 

You can double click on the discounts in blue font to view additional information.
