Customer Actions


 

You can enter or scan in contract number.

 

Selecting the "Search Option" or "Show Filters" will display additional search features when searching for a contract. For example, Customer #, Customer Name, DL No, City, Customer Key, Phone, Email).

 

Entering an * in the Contract Search field will display all customer contracts sorting by Key, Contract No, Name City, Address, Telephone, Status.

 

Selecting the contract number, will display more detailed information about the contract.

 

 

Information

Selecting these options will display detailed information about the customer to include (Customer Key, Email, Phone, Mobile, Bill Contact, Salesperson, Work #, and Billing Phone #).

 

There are many useful tools a salesman or driver can use to access, enter, or update a customer contract in the field.

 

Call Logs - This allows you to create add notes to a call log. This will be updated on the customer contract.

Get Directions - Will use your default mapping app to display the address and allow for turn by turn directions. (Tested with Google Maps and Waze; may work with other map apps).

New Search - Will allow you to search other customer contracts.

 

Show/Hide Filter

The Show/Hide feature displays options to refine your Contracts, Customer, and Item Searches by Item Type, Contract Status, Sales Rep, Customer Type. These selections are saved to your device, when the next time you do a search, these features will apply.
