Customers by Finance Charge Exempt




Overview
 

This article will discuss the Finance Charge Exempt report. This report lists all customers who have been marked as finance charge exempt in their customer record.

 

Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Income Menu > Customer Income by Grouping > Group 1: Finance Charge Exempt
Elite: Right Menu > Reports > Search: Customer Income by Grouping

 

From the report generator, select Finance Charge Exempt for Group 1.
 


 

This is a drill down type of report where the first screen will look like the following:
 


You can double click on the options in blue font to view additional detailed information.
