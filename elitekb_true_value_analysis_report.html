True Value Inventory Analysis Report

 

This report breaks down your active inventory by Item Utilization and Purchase Cost.

 

Utilization -  Will break down the Utilization by Month to Date and Lifetime to date.

            Month to Date – Will show number of times out, income, expense Avg rental, and Return on Investment (ROI)

            Life to Date – Will show Net gain and Return on your Investment (ROI)

 

Purchase Cost – Will show the following items:

            Last Date – Last date the item was purchased

            Purchase Date – When the item was originally purchased

            Cost – Purchase Cost of the item
