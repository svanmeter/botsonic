System Notifications

How to get there:  Program Menu > Configurations > Reports and Notifications > System Notification

 

System Notifications can be set up in Point of Rental, sending a notification to a person based on modifications or changes that are made to contracts, purchase orders, etc. Below are types of notifications.

Bank Deposit Made– Creates notification every time a Bank Deposit is generated in the system. If you are integrated with CC and have either the 'Bank Deposit Made' or the 'End of Day Complete' Notifications setup. If there is a Credit card imbalance and the System Generates an EOD adjustment a Second Notification is sent that states there was a problem.

Cancel Reservation- Creates notification whenever a Reservation is canceled.

Cancel Reservation within X Days- Creates notification whenever a Reservation is canceled.  Criteria to be entered for within how many days of cancellation before out date

Contract Needs Billing Review- Creates notification when the box is checked in Contract Processing that bill needs to be reviewed.  Would only use this if you haven’t set up defaults for Review Billing.

End of Day Completed - Creates notification when End of Day has been completed. If you are a Credit Card integrated and have a credit card imbalance at the end of the day, there will be a second notification stating there was a problem with the AutoClose. If you are integrated with CC and have either the 'Bank Deposit Made' or the 'End of Day Complete' Notifications setup. If there is a Credit card imbalance and the System Generates an EOD adjustment a Second Notification is sent that states there was a problem.

GPS Geofence Alert– For those customers who use TSO for their AEMP GPS tracking you will now be able to set up a notification if an item breaks the GeoFence that TSO sets at the End of the Day.

Item due for maintenance– Creates notification if there is a piece of equipment that has been assigned a maintenance schedule. The notification will only be sent out if the frequency is greater than 1. To avoid notifications for every rental maintenance schedule. Criteria would be the item number.

Item due for Re-Order- Creates notification if items are set up to have Max/Mins and have exceeded their minimum.

Item Overbooked- A person can be notified when items that are overbooked needs attention.

Modification of Locked contract– If you do or don’t have a password in place to unlock a reservation, this will Create a Notification when a locked contract is modified.

Modified Contract lowered amount-  You must enter a $ amount for the criteria based upon an order being lowered by a minimum of $150.00 for example.  It will give you a $ amount of the change as well as what the current total is on the order.

New Call log for customer #- Creates notification for a specific customer if a new call log gets created.

New Call log for type #- Creates notification for a specific customer type if a new call log gets created.

New Contract for over amount – You must enter a $ amount for the criteria based upon a new order being entered by a minimum of $500.00 for example.  It will give you the current total that is on the order.

New Missed Rental added– Creates notification for the entry of a Missed Rental including the reason for the Missed Rental.

New Purchase Order over amount – You must enter a $ amount for the criteria based upon a new Purchase Order being entered by a minimum of $500.00 for example.  It will give you the current total that is on the Purchase Order.

New quote over amount – You must enter a $ amount for the criteria based upon a new Quote being entered by a minimum of $500.00 for example.  It will give you the current total that is on the Quote.

New Rental item created – Creates notification of who entered the new item along with the description of the New Item.

New Reservation within X days - You can now set up a System Notification every time a new reservation is written with a reservation date within X days.

New Sales item created – Creates notification of who entered the new item along with the description of the New Item.

New Subrental request within X Days – You can now set up a System Notification every time a new Subrental is written with a Subrental date within X days of Out Date.

Overbook within X Days – Creates notification if an item is entered and overbooking on an order with criteria based upon the # of days before the Out Date of the order.

Refund on Closed Contract – Creates notification for every Closed contract that gets modified and money has been refunded.

Refund over a certain amount – Creates notification for every Closed contract that gets modified and money has been refunded with criteria of over a certain amount.

Rental Item marked for Sale–Creates notification that informs you of the item and the Sell Price that has been established for the item.

Sold Asset Over $ x Cost – Creates notification of Rental item being sold with criteria set for over a specific $ amount and who generated the sale.

Web Payment received – Creates notification of Customer and payment amount

Web Quote received– Creates notification for Web Quote received if you have integrated website.
