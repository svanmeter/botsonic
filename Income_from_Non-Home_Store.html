Income from Non-Home Store

 

This report shows income generated on items rented at a store other than its home store.  This report is useful to determine a revenue split between stores or whether you should be purchasing a certain item for the other store. You can select the beginning date and ending date of the report.  It will look like the following:

 

 

Click on the blue amount to show a more detailed report.
