Adding Serialized Inventory Stock/Quantities

This article guides you through the process of adding serialized inventory stock and quantities in the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Understanding serialized inventory
  • Adding serialized items to inventory
  • Managing stock quantities for serialized items

Understanding serialized inventory

Serialized inventory refers to items that are uniquely identified with a serial number, allowing for more precise tracking and management.

Adding serialized items to inventory

Add serialized items to your inventory to keep accurate track of each individual item.

  1. Navigate to the Inventory section in the system.
  2. Select the option to add a new item and choose ‘Serialized’ as the item type.
  3. Enter the item details, including its unique serial number.

Managing stock quantities for serialized items

Manage the quantities of serialized items to ensure accurate tracking and availability.

  1. For each serialized item, update its stock quantity as items are added or removed from inventory.
  2. Monitor the status of serialized items to track their movement and usage.
  3. Regularly audit serialized inventory for accuracy and discrepancies.
