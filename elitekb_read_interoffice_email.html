Read Interoffice Email

 

If you want to read your Email, select the Read Email button. You may print the message by using the Print button. After reading your Email, you may use either the OK or Cancel buttons to go back to the Counter System Menu. If you select the OK button, you will be given the opportunity to delete your Email. The Cancel option does not offer the delete option

            * Note: When anyone has a new Interoffice Email, they will be notified every time they enter their operator ID in Counter System until it is read.
