Customer Revenue Trend




Overview
 

This report lists customer revenue by cash and account customers. It will show current year revenue as well as revenue for the past four years.


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Customer Analysis > Customer Revenue Trend
Elite: Right Menu > Customer Revenue Trend 

 

1. Select your grouping parameters and run your report.
