Alert Notifications

 

 

 

Overview

 

This article explains how to create and send alert notifications. You can send out alert notifications to customers, such as weather advisories, traffic delays, or major accidents. Notifications are a good way to communicate with your customers about any potential delays or warnings in relation to your customer's delivery, pickup, or event. You can always keep your customers informed regarding any potential concerns that might affect contract fulfillment.

 

 

Topics included in this article

 

  • Creating Alert Notifications
  • Sending an Alert Notification

 

Prerequisites

 

Only applicable for users licensed with the MASS/FAX/EMAIL module.

 

 

 

Here's how to get there:

Expert: Program Menu > Configuration > System Configurations > Email Letters

Elite: Gear icon > App Configurations > System Configurations > Email Letters

 

 

 

Creating Alert Notifications

 

1. Select Add to create a default alert email with the type of advisory you would like to send to your customers. You can set up multiple email letters for different types of advisories.

 

2. Select a message status.

 

3. Select one, or all, stores.

 

4. Enter your message description and subject.

 

5. Enter your message in the body field.

 

 

 

 

Sending an Alert Notification

 

1. From Day at a Glance, you can select letters, located at the top of the page. From the drop-down, select the type of advisory to be sent out. This will automatically send out an alert to all customers located under the current tab.

 

For example, if you have the reservation tab opened, an alert will go to all customers under reservations. If you have the went out tab opened, then an alert will be sent out to all customers located under the went out tab.

 

Note: Program Menu > Counter System > Day at a Glance

 

 

2. You can refine your selection when sending out alerts by departments, zip codes, customers, etc.

 

3. For departments, check the Show Item Detail box and select the department you would like to send the alert to.

 

 

4. You can also hover your mouse over any of the select green columns and click on the black triangle arrow. This will display only items in the column. Check only the boxes you would like to send an alert to. 

 

5. You can right-click on the green header bar and check the box for zip codes. From there hover your mouse over the word zip code. A small black triangle will appear. Click on the triangle and a box will open to only show all zip codes for those contracts. Check only the boxes for the zip codes you want to send an alert to.

 

For example, if there is a wind advisory for a certain area, you can select only the zip codes that are affected in that area.
