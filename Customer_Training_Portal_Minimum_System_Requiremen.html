Customer Training Portal Minimum System Requirements


 

 

The Point of Rental Learning & Development team would like to introduce the Customer Training Portal. This portal has over 200 courses for you to sit in a live training session or a self-paced course. Our self-paced courses have self-paced video and interactive courses to help you and your employees learn about the software Point of Rental has to offer. If you would like more information, please email the team.

 

 

Minimum System Requirements
 

The Customer Training Portal (LearnUpon) works with the below browsers. LearnUpon will support the most recent major and previous major versions. 

When using a supported browser for LearnUpon, you may need to enable JavaScript, enable cookies, and use TLS v1.2 or later. Pop-up blockers might need to be disabled for this site as well.

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge.
