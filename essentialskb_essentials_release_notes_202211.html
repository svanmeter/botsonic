8/25/2022 - Version 2022.11 

 
  • Front End custom Menu enhancements - If you have enabled your Front End Custom Menu, you now have the option to set one of the 'nodes', which is one of your top level selections as your Homepage, as well as create a custom header and footer. This gives you the option to customize the storefront. Note: If you are not using Rental Only as your workflow, you still have to make the event selections first before it will take you to your new homepage.
  • Custom Questions enhancement - Custom Questions can now show phone# as one of the selections, and you get the new fancy-shmancy phone format with countries/international selection both at the transaction and customer level.
  • Hourly Availability corrected - If you went into your inventory, clicked calendar view/more details/hourly, you got to see a view of that item's availability for each hour. Well, not really because it didn’t display anything. It was a really well designed calendar though. We have corrected that so it’s now showing your data all proper and stuff.
