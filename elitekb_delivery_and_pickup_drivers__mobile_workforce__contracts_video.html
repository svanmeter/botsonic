Delivery and Pickup Drivers Mobile Workforce - Contracts

In this video, you will learn how contracts work within the Mobile Workforce module. This video will go through some of the features as if used by a driver or Sales Rep.

Who is the Mobile Workforce designed for?

This module would normally be used for drivers or Sales Reps,

What does Mobile Workforce allowing them to do?

With a mobile device a driver or sales rep will be able to see contracts and inventory, take pictures, capture signatures, create call logs, and call items off rent, as well as clock in and out from the Jobsite
