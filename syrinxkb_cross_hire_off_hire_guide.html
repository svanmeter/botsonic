Syrinx® - Cross Hire Off Hire



 

Overview: This guide is designed to help you with the basics of off-hiring a contract with a cross-hire machine.




 

From the ribbon at the top you need to go to into Off Hire and click on Contract. You will then be presented with the Search Box.
 

 

You can search on any of the fields below. If you know the contract number, you can enter this and click search. Alternatively, as in the example below, I am filtering by customer. Once you have finished click Search or press Enter.


If the customer has more than one machine on hire based on your search, then you will be presented with a Find Contract screen. Select the contract you want by clicking on the line and then click OK.
 


Once you have clicked OK, you will be presented with the Off Hire Contract screen as shown below: 
 



You can click onto the Collection tab and enter any information regarding collecting the equipment:

 

 

When selecting request off hire, the system will prompt you for an off hire number from the supplier. If they do not use them, you can enter the date or the person’s name that you have spoken to.

 


You will then be presented with a confirmation screen. You need to double check the details of the off hire with the supplier.
 

 

On clicking Close, you will be asked if you would like to print an off request for the supplier. This can be sent to the supplier as confirmation of the off hire.

 


Once you have entered your info, you can click Print at the bottom and produce your collection note/off hire note:

 

 

From here you can print the document by clicking Quick Print (this will use your default printer), or alternatively, you can email the document straight to your customer.

 

 

Note: If you are using the Syrinx driver app then there is no need to print the paperwork. Simply tick ‘Requires Collection’ and the job will be available in the driver scheduler.

 
If the customer has returned the machine and you are happy with the condition etc., then you can confirm the contract as off hired.

 

The rest of the buttons are described below.
 

Off Hired at

This shows the depot where the off hire is generated from

Transport Order

This allows you to raise a purchase order for a 3rd party haulage company

Partial 

You can switch the off hire to a partial off hire if not all of the equipment is returned

Amend

The contract can be amended by clicking on this

Add Sale/Charge

You can add additional sales/charges to the contract from here

Meter 

If the machine has an hour meter, then you can log the hours at off hire

Cost

This will show the cost of the hire. If this is a cash contract, this is where you would take additional payments or refund from

Confirm

This confirms that the contract is closed

Close

This will close the off hire screen and leave the off hire unconfirmed
