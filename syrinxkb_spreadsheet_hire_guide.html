Syrinx Guide® - Spreadsheet Hire Guide

 

 

 

 

Overview: This guide is to help you with creating the contract where you have multiple items on hire to a customer, and they send through a spreadsheet with hire dates at end of the month.

 

 

 

 

If you add a line item into the contract and remove the stock number you can update the quantity and when hitting tab, you will then get an alert asking if you want to replace the line with (multiple) line items. Select Yes.

 

 

You can then, when ready, apply the asset numbers to the contract line by line.

 

 

By double-clicking on the description, or the charge amount you can select the on-hire (first-day hire) and off-hire (last-day hire) in the asset details. Each item can be different from the contract start date.

 

 

For days that are not in use during the on-hire and off-hire date, you will stand down these days from the amend contract screen once the contract is confirmed on-hire.

 

 

The suspend days are from- the start of the day, to - the end of the day, so one day will show the same date in the from and to sections of the stand down.

 

Each line item will have its own start and end dates, and also highlight any stand-downs added to the contract that apply during the month.

 

 

 

If you off-hire the contract each month, you can create a copy of the off-hired contract at the bottom once the off-hire is confirmed. This will save you from having to add each line again into the contract. All you will have to do is update each line's start and end dates.
