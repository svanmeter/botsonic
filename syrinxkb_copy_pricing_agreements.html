You can create pricing agreements for new customers that are copies of an existing pricing agreement. In order to create the new agreement for a customer, you simply need to open the Customer's record using the Amend button in the "Amend" section of the Syrinx SP\CRM tab. Once you have identified the customer record, you should press the "Pricing" button at the bottom of the "Amend Customers" screen. This will create a new pricing agreement for the customer if the customer is not already linked to an agreement. You can then use the "Copy From" button on the bottom of the "Amend Pricing Agreement" screen in order to copy over any agreed rates/sales prices/discounts from an existing pricing agreement.
