Credit Card Report




Overview
 

This report will show each credit card for the period you select sub-totaled by credit card type. This report can also be printed in summary where only the total by credit card type is shown.


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Accounting Reports > Accounting Reports Menu > Credit Card Report
Elite: Right Menu > Credit Card Report


The report will look like the following:

 


Note: There is an export to Excel icon in the upper left corner of the report. It will show expanded information once exported to Excel.
