7/5/2022 - Version 2022.09


  • Additional Fields for Transaction Notifications - Several of the notifications, including Last Minute Reservation and Reservation Created now have additional fields for you.
    • Quantity Reserved
    • Contract Number
    • Delivery Location
    • Pickup Location
 
  • Click to Pay - When you Collect Payment, there is a line called ‘Total Amount to Collect’ that shows the total amount owed. If you click it, did you know that it will pay the entire amount into ‘Amount Being Collected?’ It’s a really neat trick, but if you didn’t know it, don’t feel bad. We didn’t think it was clear that you can do that so we added text that says ‘Click to Pay’ to make it a bit more user friendly.

Item Hyperlinks in Email - If you have item hyperlinks, those will be included in the body of the email your customers receive. Useful for product and safety links for your items.
