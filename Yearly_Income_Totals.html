Yearly Income Totals


Location: Program Menu > Analysis Report > Income Analysis Reports > Yearly Income Totals.


Overview: The Yearly Income Totals report shows a breakdown between rentals, sales, damage waiver, other income, etc. for each of the previous years on record. 

  • You will be able to select a store location.
  • You will be asked whether to display total yearly income or year-to-date income. 
  • These amounts will be shown in a bar graph as well as the detailed income per year.
