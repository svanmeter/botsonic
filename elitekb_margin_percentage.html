Margin Percentage

With the Margin or Markup fieldset, it will calculate and set the selling price for an item.

 

From within a contract with sale items, you can right-click on the blue header bar. (located above the first item on the contract).

Select the Margin % box. You can move the column up or down by selecting the Move Up or Move Down button.

 

A new column will be added to your contract, displaying the margin or markup percentage.
