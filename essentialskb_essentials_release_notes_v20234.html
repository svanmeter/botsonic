3/13/2023 - Version 2023.4

 

 

I Storefront Enhancements - We have done several enhancements to make this easier for your customers

  • Storefront Contact Page - You now have the ability to add a contact page that your customers can send you a message or ask a question. "Is your refrigerator running? You better go catch it"
  • Storefront Cart Enhancement - We have given you an option to set the storefront checkout process to not require your customers to click continue shopping after every item added. Fewer clicks for your Customers equals happier customers and puts more money in your pocket faster.
  • Custom Tag Pricing on Storefront - A while back, we implemented being able to have special pricing by item based on a customer tag. So 'Great customer' got the item for $5 instead of $7. Or maybe 'Bad pain the neck customer' got the item for $10 instead of $7. However, we only did that on the counter. On the storefront the experience was a little wonky as it didn't display the special price until the very end. Now we have made it so that once the customer has logged in, that pricing is visible throughout the rest of the transaction.
 
  • Mobile Device Visual Enhancements 
    • Customer search shows the customers the full width of screen
    • Buttons have been redesigned to be more 'finger friendly' size
 
  • Batch Statements - We found that the Last Statement Date was not displaying in the customer record when running statements. We corrected this.
