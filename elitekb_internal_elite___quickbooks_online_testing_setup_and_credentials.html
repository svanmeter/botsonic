Steps for Testing QuickBooks Online


1. Configure POR settings.
 

a. Set the Export Format in the Account Numbers screen.
 


b. Click the Settings button > enter credentials.
 


 

2. Start the PORAccountingSyncService.

 

a. Go to Windows > Services
 


 

b. Scroll down to PORAccountingSync. Right click > Start. If it’s already running, stop and start it again to refresh the connection.
 


3. Write a contract.
 

a. In Point of Rental, write a contract and close it. Once it is closed, the PORAccountingSync service will send it over to QuickBooks Online automatically in about one minute.

b. You want to test with various types of contracts, so get creative here with how you write the contract.

c. Transaction Mode - In Transaction Mode, once a contract is closed in POR, payments can no longer be made towards the contract in POR. Payments must be made in QuickBooks Online. Test that a payment made in QuickBooks syncs back to POR and updates the contract.

 

4. Verify that the contract synced over to QuickBooks Online.
 

a. In QuickBooks Online, make sure you are set to “Accountant View.”
 


 

b. Transaction Mode - In QuickBooks Online, to view transactions sent over in Transaction mode, go to Left Menu > Invoicing > Invoices.
 

 

1. Verify Date.

2. Verify Customer.

3. Verify Line Item amounts.

4. Verify Accounts.

5. Verify Contract Total.

6. Verify Contract Number in the Notes/Memo field.


 

c. Real-Time & Batch Modes - In QuickBooks Online, to view Journal Entries sent over in Real-Time or Batch Mode, go to Left Menu > Reports > General Ledger

 


 

d. Look for lines in the General Ledger that say “Journal Entry” as opposed to “Invoice.”

 

 

1. Verify Date.

2. Verify Accounts.

3. Verify Debits & Credits.

4. Verify Description.

5. Verify Contract Number in the Notes/Memo field.

 

5. Write a Purchase Order.

 

a. In POR, write a Purchase Order and Receive All Items. Then export the PO to AP.
 


 

b. After the PO has been exported to AP, it will be synced to QuickBooks Online in about one minute.
 

6. Verify that the Purchase Order synced over to QuickBooks Online.

 

a. In QuickBooks Online, to view Purchase Orders that have been synced over, go to Left Menu > Expenses > Expenses.
 


 

b. Look for Expenses titled “Purchase Order.”

 


c. Verify the following details:

 

1. Vendor Name

2. Mailing address

3. Purchase Order date

4. Category, description, amount, and received

5. Vendor message
