Overview

A new screen has been added that combines information from the Hire Stock List, plus Hire Contracts, Off Hires and Exchanges, to display the current activity for the fleet items within a selected category:

 

Bands

The top (green) band shows items that are due to go out, either through unconfirmed hire contracts, or unconfirmed exchanges.  Items are listed regardless of whether or not a fleet item has been allocated. The ‘Type’ drop-down at the bottom lets you include quotations or exclude provisional bookings (as with the Unconfirmed Hire Contracts screen).  The Cancel Contract button will cancel the unconfirmed contract, and double-clicking the contract number will open the Enter Hire Contract screen.  The ‘Show All Unconfirmed?’ tickbox at the top of the screen will show unconfirmed contracts for all categories, rather than just those for the selected category. 

The second (un-coloured) band shows ongoing hires – i.e. items on open hire contracts where no off hire has been raised.  The Off Hire button can be used to raise a new off hire note for the item, and the ‘Exchange’ button can be used to raise a new Exchange Note.

The third (red) band shows items that are due off hire, either through unconfirmed off hire notes, or unconfirmed exchanges. The Off Hire button can be used to open the corresponding off hire note.

The final (blue) band shows items that are neither on hire, nor on an unconfirmed contract or exchange.  The Customer and Site columns show the last hirer of the item.  The ‘New’ button lets you raise a new contract for the selected item.

Substitutions

When entering a new contract, the detail screen for a hire item now lets you flag that an item is being provided as a substitution for another item:

These items are shown as ticked under the ‘Lieu’ column in the day book screen.  It also possible to filter the screen to only see these substitution items for a particular category by ticking the ‘Lieu?’ option at the bottom of the screen.

Similar Items

There is a box at the top of the Fleet Daybook screen that can be ticked to include other categories that are similar to the selected category.  To define the similar categories for the given category, double-click on the category at the top of the screen to open the ‘Amend Hire Category’ screen, click on the Daybook tab, and press the ‘Add Categories’ button:

There is also an ‘Exclude From Daybook’ box that can be ticked to indicate that the category should not be listed on the Fleet Day Book screen.

Bulk Items

Although the screen is designed primarily for use with individually numbered items, it will also list bulk items.  The ‘Qty’ column shows the quantity on hire for each of the hire contracts, and the quantity to be collected for items that are due off hire.  The bottom (blue) section does not list bulk hire items.

 

Additional Settings
