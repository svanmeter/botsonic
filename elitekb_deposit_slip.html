Deposit Slip

 

Go to Program Menu, Counter Systems, Tools.
 

There are times when you might take in large sums of cash or checks you do not want to keep in your cash drawer. You do not have to wait until the End of Day to deposit. You can create a deposit slip and take it to the bank.
 


Select the Deposit Slip. You would create the deposit slip the same way you would at the End of Day. The deposit slip form will save and exit after being left up for 30 minutes without any activity.

 


To reprint your deposit slip, go to Program Menu>>Accounting Reports>>Report Bank Deposit Slip.
 

 

To see how to add bank account numbers to the deposit slip, click Reprint Bank Deposit Slip .
