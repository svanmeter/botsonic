Customer WO Item Maintenance




Overview
 

This report shows all your customer’s items that are due for maintenance. For the items to show on this report they must be entered as Work Order Items and their maintenance must be set up in the item record.


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Maintenance Reports > Customer WO Item Maintenance
Elite: Right Menu > Reports icon > Customer WO Item Maintenance
