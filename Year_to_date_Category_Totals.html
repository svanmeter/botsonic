Year to date Category Totals

Location: Program Menu > Analysis Report > Income Analysis Reports > Year to date Category Totals


Overview: The Year to date Category Totals report shows each category’s total income for the monthly range that you select for each year.  For example, you can select a starting month of July and an ending month of September to give you the third quarter income for the last number of years.

You can choose to view the Year to Date Category Totals for:

  • Rental Items,
  • Sales Items,
  • Parts Items,
  • All Items. 


Instructions:

1. Select the starting months to report.
2. Select the ending months to report.
