Customer Portal Module




Overview
 

Take web payments and improve customer service with this feature that is available through our integrated websites. The Customer Portal gives your preferred customers protected access to their account information, including current balance, last payment amount, date, and past due amounts. Quotes, reservations, on rent contracts, invoices, and statements can be viewed and printed from here, giving your customers the freedom to access data securely—and saving you time and money as more customers self-serve.
