With our Online Customer Reservations and Payments add-on feature, your customers can go to your website, add things to their cart, create an account, and reserve and pay (if your credit card is integrated) for their items.  They can also log into their account from your website at any time to manage their profile and reservations.

Topics included in this article:

 

  • My Profile
  • My Reservations
  • Logout
  • View Cart 



My Profile
 
Customers can update any of their contact information, answer any custom questions you have created to show on your website, and opt in or out of any marketing emails your company sends.

 

 

Once all their information is updated, they will click Update Profile.


 


They can reset their password from here too.


 



My Reservations
 
Customers can see any order they may have whether it be a quote, reservation or open order.  Depending on your Front End Configurations, they can view and/or edit their orders.


 



Logout
 
Customers can log out of their online account and walk away from their computer without anyone else seeing their account information. 


 



View Cart
 
Customers can view any items in their existing cart that haven't been sent over as quote and/or reservation to you yet.
