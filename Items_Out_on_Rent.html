Items Out on Rent

 

This report will show all the items there were out on rent at the end of business on the selected date.  This is useful to find out whether an item was not on your premises.  You will be asked whether to print the contract detail.  If you answer “Yes” then each contract will be shown.  If you answer “No”, then only the item, the times rented, and the total income will be displayed.
