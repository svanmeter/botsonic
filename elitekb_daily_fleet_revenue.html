Daily Fleet Revenue




Overview
 

This article will discuss the Daily Fleet Revenue report. This report shows the amount of income out on rent for the day. It can also be run for a past date. This report will average the income out over the duration of the rental. For example, an item rented for 20 days will have its income listed as 1/20 of the total revenue.


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Income Analysis > Daily Fleet Revenue
Elite: Right Menu > Reports Icon > Daily Fleet Revenue
 

 

1. Select the date you would like to run for your report.



2. Enter your parameter values for the report.



3. Press OK.
