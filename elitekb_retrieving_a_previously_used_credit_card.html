Retrieving a Previously Used Credit Card

 

Point-of-Rental allows you the ability to retrieve and use credit cards that were previously used either in the same transaction or from any transaction in history.

 

Same Transaction

• Best practice is to swipe the customer’s credit card and verify their identity whenever possible.

• When prompted to swipe or enter the credit card number, press the Page Up key.

• You will be prompted to acknowledge that the transaction is not swiped.
