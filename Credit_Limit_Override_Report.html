Credit Limit Override Report




Overview
 

This selection produces a report of all credit limits that have been overridden. It shows the customer and the amount over their credit limit. This report is grouped by the operator who overrode the credit limit. First, you must enter the beginning date and the ending date of the report.


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Override Reports > Credit Limit Overrides
Elite: Right Menu > Credit Limit Overrides
