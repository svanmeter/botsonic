Coupons Given Report




Overview
 

This selection produces a report of all coupons given to customers. The report is sorted by coupon. It shows the coupon amount and the percentage discount off the entire contract amount. 


Here's how to get there: 
Expert: Program Menu > Override Reports > Coupons Given Report
Elite: Right Menu > Coupons Given Report

 

1. First, you must enter the beginning date and the ending date of the report.
