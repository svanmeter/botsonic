Repair Builds

 

Repairs Build: Allows you to track additional costs on serialized sales items. Trade ins and sales items often require parts and labor. This allows you to track those costs against the sales item. Any additional costs are now added under the “extra” field under the purchases tab. Create a new customer with the key as “Repairs-build”. Add the item that needs parts or labor first then add any additional parts or labor.

 

First create your repair customer with the key of Repairs-Build.   Make sure that customer is marked non-taxable.   

 

Go into the counter system, open a new repair contract.   Choose Repairs-build, add your sales item that you are going to improve.     Add your parts and labor to the contract.   

 

You will receive a notification about the cost of the repairs being added to the item.

 

When the work is completed, you will close the Repairs-Build contract and Point of Rental will add the cost of the labor and repairs to the extra cost field in the purchase line.
