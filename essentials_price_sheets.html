  • Understanding price sheets

Understanding price sheets

Gain a comprehensive understanding of what price sheets are and their role in pricing management.

Creating and customizing price sheets

Learn how to create and tailor price sheets to suit your business needs and pricing strategies.

  1. Create custom price sheets based on product categories or customer segments.
  2. Set pricing rules and discounts for specific items or groups.
  3. Modify price sheets to reflect changes in pricing strategy.

Applying price sheets to transactions

Discover how to apply price sheets to transactions, ensuring accurate pricing and discounts for customers.

  1. Select the appropriate price sheet for each transaction.
  2. Automatically calculate prices and discounts based on the chosen price sheet.
  3. Review and verify pricing adjustments before finalizing transactions.

Monitoring price sheet performance

Explore methods for tracking and evaluating the performance and effectiveness of your price sheets.

  1. Analyze sales and revenue data related to specific price sheets.
  2. Observe customer response and satisfaction with pricing changes.
  3. Make data-driven adjustments to price sheets for optimization.
