2/20/2023 - Version 2023.3



 
  •    Batch Statements - Hooray! Our development team put in a lot of work to save you time. This new feature allows you to send multiple customers statements at once.
    • Set up individual customers to receive statements
    • Mark whether you want them to print or email based on the email address in their customer record
    • Add a new field in column manager called 'Received Owed'
    • Create a filter to only show customers set to send statements that have received amount owed
    • Most importantly, as the title suggests, you can run statements for more than 1 customer at a time. 
 
  •    Bulk Email Transactions - You now have a button at the bottom of the customer history section of each customer that you can email contracts in mass to your customers. Perfect for those times when customers want all their reservations, or they ask you to send them all received contracts they owe money on. Or more precisely when you want them to have the received contracts they owe you money on so they will pay up and quit stalling.
 
  •    Personal Page Size - At the bottom of several screens, you have a selection for # of records that defaults to 25. If you changed it to say 100, navigated away and came back, it reverted to 25. Now it saves your setting per user for customers/inventory/inventory search when writing contracts, and of course, Workbench. How cool is that? Or if you are a California customer, it's Rad.
 
  •   New Job Number Field - We have a field in the transaction already called PO Number, but we now have the ability for you to add another one called Job Number. This only displays and prints on the contract if you enable it in configuration.
 
  • Updated Example Contract Formats - We have examples in configuration of the contract formats to show you what they look like. The problem was they were old. Like Back to the Future day old. We updated with current information and current dates. Great Scott!!!
 
  •    Availability/Overbook Enhancement (Mid Release 2/7/2023)- We have made a change to how availability and overbooking calculates. We received a lot of feedback asking us to change this. In the past, if an item was overdue, it took it out of availability for the near and distant future, causing overbooks. Our change is simple, but effective. This will keep overbookings from clogging up your “far in the future reservations” with overbooks just because something is a day or 2 late, or you just haven’t had time to pick it up yet.
    • Overdue but due back same day - Availability will show out until the end of that business day only
    • Overdue due yesterday - Will be subtracted from availability for the next 7 days
    • Overdue due 2 days prior or before - Will be subtracted from all future availability
 
  • Corrected issue with Item Short Description - We had this pesky issue where if you added a short description, saved it, and then went to another item and back, it removed the text you typed in. It’s been corrected.
