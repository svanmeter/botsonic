Customer Income Menu



Overview
 

The Customer Income Menu has multiple reports to show income by customer. 


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Income Menu
Elite: Right Menu > Search bar

 

Note: In Elite you will have to search for each report individually as there are no menu groupings like there are in Expert.
