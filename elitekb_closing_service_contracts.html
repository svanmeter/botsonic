Closing Service Contracts

 

 

 

Overview

 

For those companies that run hundreds of service calls every week, staying on top of closing them out can be a thorny issue. This article explains the most efficient way to close service contracts through Day at a Glance.

 

 

 

Closing service contracts

 

 

1. If you are using Expert, go to Counter System > Day at a Glance > Service Calls tab. If you are using Elite, go to Tools > Day at a Glance > Service Calls tab.

 

 

2. From the Service Calls tab in Day at a Glance, set a date. You can close service contracts up to 14 days forward in time from the date selected.

 

3. Then go up to Options and select Close All Service Contracts.

 

 

Note: During the end of day process, any service contract older than thirty days with no repair cost assigned will be auto closed.
