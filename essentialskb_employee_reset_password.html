Do you have an employee that has forgotten or misplaced their Rental Essentials password? It’s very simple to reset their password. They can always click the Forgot Password link on the login screen and follow the steps in our Forgotten Password document.
 


 

1. An admin can also reset their password for them by going to People > Employees.

2. From here, click the edit icon next to the employee’s name.

3. To your right, you will see a Reset Password button which you will click to reset the employee’s password. Complete and confirm their new password. 


Checking the Notify Employee box will send the employee an email with their new password. Don’t forget to click Reset Password.
