Missed Rentals

 

Overview: Allows you to enter a reason for not being able to fulfill customer request for an inventory item. You will be able to enter a missed rental from either the Counter System or through Item Availability. 

Go to Counter System>Missed Rental
 

Item Look-up – This allows you to select items from inventory selection to look up. Once the item is selected, it will show in the Item Name.

Item Name – Allows you to enter the inventory name that you do not have in stock.

Customer – Clicking the blue customer name will put you on the customer selection screen, where you can select the customer requesting the missed inventory.

Phone in/Walk-in – This allows you to check how the customer asked about renting the inventory item.

Quantity Desired – This allows you to enter how many of the missed rental the customer is asking for.

Approximate Rental Amt - - The amount of missed rental.

Reason – This allows you to enter a reason why the inventory is not in stock.

Note – Add any extra notes or comments about missed rental.

 

*Note – You can create a system notification to notify a sales rep or customer service person immediately, allowing you to either substitute or subrent the item

 

 

Missed Rental Report

This report lists all the items that you missed a rental on. It is sorted by item name.  The information from this report comes from the Missed Rental input.  If the counter personnel doesn’t enter items as they are missed, then nothing will print.  You will be asked to enter a beginning date and an ending date for the report.
