Customer Analysis Reports




Overview

The Customer Analysis Reports menu will provide you with a variety of customer-centric reports that can be run.

 

Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Customer Analysis
Elite: Right Menu > Searchbar
 

Note: Elite does not have report groupings like Expert. You will have to search for each report individually.
