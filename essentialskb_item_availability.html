Item Availability
 

Item Availability can be accessed by hovering over Inventory in the top menu bar, and selecting Inventory.
 

 

Once Inventory has been opened, locate the desired item, and press the eye symbol, located next to the name of the item.  
 

 

Note: Current availability is displayed in the column with the header "Available."

 

After selecting the eye symbol, the Item Availability calendar will appear.

 

 

In the calendar, each day has a set of three numbers that indicate the availability of an item throughout the day. The color code indicates the time of day and minimum availability.

  • Orange - Displays the item availability at the start of the day. 
  • Blue - Displays the item availability at the end of the day. 
  • Light Blue - Displays the item availability if all scheduled returns are not returned.
  • You can also choose to show availability on days you are closed, per user. 

 

 

To access a more detailed view of Item Availability, select More Details. In this menu, there are three sections: Rates, Availability and Specifications.  

 

Note: Overdue items will be subtracted from availability until they are returned or due date updated for future due date, based on the following conditions.
 

  • Overdue but same day - Item availability will show out until the end of that business day only.
  • Overdue due yesterday - Item will be subtracted from availability for the next 7 days.
  • Overdue due 2 days prior or before - Item will be subtracted from all future availability.


 

Rates
 

This section displays the pricing rates of the item and other pricing information that includes Minimum Rental Period, Prepayment, Replacement Cost, Maintenance Fee and Damage Protection Override. 
 

 

Note: Damage Waiver Override will not populate any information if there is a default percentage set in Configuration > Company Configuration.  

 

Availability
 

The Availability section can display item availability by month, week, or hour. The Show Category Availability button displays the availability of other items in the same category.  The Show Availability Chart button allows the user to select a date range to view item availability.
 



 

Multi-Store Availability

If you are multistore, you will be able to see your availability at other stores by clicking Show Multistore Availability.
 


 

Variant Inventory


If your item has variant properties, then you can choose the variant that you want to view to see how much is in stock.

 

 
 

Specifications

 

The specifications of the item can be viewed if Show Details is selected.
