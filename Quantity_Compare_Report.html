Quantity Compare Report

 

This report shows the maximum number of items rented on a given day, report can be run for a 12,24,36,48 or lifetime, showing the Qty on Rent, Days Rented, Income, Cost, Annualized ROI. This report is helpful to determine whether you own too many of the item or whether you should purchase more. The Max Overlap by Month is the maximum units you had rented per day for that month
