Data Management Tools:

 

Sub Rent Quantity Change

 

1. Select the Sub Rent Quantity Change from the Menu.

2. Make your Selection by Division, Item Type, Category, and Bulk or Serialized.

3. You may also enter the Old Quantity to change or check the box for All Items.

4. Enter the New Quantity you want the records updated to.

5. Select Go and the records will be updated. A window will pop up when letting you know the process is done.
