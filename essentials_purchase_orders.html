Purchase Orders

This article outlines the process for creating and managing purchase orders in the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Creating a purchase order
  • Managing supplier information
  • Tracking purchase order status
  • Handling purchase order revisions

Creating a purchase order

Create purchase orders to manage and track the procurement of goods and services from suppliers.

  1. Navigate to the Purchasing section in the system.
  2. Select ‘Create Purchase Order’ and enter the required details, such as supplier, items, quantities, and prices.
  3. Review and submit the purchase order for processing.

Managing supplier information

Keep supplier information up-to-date to streamline the purchasing process.

  1. Maintain a database of supplier contacts and profiles in the system.
  2. Regularly update supplier information, including contact details and terms of service.
  3. Manage supplier performance and history to inform future purchasing decisions.

Tracking purchase order status

Track the status of purchase orders from creation to completion to ensure timely fulfillment.

  1. Utilize the system's tracking features to monitor the progress of each purchase order.
  2. Set up notifications for updates or changes in purchase order status.
  3. Coordinate with suppliers and internal teams for any necessary follow-up or adjustments.

Handling purchase order revisions

Manage changes or revisions to purchase orders efficiently to maintain accuracy and alignment with procurement needs.

  1. Amend purchase orders in the system when there are changes in requirements or conditions.
  2. Ensure all relevant parties are informed of the revisions and adjustments.
  3. Track the revisions history for record-keeping and audit purposes.
