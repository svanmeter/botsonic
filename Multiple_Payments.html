Multiple Payments

 

Overview: Paying a contract with multiple payment methods is a simple process in Point-of-Rental. When a customer wants to pay part of a contract by cash or check and the balance by credit card it can be handled very easily using the Another Payment button.

 

1.   Enter a partial payment in the Rental / Sale amount field.

2.   Choose the first payment method.

3.   Click on the Another Payment button to the right of the payment method selection box.

 

 

After processing your payment, the payment screen will appear again with the new balance due in the rental/sale amount.

 

 

4.   Enter a partial payment or the full balance due in the Rental / Sale amount field.

5.   Choose the next payment method.

6.   To continue making Another Payment repeat step 3, otherwise click the OK button in the bottom right corner to finish.

 

 

This procedure can also be used when a customer wants to pay with two credit cards.
