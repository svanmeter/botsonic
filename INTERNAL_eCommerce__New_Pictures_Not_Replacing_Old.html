INTERNAL eCommerce | New Pictures Not Replacing Old Ones on the Website



 

If a customer has replaced item or category images in their POR, then updated the website, but they're still seeing the old images on the website, they most likely have a caching issue.


Have them press the Ctrl and F5 keys on the pages with the old images, and most of the time that will solve the problem.


However, there are some times when they actually need to open the item or category, right click and copy the image, then right click and paste it.  Seems wonky, but sometimes that works!


In rare instances, there is an option in the Update Website to Force Picture Upload.  That WILL fix the issue, but it takes a long time and sucks resources, so we only use it as a last ditch option.  To use it, after selecting Update Website and clicking OK on the popup message, click on the Tools > Options menu, then check the Force Picture Upload box and click OK.  Then run the Update.  Once it has completed, the new pictures should all be showing on the site.
