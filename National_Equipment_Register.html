National Equipment Register

 


 

Some insurance companies will give you a discount if you register your equipment with the National Equipment Register. This report lists all the information needed to register with the National Equipment Register. When you run it, you will be asked to enter several fields. You can either type in the response or leave it blank and fill them in on the NER’s site. Once the report is displayed, you can click the Export button at the top of the report (looks like an envelope). Then select Excel and export the file. Once you give it a name, you can upload the file to NER. You can find more information under their website at www.ner.net.
