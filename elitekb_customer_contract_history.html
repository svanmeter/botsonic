Customer Contract History




Overview
 

This report will show all the contracts for a customer. The report includes the contract number, date, rent amount, sale amount, tax amount, and job description. You will be asked to select the customer you want for the report. 

 

Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Contract History Reports > Customer Contract History
Elite: Right Menu > Customer Contract History 

 

1. Select your customer.

 

2. Adjust your Report Date and Store parameters as necessary.

 

3. Select the Generate button.
