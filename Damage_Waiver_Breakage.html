Damage Waiver Breakage




Overview
 

This article will discuss the Damage Waiver Breakage report. This report shows the income from damage waivers and the cost of covering breakage under damage waivers. It will also include non-damage waiver coverage to give an overall profit for your items.


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Income Analysis > Damage Waiver Breakage
Elite: Right Menu > Reports Icon > Damage Waiver Breakage
 

 

1. Select your report beginning and ending dates.
 

2. Select your report data grouping.
 

3. Select which store, or all stores, to report on.
 

4. Define your printer.

5.  Select Generate.
