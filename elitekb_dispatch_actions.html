User is presented with applicable action for the selected contract:

There are three icons, across the top of the screen, by selecting one of these icons, additional information will be displayed in relation to that particular icon (Contract, Customer, Dispatch)

 

Contract information will be displayed to include (Store, Information, Ordered By, Type of Customer, PO#, Job#, Salesman, and Comments).

 

Customer information will be displayed to include (Status, Email, Phone, Mobile, PO#, Work, Billing Contact, Billing Phone)

Dispatch information will be displayed to include (# of Stops, D/P State, Plan On-Site, Plan Arrive, Plan Depart, Required Arrive, Arrived, Departed, Delivery Notes).

There are many useful tools a salesman or driver can use to (access, enter, or update) a contract in the field.

Call Log- This allows you to create a call log to the customer's contract.

Once you enter the information and submit it, you will see the status will change to "Call Log Success" letting you know the action was sent.

Photos - Allows a driver or salesperson to take a picture on-site and have it added to the contract.

Select Serialized - This allows you to see serialized items listed on the contract.

On the Way - This will inform the customer through an email notification that the driver is on the way to the customer.

Signature- Allows the driver to capture the customer's signatures on-site, adding them to the contract.

View Items - Shows items listed from the contract.

Arrived - Allows the dispatcher to know when the driver has arrived on site.

Departed - Allows the dispatcher to know when the driver has left the site.

Call-Off Rent - The driver can call an item off rent from the Jobsite.

Pick Up Items - Your driver can select the equipment being picked up from the job site. If the total quantity is being picked up, then you can select either all button or check the box next to each item.

* Note: You will need to have the contract fulfillment module to be able to use this feature.

If you are picking up another amount other than the total quantity, you can select the green item key and enter a different quantity amount. Once done, select submit.

Get Directions - Will use your default mapping app to display the address and allow for turn by turn directions. (Tested with Google Maps and Waze; may work with other map apps).

Route Details - Shows driver a list of deliveries/pickups for the day. Routes to include (Stop #, Contract #, Status, Plan Arrive, Plan Depart, Required Arrive, Site, Store location). Here he can select the start route to let the dispatcher know he is starting the route.

            *Note – The dispatcher who created the routes will see those routes on the WorkForce without having to assign themselves as a driver or crew.
