Please refer to POR uLearn or call POR Support for guidance in updating Calendar Maintenance.

 
 

Special/Holiday Hours

 

Special/Holiday hours may also be shown on your site. These are set by the entries in the Special Days matrix in POR. If you add entries for your holiday hours/closures, these will then show on the website.

TIP: If you see dates showing up that are not holidays or entries that you made yourself, there is most likely a discrepancy in the open hours between what’s entered in Calendar Maintenance and in Special Days. For example, let’s say your EOM entry for October has open hours of 8am-5pm on Special Days. But your actual open hours in Calendar Maintenance for that day of the week are 7:30am-5pm. That discrepancy will cause our code to show that day under the Special/Holiday Hours heading. The fix? Update the EOM entry to show the same hours as that day of the week in Calendar Maintenance.

You can opt to NOT show this section on your website; just let your Web Design Team know.
