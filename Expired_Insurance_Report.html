Expired Insurance Report

 

The Customer Expired Insurance Report prints all the customers whose insurance has expired by the date you choose.  The report is grouped by the year expired. This report is a drill-down type report where the first screen you will see will look like the following:


 

You can double click on the expiration year in blue font or on the dollar amounts to view detailed information pertaining to the option you select.
