Item & Transaction Discounts

This article details how to apply discounts to items and transactions within the system.

Topics included in this article

  • Applying item-level discounts
  • Applying transaction-level discounts
  • Discount approval process
  • Reporting on discounts

Applying item-level discounts

Item-level discounts are applied directly to individual items in a transaction.

  1. Select the item within the transaction.
  2. Enter the discount percentage or amount in the designated discount field.
  3. The discounted price will be automatically recalculated and displayed.

Applying transaction-level discounts

Transaction-level discounts are applied to the total amount of the transaction.

  1. At the transaction summary, locate the discount field.
  2. Input the discount percentage or amount to be applied to the total transaction.
  3. The system will update the total amount to reflect the discount.

Discount approval process

In some cases, discounts may require approval from a supervisor or manager.

  1. Submit the transaction with the discount for approval.
  2. The designated approver will receive a notification for review.
  3. Once approved, the discount will be finalized in the transaction.

Reporting on discounts

Generate reports to track and analyze discounts applied to transactions.

  1. Go to the Reports section.
  2. Select the appropriate report type for discount analysis.
  3. Review the report to understand discount trends and impacts.
