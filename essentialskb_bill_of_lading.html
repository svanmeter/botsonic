A Bill of Lading is a shipping document, either outgoing or incoming. In order to set it up, you may want to define specifications in the items you will be shipping. You can define to use standard dimensions, and set up Length and Width as an example.

 



At the transaction level, you can print a bill of lading. When you choose that, you will get this screen. You can fill in Outgoing or Incoming (Incoming will not include sales items). You can populate all the information below. 

 



Once you save, you will get the Bill of Lading and can print.
