Contracts Opened per Hour




Overview
 

This report shows how many contracts were opened and closed for every hour of the day for each day of the week.


Here's how to get there:
Expert: Program Menu > Analysis Reports > Transactions Analysis > Contracts Opened per Hour
Elite: Right Menu > Contracts Opened per Hour

 

1. Select your parameters and click Generate.
