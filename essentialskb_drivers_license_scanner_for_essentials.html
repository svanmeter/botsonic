Point of Rental's Essentials software can support the entry of customer data by scanning the 2-D driver's license barcode, which is supported in most states. Driver’s license data does not include email or phone numbers, so you must still collect these manually.

 

Scanning of 2D driver's license barcodes is only supported using the Motorola/Symbol DS9208-DL00004NNWW 2-d barcode scanner with driver's license support. Please contact our support team before purchasing a barcode scanner for driver's license scanning so that we can help you to ensure that your choice will work with the system.

 

Note: If you purchase a non-recommended barcode scanner, then your barcode scanner might not work correctly with the Point of Rental system.
 

For setup instructions, please go to Configuration > Barcode Configuration Information.
