Desired Deposit 

 

You can add a Desired Deposit to your quotes within Point of Rental. On the contract processing screen, there is a field that will allow you to manually enter the amount of the deposit that you are requiring on this cash customer rental. Additionally, if you have a deposit on an item, it will automatically pull that into the desired deposit field. 

 

 * Note; If you would like to remove the deposit amount, you would need to zero out the deposit on the contract.

 

The Desired Deposit will print out on your Quote.
