Work Order Format Sections

Location: Program Menu>Configurations>System Configurations>Work Order Formats

 

Key Features:

  • Shading - Let’s choose from 4 different shade levels. This is useful if you want no shading, or you can adjust the darkness of the shading depending on your printer. 


Example:


Descriptions:
 

Delivery CommentsThis will print delivery comments onto the Work Order.Backside LegaleseThe check box next to Backside Legalese will tell Point-of-Rental whether to print the Backside Legalese in before the contract. (Must be an RTF format).
Delivery at TopThis will print delivery information on the top of the Work OrderCredit Card AuthorizationWill print Credit Card Authorization number onto the Work Order. (Must be in an RTF format). 
Print Delivery TruckPrints the delivery truck onto the Work Order. Marketing PagePrints out a Marketing Page (Must be an RTF format)
Contract CommentsPrints any comments from the contract comment tab onto the Work Order.Print SummaryWill only print a summary version of the Work Order. 
Payment DetailsPrints any payment details onto the Work Order.Print After ContractIf checked, the Backside Legalese will print after the contract as a second page. 
Trans ModificationsPrints transaction modifications number onto the Work Order.
