INTERNAL Elite | QuickBooks Online Connection Instructions




This article explains how to connect QuickBooks Online to Elite.


Topics in this article include

 
  • Setting Up Intuit to Allow Oauth 2.0
  • Connect Point of Rental to QuickBooks Online



 

Setting Up Intuit to Allow Oauth 2.0

This process is required in order for Intuit to connect with Point of Rental. If this process is not completed, then the QBO Integration will not work.

 

1. In your browser, navigate to https://developer.intuit.com/app/developer/homepage.

2. Click Sign In in the upper right corner. Use the same credentials as your QBO account sign-in.

 


3. After you log in click on Dashboard located on the top of the screen.
 



4. From the App Dashboard, click on + Create an app.
 



5. Choose the option you prefer, for this example we will choose QuickBooks Online and Payments.
 



6. Give the app a good name. Check the box for com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting, and select Create app.



7. From the left-hand menu, under Production Settings, select Keys & credentials.
 



8. Complete all the bullet points listed below. You can use real data or dummy data to fill out this information. If you want to use dummy data, you can use the data entered below.
 


 


 


 



9. Finally, on the last screen, fill out the assessment questionnaire and click Submit. Please note this step will take about 20-30 minutes to complete.
 


 


 


 


 


 


 


 



10. After you click Submit on the questionnaire, return to your Dashboard.
 



11.  From your User Icon, select Playground.
 



12.  On the Select App, choose the production option from the dropdown.
 



13. Your Client ID and Client Secret will be here. Please take a note of these as you will need them later.
 



14.  Under the OAuth Settings, select the com.intuit.quickbooks.accounting Scope. After that, click Get authorization code.
 



15.  Under the Connection Verification screen, click Connect.
 



16.  After you click Connect, you will return to the OAuth screen where you will get your Realm ID. Copy that value, and then click Get tokens.
 



17. Scroll down to Step 4, your Refresh token will be listed here. Copy that value.
 



18.  Finally in the Intuit Developer Keys & Credentials area, under your app, click Add URL and use https://www.example.com/. Then click Save.
 


 

Connect Point of Rental to QuickBooks Online

To get started, please ensure that the POR Accounting Sync Service is installed on the customer's server. It is also important to verify the account numbers on both the QBO and Point of Rental sides.
 

1. From Elite, go to the Configuration Gear > Core Options > App Configuration > Accounting Configuration > Account Numbers and log in to store 1.

2. Under the Miscellaneous Section of Accounting Numbers make sure that you select the API – QuickBooks Online.
 



3. Once that is selected, click Settings and a new window will pop up.
 



4. Fill out the information that you got from the Intuit Developer instructions prior.
 



5. Choose the Export Mode that you would like the system to use to sync to your QuickBooks online. You can choose between Real-Time GL or Transaction based.


 

Real-Time GL mode

 

  • Real-time income, payment, and bank deposit postings

    • + Batch end-of-day entries

    • Entries made in real-time mode are omitted from the end-of-day batch entries.

    • Income is posted when transactions close.

    • Payments are posted to the cash drawer and suspense or income accordingly.

    • Bank deposits post to the bank and the cash drawer.


 

Transaction mode

 

  • Invoice detail so AR can be done from QBO.

    • + Batch end-of-day entries

    • Entries made in transaction mode are omitted from the end-of-day batch entries.

    • Creates/updates customer records in QBO when necessary.

    • Invoices are created in QBO.

      • Transactions with monies owed (account or cash) create a full invoice that can be sent to the customer from within QBO.

      • Fully paid Account contracts create a fully paid invoice against the customer for visibility within Intacct.

      • Fully paid Cash contracts can be created against a single default customer record or the actual customer.

      • No inventory linking within the invoice record in QBO.
      • Income is posted by way of the invoice.
        • Closed transactions cannot be modified within Point of Rental.
        • Closed transactions that are modified will create credit adjustments in a new transaction that gets passed to Intacct for A/R purposes.
        • Closed transactions cannot be paid directly in Point of Rental.

 



6. Click OK when finished.
