INTERNAL | eSign Billing Site Setup and Configurations



This article is going to give you basic information on how to create an account for our customers on the eSign Billing Site.


Topics in this article include
 
  • Region
  • Creating the Account
  • Billing Site - Cancelling a Free Trial
  • eSign Billing Information

Region


There are different regions for eSign. Please make sure you are signing the customer up in the right region. Also, there are specialty sites for Enterprise Customers. Please reach out for assistance with those accounts.
 

US: rentalesign.com
UK: rentalessentials.co.uk
AU: rentalesign.com.au

 

Creating the Account

In this article, we are going to work on the Point of Rental eSign US Prod. The steps will be similar for the other regions.
 

1. Go to rentalesign.com/signup and fill out the customer information. This will work best in your browser in Incognito or Private Mode.
 


 

2. Under Package Information choose a package for the customer. We are going to change this when we get to the next step.
 


 

3. Agree to all the Terms and Conditions and do the reCAPTCHA.

4. Click on Get Started. This is going to create the account for the customer so they can sign in and get started. Billing will not be done during this process. We will have to send an invoice to the customer after we finalize the billing for the customer.

5. After this process is done close this tab and open up the billing site.


 

Billing Site - Cancelling a Free Trial

 

1. When you log in to the eSign Billing Site it is going to create a contract with a free trial. This will need to be canceled.

2. To do that, click on the contract from the Dashboard.

 


 

3. From the Contract Screen, click on Cancel Transaction from the blue bar on the top.

 

 

4. When it asks you "are you sure you want to so?" click on Cancel Transaction.
 


 

The contract is now canceled and it will take you to the Workbench where you can see other contracts and customer invoices in the Essentials Program.


 

eSign Billing Information


Before we talk about adding the eSign package, it is important that you look at the Purchase Doc and Opportunity before continuing. You might also want to have the Pricing Guide pulled up if you have questions about pricing. Some notes about eSign, everything as of late 2020 is accounting billed. This means we will still have to create contracts and it is just stored as a placeholder on the eSign level. Also, there are multiple eSign levels, whether Elite EL2, Expert, or Syrinx eSign.

As of Level 3 packaging, these are the few things that have changed:
 

  • Syrinx 365 Basic has eSign Remote Unlimited while Syrinx 365 Advanced has eSign Remote and Counter Unlimited.

  • Elite Basic has eSign Remote and Counter Unlimited.


 

Setup Current Features


For this next step, you will need to have the Purchase Documentation pulled up to make sure that you are assigning the right information and package to the customer. You might also want to have the Pricing Guide pulled up if you have questions about pricing.
 

1. From the eSign Billing site go to People > Companies.
 


 

2. Locate the customer, and then click the pencil icon next to the company you are creating the billing for.
 


 

3. For this part, you will need to make sure that it matches the billing site. Scroll down to the Current Live Company Features. This is where you will be working at.
 


 

4. From the Add Feature dropdown, you will add the features that the customer is supposed to have. If the customer has eSign Pro Plan they will have Arbitraryesig, eSign, OtherSignatures will be 500, and users will be 99999. When you add the feature it will replace what is currently up on the top after you refresh the screen to the correct amount. Please keep the bottom line as well. This is for Custom Development if the customer would like that.

 


 

5. Refresh your screen to make sure that everything is correct.
Keyboard Shortcut: Windows: ctrl + r; Mac: cmd + r


6. After you refresh you will see the updated information.

 


 

Set up Billing

For this next step, you will need to have the Purchase Documentation pulled up to make sure that you are assigning the right information and package to the customer. You might also want to have the Pricing Guide up if you have questions about pricing.
 

1. From the Essentials Billing site go to People > Companies.
 


 

2. Locate the customer and then click the pencil icon next to the company you are creating the billing for.

 


 

3. For this part of the billing site, you will need to ensure that it matches the billing site. Scroll down to the Current Company Packages (For Next Billing Period). This is where you will be working at.
 


 

4. Add the necessary package. In this example, the Standard Plan (Accounting Billed), and 2 Additional User (Accounting Billed) packages will be added.

 

5. Click the Add Package button when you are adding the package line.

 


 

6. Refresh your screen to make sure that everything is correct.
Keyboard Shortcut: Windows: ctrl + r; Mac: cmd + r

 

7. Once refreshed, you will see the items that you have added.

 

 


8. Now, create the contract. Under the Billing Period, you can choose Monthly, Quarterly, or Annually.

 


 

9. Then click on N/A - Create Quote for Customer under the Current Contract area.

 


 

10. This is going to take you to the Contract Creation Screen. Enter the Start date and the End date will automatically populate.

11. Click on Create Reservation when finished.

 

 


12. Once on the contract screen you will have to add the items from the Purchase Documentation again to line up what you placed earlier. To do that, use the Search Bar in the middle of the screen.

 

 


13. With eSign you will see overbooking on several items, proceed with the contract as this is not going to affect the customer experience. Press OK on the message.

 


 

14. This is going to also add other items that are needed for the account, called Suggested Items in Essentials. You can leave these be.

 

 

 

15. After you add all those items to the contract you can invoice the customer. If the customer is going to be billed using the Essentials Application, click on the Invoicing option and create a new invoice.
 



16. It is recommended that you print out the contract from the Essentials Billing Software and place it in your Project or Case. This way it is updated from the Point of Rental side.

17. Have the customer follow the eSign Counter Setup and eSign Remote Setup.
