Inspection App - Reports



Overview

This article will walk you through how to access both Inspection History and Inspection Detail Reports.


Topics in this article include
 
  • How to get there
  • Inspection History & Inspection Detail Report


How to get there

Under the Maintenance tab select Inspection History at the bottom of that list.




Inspection History & Inspection Detail Report


Inspection History

Inspection History gives you a list of all the inspection jobs that have been created on the item. It also gives you details about the job, if it was completed, who it was assigned to, and the dates. If you double click on the job it will give you a printable report for that job.



You have the ability to pull up reporting by double-clicking the line.

 

Inspection Detail Report

Inspection Detail Report gives you the ability to print out all the inspection jobs for a given date range.
 

1. Enter your date range.



2. Click Generate.

 

 You will be shown all the inspections for that date range.
