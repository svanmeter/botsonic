Edit Contract Attachments.

 

Allows you to define your own wording to print out on the contracts that match the criteria of the options below.

Edit Contract Attachments - opens the window in which you can add verbiage to the Backside Legal, Owner Signature, Credit Card Authorization, and Marketing Page. When selecting Edit Contract Attachments, this will default to the Backside Legal Tab.  You can Load your backside legalese into the system for emailing or printing with your contracts.   

* Note:  You will need to save your Document on your Server in a Rich Text Format (RTF).
