INTERNAL | PORCloud v2 POR Update Schedule



This document is intended to answer questions about the PORCloud V2 servers, and why customers cannot install updates on their own.


Topics in this article include
    
  • When are POR updates installed?
  • Why can't customers install their own POR updates?
  • How are updates installed on PORCloud v2 servers?
  • Is the customer given any notice?
  • What do I do if the customers need updates installed sooner?
  • Submitting a ticket through the Help Desk


 

When are POR updates installed?

POR updates are installed at 10 PM CST on the Wednesday after an update is released.

 
 

Why can’t customers install their own POR updates?


For customers on PORCloud v2, the ability to install updates has been disabled by the DevOps Team. The reasons for this include:
 

1. PORCloud v2 deployments are RDS farms with a load balance in place. This means that an employee might be logged into one server the first time they connect, but may be connected to a different server if they log back in. This is done if the original server has a high number of other employees logged in or is being heavily used by the POR software.

2. PORCloud v2 customers have limited access to the RDS farms, preventing them from upgrading the servers as they normally would. Cloud customers are given remote apps that only provide access to the POR software screens. They do not have a full desktop.

3. This is an issue because ALL servers in the RDS farm must be updated to the same version of the POR software to prevent issues. Customers that have on-premise RDS farms have access to all servers individually to update the POR software and can update the software as normal.

 


How are updates installed on PORCloud v2 servers?

Because of the issues in the previous question, the DevOps Team has created an automatic schedule to install POR updates at night.


Is the customer given any notice?

Any customer logged into the server is given notice at 9:55 pm CST that updates will be installed in 5 minutes. At that time, they need to save their work and close the POR software cleanly.


What do I do if the customers need updates installed sooner?

For any customer needing updates installed before the scheduled time, a ticket needs to be submitted through the https://helpdesk.point-of-rental.com/ website.


Submitting a ticket through the Help Desk

How do I submit a ticket through https://helpdesk.point-of-rental.com/?


1. Open https://helpdesk.point-of-rental.com/ in a web browser (FireFox, Chrome, Edge).

2. If prompted to log into the company google account, select the Log In With Google button.
 


 

3. Enter your company Google credentials.
 



4. Select the NetOps/IT option.
 



5. Fill out the entire form with your contact details.
 



6. Select the Next button.
 



7. Fill out the second form, then select Next.
 


 

8. Use the drop-down menu to select Server/Greenscreen, then fill out the form, stating the customer would like the POR updates installed ahead of schedule and as soon as possible. Be sure to add the CUSTOMERS NAME and CNUM. These are important pieces of information.

9. Select the Next button.
 


 

10. Review the information and confirm it is correct by checking the I have verified the information above check box. If you need to make changes, use the Previous button.
 


 

11. If everything is correct, and the I have verified the information above box is checked, select the Submit button to submit the request to our NetOps and DevOps Teams.

12. Update your case and customer that the request has been submitted.
