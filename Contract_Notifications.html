Contract Notifications



 

Overview:

This article discusses contract notifications and how they can be added to generate on a contract. Contract notifications are a method used to automatically send a notification at certain stages of a contract's life. These notifications can be internal among your employees or external to your customers. For email or text notifications your email program must be set up on the terminal generating the notification.


Topics in this article include

  • Contract Notifications
  • Notification Options



Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Counter System > Modify Contract
Elite: Transactions > Modify Contract


Contract Notifications
 

1. From within the contract processing screen, click the Notifications tab. 

 

2. Click the 'New Notification' button. The following box will pop up (see below).

 

  • The top left dropdown menu gives you three options for sending messages: email, text message, or inter-office email (within the Point of Rental program).

 

  • If you select email, you can choose from customer email addresses in the 'Select Email' dropdown or type the address in the box below.

 


 

  • If you choose text message you will see a box to enter the cell phone number and another with a dropdown menu where you can select the provider.

 


 

  • Finally, if you choose Inter-Office Email, you will have a dropdown with a list of all your employees.

 

  • Then you will have a list of transaction operations with check boxes. If a box is checked, such as Called Off Rent, when you call off a rental, the system will automatically generate a notification.

 



Notification Options
 

Open - When the contract is sent out and the status changes to open.
 

Marked Locked - When the contract has been marked as locked through Day at a Glance.
 

Modify - Whenever a change has been made to the contract.
 

Send Out Reservation - Would show as On The Way through Mobile Workforce.
 

Delivery - When the contract has been marked as delivery on a contract.
 

Call Off - When the contract has been marked as called off rent.
 

Close - When the contract has gone from an open to closed status.
 

Completed - When the contract has gone from a closed to completed status.
 

Change Operation - When the operations status on the contract has been changed either through the contract or through auto continuation.
