Customer Call Log Report




Overview
 

The Customer Call Log Report prints all the call logs entered for the time you chose. The report is grouped by customer name.


Here's how to get there
Expert: Program Menu > Customer Reports > Customer Call Log Report
Elite: Right Menu > Search bar > Customer Call Log Report

 

Select your report's beginning and end dates.
