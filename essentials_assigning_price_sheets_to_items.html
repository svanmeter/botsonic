Assigning Price Sheets to Items

This article outlines the process of assigning price sheets to items within the system for pricing management.

Topics included in this article

  • Overview of price sheets
  • Creating and managing price sheets
  • Assigning price sheets to items

Overview of price sheets

Price sheets are used to set standardized pricing models for different items or services offered.

Creating and managing price sheets

Create and manage price sheets to streamline the pricing process.

  1. Go to the Pricing section in the system.
  2. Create a new price sheet by specifying the pricing details and conditions.
  3. Manage existing price sheets by updating or modifying them as needed.

Assigning price sheets to items

Assign price sheets to items to apply consistent pricing across your inventory or services.

  1. Select the item to which you want to assign a price sheet.
  2. Choose the appropriate price sheet from the list of available sheets.
  3. Confirm the assignment to ensure the item follows the specified pricing model.
